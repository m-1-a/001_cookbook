Chicken Liver Dip

Cut 1/2 lb. chicken livers into small pieces. Melt 2 Tbs. butter or margarine with 1 clove pressed or minced garlic. Saute livers till cooked through. Remove from heat. Stir in 3 Tbs. Bacardi Dark Rum. Cool. Combine mixture in a blender with 1 (8 ounces) package softened cream cheese, 1/4 cup plain yogurt, 1/2 tsp. salt, 1/4 tsp. crumbled basil. Blend smooth. Salt and pepper to taste. Chill several hours. Serve with crackers and/or raw vegetables pieces. Makes about 1 and 1/3 cups.

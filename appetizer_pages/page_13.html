Sauteed Shrimp

Marinate 1 and 1/2 lbs. medium shrimp, shelled and deveined in 1/2 cup Bacardi Light Rum for several hours. Melt 1/4 cup butter or margarine in a large frying pan. Add shrimp and rum mixture with 1/2 tsp.garlic salt. Saute for 8 to 10 minutes or until shrimp cook through. Sprinkle 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese and ground pepper over shrimp. Broil for 2 to 3 minutes or until cheese browns. Serve hot. Makes about 36 servings

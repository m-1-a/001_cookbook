Mini Meatballs

Combine 1 and 1/2 Tbs. Bacardi Light Rum, 2 Tbs. soy sauce, 1 pressed garlic clove and 1 tsp. ground ginger; blend. Add 1 lb. ground beef chuck. Blend well. Shape into balls about 1 inch in diameter. Bake at 300 F for 12 to 15 minutes, turning once. Serve with wooden picks.

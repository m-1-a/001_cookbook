Hot Crab Meat

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-ounce) package Philadelphia Brand Cream Cheese
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (7 and 1/2-ounce can) flaked drained crab meat
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped onion
  • 2 Tbs. milk
  • 1/2 tsp. cream style horseradish
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/3 cup sliced almonds, toasted

Prep Steps

  1. Combine softened cream cheese, crab meat, onion, mil, horseradish and seasonings; mixing until well blended
  2. Spoon into 9 inch pie plate or oven proof dish; sprinkle with nuts
  3. Bake at 375F for 15 minutes
  4. Serve as a dip or a spread with crackers, chips or raw vegetables
  5. Makes about 2 cups

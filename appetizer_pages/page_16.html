Sausage and Hot Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-ounce) package Swift Premium Brown 'N Serve Sausage, any flavor

Prep Steps

  1. Cut sausage links in halves
  2. Brown according to the packaging directions
  3. Keep hot on hot tray or in chafing dish
  4. Spear sausage pieces with smooth toothpicks
  5. Serve with Hot Mustard Sauce for dipping
  6. Makes 20 appetizers

Hot Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1/3 cup Dijon-style mustard
  • 2 tsps. horseradish
  • 2 Tbs. sugar

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan
  2. Stir in flour and salt
  3. Gradually add water
  4. Add bouillon cube, mustard, horseradish and sugar
  5. Stir and cook until sauce thickens
  6. Makes 1 and 1/2 cups

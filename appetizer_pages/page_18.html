Party Mix

Ingredients

  • 6 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 tsp. seasoned salt
  • 4 tsps. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups Corn Chex cereal
  • 2 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • 2 cups Wheat Chex cereal
  • 3/4 cup salted mixed nuts

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250F
  2. Heat butter in 13 * 9 * 2 inch baking pan in oven until melted
  3. Remove
  4. Stir in seasoned salt and Worcestershire sauce
  5. Add Chex and nuts
  6. Mix until all pieces are coated
  7. Heat in oven for 45 minutes
  8. Stir for every 15 minutes
  9. Spread on absorbent paper to cool
  10. Makes 6 and 3/4 cups
  11. Note: Party Mix may be frozen, so make a double batch. Thaw at room temperature in container in which it was stored

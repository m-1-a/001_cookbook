Oriental Ham Tidbits

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plum or red currant jelly
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 Tbs. cornstarch
  • 1 lb. Golden Star Ham by Armour, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 1 (8-oz.) can water chestnuts, drained, cut in half
  • 1 green pepper, cut into 1-inch chunks

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet, combine jelly, lemon juice and cornstarch
  2. Heat, stirring constantly, until thickened.
  3. Add ham, water chestnuts and green peppers, stirring to coat pieces; heat through
  4. Serve with wooden picks
  5. Makes 5 cups

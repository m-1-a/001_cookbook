Roast Beef Party Snacks

Ingredients

  • 12 to 15 melba rounds
  • 1 (4 and 3/4 ounces) can Underwood Roast Beef Spread
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese
  • 1 Tbs. minced onion

Prep Steps

  1. Spread melba round with roast beef spread
  2. Top with grated cheese and minced onion
  3. Broil for 3 to 5 minutes or until cheese bubbles
  4. Makes 12-15 snacks

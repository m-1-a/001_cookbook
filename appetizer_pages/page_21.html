Horseradish Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 1/2-oz.) can Bumble Bee Pink Salmon
  • 1 (8-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup cup dairy sour cream
  • 1/4 cup horseradish
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. garlic powder
  • Crisp romaine lettuce
  • Paprika
  • Crackers
  • Celery sticks

Prep Steps

  1. Drain salmon
  2. Remove skin, if desired
  3. Mash bones
  4. Beats cream cheese, sour cream, horseradish, salt and garlic powder until smooth
  5. Beat in salmon and bones until blended
  6. Arrange romaine lettuce in a bowl
  7. Spoon in salmon mixture
  8. Sprinkle with paprika
  9. Serve with crackers and celery sticks
  10. Makes 6 to 8 servings

Hot Chili Cheese Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 (15-oz.) can Armour Star Chili (no beans)
  • 1 (4-oz.) can chopped green chiles
  • 1 lb. processed American cheese, shredded
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • Corn chips

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, except chips
  2. Heat, stirring occasionally, over low heat until cheese melts
  3. Serve heated as a dip with chips
  4. Makes 4 cups

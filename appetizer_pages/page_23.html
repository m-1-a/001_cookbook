Liptauer Spread

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) package Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 cup soft Parkay Margarine
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped onion
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. anchovy paste
  • 1 tsp. Kraft Pure Prepared Mustard
  • 1 tsp. capers, chopped
  • 1 tsp. caraway seed
  • 1 tsp. paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Combine softened cream cheese and margarine, mixing until well blended
  2. Add remaining ingredients; mix well
  3. Chill
  4. Serve as a spread with French or pumpernickel bread
  5. Make 1 and 1/2 cups

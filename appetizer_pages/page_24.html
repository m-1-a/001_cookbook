Coventry Cheese Ball

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup (4 oz.) shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 2 Tbs. minced parsley
  • 1 tsp. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. In a bowl, blend cream cheese and Cheddar cheese
  2. Add onion, parsley, Lea & Perrins and salt; blend well
  3. Shape into a ball
  4. Chill and serve with assorted crackers, if desired
  5. Makes 1 cheese ball

Chicken Liver Pate

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Planters Peanut Oil
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 lb. cooked chicken livers
  • 1 hard cooked egg
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Heat Planters Oil in a heavy skillet
  2. Add diced onion and saute until transparent, for 5 minutes
  3. Allow to cool in skillet
  4. Grind or chop together chicken liver, egg and entire contents of skillet
  5. Stir in salt and pepper
  6. Chill until ready to serve
  7. Makes about 1 and 1/2 cups spread

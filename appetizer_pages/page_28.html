Bacon Onion Pinwheels

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup soft Parkay Margarine
  • 6 crisply cooked bacon slices, crumbled
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 2 (8-oz.) cans Pillsbury's Refrigerated Quick Crescent Dinner Rolls

Prep Steps

  1. Combine onion, margarine, bacon and parsley; mix well
  2. Separate dough into eight rectangles
  3. Firmly press perforations to seal
  4. Spread margarine mixture over dough
  5. Roll up each rectangle, starting at the short ends
  6. Cut into four slices
  7. Place on un-greased cookie sheet
  8. Cut sides down; flatten slightly
  9. Bake at 375F for 15 - 20 minutes or until golden brown
  10. Makes approximately 2 and 1/2 dozen

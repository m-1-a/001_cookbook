Zucchini Appetizers

Ingredients

  • 3 cups thinly sliced unpared zucchini
  • 1 cup Bisquick baking mix
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbs. snipped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
  • 1/2 tsp. dried marjoram or oregano leaves
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F
  2. Grease oblong pan 13 * 9 * 2 inches
  3. Mix all ingredients; spread in pan
  4. Bake until golden brown, about 25 minutes
  5. Cut into about 2 * 1 inch pieces
  6. Makes 4 dozen appetizers

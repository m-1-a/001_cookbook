Pick Pockets

Mix well 2 cups finely grated American cheese, 1/2 cup softened butter or margarine. Using pastry blender, cut in 1 cup all-purpose flour, dash cayenne. Divide into 2 balls; chill. Cut 36 strips (2 * 1/2 inch) from Heinz Dill or Sweet Pickles. Roll each ball into 1/8 inch thickness on floured board; cut in rectangles (2 and 1/2 * 2 inch). Wrap a pickle strip in dough; seal ends well. Place on un-greased baking sheets. Bake in 425F oven, for 12 to 15 minutes. Make 3 dozen.

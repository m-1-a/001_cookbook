Mushroom Cheese Appetizers

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick baking mix
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1/4 lb. bulk pork sausage
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 35 (about 1 lb.) medium mushrooms
  • 2 cups (8 oz.) shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F
  2. Grease oblong pan 13 * 9 * 2 inches
  3. Mix baking mix and water until soft dough forms
  4. Beat vigorously 20 strokes
  5. Press dough in bottom of pan with floured hands
  6. Cook and stir sausage in skillet until brown and drain
  7. Mix sausage, onions and mayonnaise
  8. Remove stems from mushrooms
  9. Finely chop stems
  10. Stir into sausage mixture
  11. Fill mushroom caps with sausage mixture
  12. Place mushrooms in row on dough in pan
  13. Sprinkle with cheese and paprika
  14. Cover pan loosely with aluminum foil
  15. Bake for 20 minutes
  16. Remove foil
  17. Bake until cheese is bubbly, 5 - 10 minutes
  18. Let stand for 15 minutes
  19. Cut into pieces
  20. Makes 35 appetizers

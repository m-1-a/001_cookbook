Barbecued Korean Style Short Ribs

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. pre-cut beef short ribs 2 and 1/2 inches long
  • 1/2 cup Kikkoman Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 Tbs. sesame seed, toasted
  • 1 tsp. Tabasco Pepper Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Prep Steps

  1. Score meaty side of ribs, 1/2 inch apart, 1/2 inch deep, lengthwise and crosswise
  2. Combine remaining ingredients
  3. Marinate ribs in sauce in plastic bag for 2 hours
  4. Broil 2 inches from heat for 15 minutes or until ribs are brown and crisp on all sides

Sweet and Sour Franks

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup red currant jelly
  • 1/2 cup prepared brown mustard
  • 2 Tbs. minced onion
  • 1 Tbs. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 lb. frankfurters, cut into 1 inch chunks

Prep Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan mix jelly, mustard, onion and Lea & Perrins
  2. Bring to a boiling point
  3. Add frankfurters
  4. Return to boiling point
  5. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
  6. If desired, serve hot from a chafing dish with cocktail picks
  7. Makes about 32 hors d'oeuvers

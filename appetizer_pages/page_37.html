Popcorn Party Mix

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts popped Jolly Time Pop Corn
  • 2 cups slim pretzel sticks
  • 2 cups cheese curls
  • 1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic salt

Prep Steps

  1. In a shallow baking pan, mix popped corn, pretzel sticks and cheese curls
  2. Melt butter or margarine in a small saucepan and stir in seasonings
  3. Pour over dry ingredients and mix well
  4. Bake at 250F for about 45 minutes, stirring several times
  5. Makes about 2 and 1/2 quarts
  6. Note: 1 cup of dry roasted peanuts may be added

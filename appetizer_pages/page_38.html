Crispy Snack

Ingredients

  • 3 quarts unsalted, popped Jolly Time Pop Corn
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 1/2 (3 and 1/4-oz.) can French fried onion
  • 1/4 cup bacon bits or bacon flavored bits
  • Salt to taste

Prep Steps

  1. Toss popped corn with melted butter
  2. Stir in French fried onions and bacon bits
  3. Sprinkle with salt
  4. Place mixture on a jelly roll pan or baking sheet
  5. Heat oven to 250F
  6. Place in oven for 5 minutes
  7. Serve hot

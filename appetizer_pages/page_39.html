Bacon Cheese Pop Corn

Ingredients

  • 4 quarts popped Jolly Time Pop Corn
  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 1/2 tsp. seasoned salt
  • 1/2 tsp. hickory-smoked salt
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan or American cheese
  • 1/3 cup bacon flavored bits

Prep Steps

  1. Pour freshly popped pop corn in a large bowl
  2. Combine butter with seasoned hickory-smoked salt
  3. Pour over pop corn
  4. Toss well to coat
  5. Sprinkle with cheese and bacon bits
  6. Toss again and serve while warm

Dill Fondue Balls

Combine 2 slightly beaten egg whites, 1 cup grated Swiss cheese, 1/3 cup chopped Heinz Dills, dash garlic salt. Drop by teaspoonsful into 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs; roll and coat well, forming small balls. Chill until ready to serve. Fry in deep fat (375F) until golden brown, for 2 to 3 minutes. Drain on absorbent paper. Makes 2 dozen appetizers.

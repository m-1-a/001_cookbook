Curry Party Mix

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 and 1/4 tsps. seasoned salt
  • 1 and 1/4 - 2 tsps. curry powder
  • 4 and 1/2 tsps. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups Corn Chex cereal
  • 2 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • 2 cups Wheat Chex cereal
  • 2 cups Bran Chex cereal
  • 1 cup chow mein noodles
  • 1 cup golden raisins

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250F
  2. Heat butter in large shallow roasting pan (about 15 * 10 * 2 inches) in oven until melted
  3. Remove
  4. Stir in seasoning
  5. Add Chex, noodles, and raisins
  6. Mix until all pieces are coated
  7. Heat in oven 1 hour
  8. Stir every 15 minutes
  9. Spread on absorbent paper to cool
  10. Makes about 10 cups

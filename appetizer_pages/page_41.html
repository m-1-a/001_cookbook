Crab Ball Hors d'Oeuvre

Ingredients

  • 1 (6-oz.) package Wakefield Snow Crab Meat
  • 1 (8-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tsps. chopped chives
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Prep Steps

  1. Thaw and drain crab meat
  2. Blend softened cream cheese, chives, garlic powder and salt
  3. Fold in crab meat
  4. Shape into log or ball
  5. Roll in pecans
  6. Serve with crackers or fresh vegetables

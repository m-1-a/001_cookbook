Spinach Beef Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 (10-oz.) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 (8-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 Tbs. dillweed
  • 1 (2 and 1/2-oz.) jar Armour Sliced Dried Beef, rinsed and chopped
  • Assorted crackers

Prep Steps

  1. Combine spinach, cream cheese, mayonnaise, green onions and dillweed in container of electric blender
  2. Process on high speed for 1 to 2 minutes or until smooth and creamy
  3. Fold in Dried Beef
  4. Chill
  5. Serve with crackers
  6. Makes 3 cups

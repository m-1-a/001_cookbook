Make Ahead Ham & Mushroom Pate

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/4 lb. coarsely chopped fresh mushrooms
  • 2 (4 and 1/2-oz.) can Underwood Deviled Ham
  • 1 (3-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 tsp. ground thyme Party bread slices

Prep Steps

  1. Day before:
  2. Melt butter in a skillet and saute mushrooms over medium heat until tender
  3. Combine Deviled Ham, cream cheese and thyme; blend well
  4. Stir in sauteed mushrooms
  5. Cover and chill overnight
  6. Serve on party bread slices
  7. Makes 1 and 3/4 cups pate

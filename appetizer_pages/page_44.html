Cheesy Corn Spread

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. (3 cups) shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup dairy sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salad dressing or mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 (12-oz.) can Green Giant Mexicorn Golden Whole Kernel Corn with Sweet Peppers, drained

Prep Steps

  1. Bring cheese to room temperature
  2. In a large bowl, crumble cheese with a fork or blend with mixer to form small bits
  3. Mix in remaining ingredients, except corn, until well blended
  4. Stir in corn
  5. Cover; chill several hours or overnight
  6. Can be stored in the refrigerator up to 1 week
  7. Serve with raw vegetables or crackers
  8. Makes 3 1/2 cups

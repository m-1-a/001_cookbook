Tropical Fruit Nibbles

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup light rum
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 tsp. French's Almond Extract or Coconut Flavor
  • Melon balls, pineapple chunks, grapes, or other fresh fruit

Prep Steps

  1. Combine rum, orange juice and extract
  2. Pour over fruit and refrigerate 30 minutes
  3. Serve with wooden picks

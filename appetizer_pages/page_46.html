Alaska Shrimp Quiche

Ingredients

  • 1 (6-oz.) package Wakefield Shrimp
  • 1 (9 inch) pie shell
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 (4-oz.) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 tsp. chopped chives
  • Dash pepper
  • Dash nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup grated Swiss cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Thaw and drain shrimp well
  2. Beat together the eggs, half and half and seasonings
  3. Place mushrooms and shrimp in bottom of pie shell
  4. Sprinkle with cheese
  5. Add egg mixture and bake at 375F for 35 to 40 minutes or until center is firm
  6. To prepare quiche ahead, bake 10 minutes less than directed
  7. Cool then wrap and freeze
  8. When ready to use, unwrap and bake at 325F for 25 minutes

Alternate suggestion 1: Substitute 1 (6-oz.) package Wakefield Alaska Crab meat for the shrimp

Alternate suggestion 2: Or substitute 1 cup half and half for 3/4 cup half and half and 1/4 cup dry sherry

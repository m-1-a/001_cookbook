Swiss and Bacon Squares

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 (8-oz) package natural Swiss cheese slices
  • 1 lb. bacon, crisply fried and crumbled
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp. onion salt

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425F
  2. Grease baking dish, 13 * 9 * 2 inches
  3. Mix baking mix and water until soft dough forms
  4. Beat vigorously 20 strokes
  5. Gently smooth into ball on floured cloth-covered board
  6. Knead 10 times
  7. Pat in dish with floured hands, pressing 1/2 inch up sides
  8. Top with cheese slices, overlapping edges; sprinkle with bacon
  9. Mix remaining ingredients
  10. Pour over bacon
  11. Bake until edges are golden brown and knife inserted near center comes out clean, about 20 minutes
  12. Makes 6 to 8 servings as a main course or 12 to 16 servings as an appetizer

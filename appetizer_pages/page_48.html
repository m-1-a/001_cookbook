Rumaki Canapes

Ingredients

  • 2 (4 and 3/4-oz.) can of Liverwurst Spread
  • 2 tsps. soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped water chestnuts
  • 1/2 lb. raw bacon
  • Buttered toasted rounds

Prep Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine Liverwurst Spread, soy sauce and brown sugar and mix well
  2. Add water chestnuts and brown sugar and mix well
  3. Chill
  4. While mixture is chilling, prepare bacon crumbs
  5. Fry bacon in a frying pan until crisp and let drain on paper towel
  6. When bacon has cooled, break into small crumbles and set aside
  7. Spoon chilled liverwurst mixture onto prepared toast rounds and top with crumbled bacon
  8. Chill covered until ready to use
  9. Makes 30 hors d'oeuvres

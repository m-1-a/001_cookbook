Slimster's Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 medium-size avocado, peeled and cut in chunks
  • 1 tomato, peeled and cut in chunks
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 Tbs. French's Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp. French's Garlic salt
  • 1/8 tsp. French's Cayenne Pepper
  • Cauliflowerets
  • Carrot stick
  • Celery Stick
  • Green or red pepper squares
  • Green onions
  • Cucumber sticks

Prep Steps

  1. Combine cottage cheese, avocado, tomato, lemon juice, Worcestershire Sauce, Garlic Salt, and Cayenne Pepper in blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Serve as dip with vegetables
  4. Makes about 2 cups dip (about 300 calories per cup)

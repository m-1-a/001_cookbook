Toasty Bread Slices

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soft Parkay Margarine
  • 1/3 cup French fried onions
  • 1 Vienna bread loaf, sliced

Prep Steps

  1. Combine margarine and onions
  2. Spread on one side of each bread slice
  3. Place bread slices spread side up on un-greased cookie sheet
  4. Bake at 400F for 12 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned

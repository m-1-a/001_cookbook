Mushroom Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) can refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 2 cups mushrooms slices
  • 1/4 cup margarine, melted
  • 1/4 tsp. marjoram
  • Kraft Grated Parmesan Cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Separate dough into triangles
  2. Place on un-greased 12 inch pizza pan; press out dough to fit pan
  3. Toss mushrooms with margarine; arrange on dough
  4. Sprinkle with cheese and marjoram
  5. Bake at 375F for 20 to 25 minutes

Variation: Shredded Mozzarella, Monterey Jack or Swiss cheese are excellent alternates for Parmesan

Cocktail Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 2 Tbs. finely dry bread crumb
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped green pepper
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 (10 and 3/4-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. vinegar
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp. prepare mustard
  • Dash hot pepper sauce

Prep Steps

  1. Mix thoroughly beef, egg, bread crumbs and salt
  2. Shape into 50 small 1/2-inch meatballs
  3. Arrange in shallow baking pan 12 * 8 * 2 inches
  4. Broil 4 inches from heat until brown; turn once
  5. Pour off fat
  6. Meanwhile, in saucepan, cook onion and green pepper in butter until tender
  7. Add meatballs and remaining ingredients
  8. Cover; cook over low heat for 10 minutes
  9. Stir occasionally
  10. Makes about 3 and 1/2 cups

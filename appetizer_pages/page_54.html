Pepperoni Pizzas

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese (about 4 oz.)
  • 1 cup sliced pitted ripe olives
  • 4 ounces pepperoni, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Hellmann's Best Food Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 4 English muffins, split, toasted

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl stir together first 5 ingredients
  2. Spoon onto muffin halves
  3. Broil 6 inches from source of heat for 5 minutes about or until browned
  4. Makes 8

Texas Style Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1 envelope French's Chili-O Seasoning Mix
  • 1 (16-oz.) can tomatoes
  • 1 (15-oz.) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 and 1/4 cup biscuit mix
  • 1/2 cup corn meal
  • Shredded Cheddar or American cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in large skillet, stirring to crumble; pour off excess fat
  2. Stir in seasoning mix, tomatoes and beans
  3. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes
  4. Sprinkle yeast over warm water in large mixing bowl; stir to dissolve
  5. Add biscuit mix and corn meal; stir until smooth
  6. Knead 25 strokes on floured surface
  7. Divide in half, roll or pat each to a 14-inch circle on greased pizza pan or baking sheet
  8. Spoon filing on top of crust; sprinkle with cheese
  9. Bake at 425F for 15 to 20 minutes, until crust is golden brown
  10. Makes 6 to 8 servings

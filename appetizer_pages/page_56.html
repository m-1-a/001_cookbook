Easy Deep-Dish Pizza

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 3/4 cup cold water
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 (4 and 1/2-oz.) jar slice mushrooms, drained
  • 1/2 cup cup chopped green pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese (about 8 ozs.)

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425F
  2. Lightly grease jelly roll pan, (15 and 1/2) or cookie sheet
  3. Mix baking mix and water until soft dough appears; beat vigorously 20 strokes
  4. Gently smooth dough into ball on floured cloth-covered board
  5. STEPS
  6. Knead 20 times
  7. Pat dough in bottom and up sides of pan with floured hands
  8. STEPS
  9. Or roll into rectangle, 13 * 10 inches and place on cookie sheet; pinch edges of rectangle, forming 3/4inch rim
  10. Cook stir ground beef, onion, salt and garlic until beef is brown
  11. Mix tomato sauce and Italian seasoning; spread over dough
  12. Spoon beef mixture over sauce
  13. Top with remaining ingredients
  14. Bake until crust is golden brown, about 20 minutes
  15. Makes 8 servings

Sour Cream Pizza Stripes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 2/3 cup Borden Milk
  • 1 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 (8-oz) container Bordon Sour Cream
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup Borden Grated Parmesan and Romano Cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 Tbs. chopped pimiento
  • 1 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. In medium bowl, combine baking mix, Milk and Italian seasoning stir to make soft dough
  3. Turn onto well-floured surface; knead lightly 10 to 20 strokes
  4. Pat dough onto bottom and up sides of greased 13 * 9-inch baking dish, making a small rim
  5. In medium bowl, mix remaining ingredients
  6. Spread evenly over dough
  7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden
  8. Cut into rectangles
  9. Serve warm
  10. Refrigerate leftovers

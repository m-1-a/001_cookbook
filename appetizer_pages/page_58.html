Chili Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 2, 10 to 12-inch prepared flour tortillas
  • 1 (15-oz.) can Hormel Chili (with beans or no beans)
  • 1 Tbs. chopped onions
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 tsp. green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, grated
  • Red onions rings

Prep Steps

  1. Bake tortillas in 350F oven, 2 minutes per side
  2. Heat Hormel Chili, adding onion, garlic powder, chilies
  3. Sprinkle Monterey Jack cheese over tortillas
  4. Spread thinly on chili mixture
  5. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top
  6. Bake for 8 minutes in a 350F oven
  7. Garnish with red onion rings

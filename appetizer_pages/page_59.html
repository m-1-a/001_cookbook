Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. Planters Peanut Oil
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove of garlic, medium size, minced
  • 1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Generous dash ground black pepper
  • 12 prepared taco shells
  • Grated cheese
  • Shredded lettuce

Prep Steps

  1. Heat Planters Oil in skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add ground beef and brown,stirring to break up beef
  3. Add onion and garlic; continue cooking until onion is tender
  4. Mix in tomato sauce, salt and pepper
  5. Heat stirring occasionally
  6. Spoon beef mixture into prepared taco shells
  7. Top with cheese and lettuce
  8. Serve hot
  9. Make 12 servings

Chippy Cheese Ball

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/4 lb. Roquefort cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 lb. cream cheese
  • 2 tsps. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Tbs. grated onion
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper
  • 1 and 1/4 cups crushed Lay's Brand Sour Cream & Onion Flavored Potato Chips

Prep Steps

  1. Have cheese at room temperature
  2. Blend well with mixer or pastry blender
  3. Add Worcestershire sauce, onion, pepper, and 1/4 cup of the crushed potato chips
  4. Shape into ball and roll in the remaining chips until completely covered
  5. Chill well

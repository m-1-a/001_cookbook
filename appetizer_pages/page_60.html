Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Planters Peanut Oil
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 (13 and 1/4-oz.) can chicken broth
  • 1 2/3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 Tbs. chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cups coarsely shredded Cheddar cheese (about 5 ozs.)
  • 1 and 1/2 diced cooked chicken or turkey
  • 1/4 cup sliced pitted ripe olives
  • 8 (7-inch) Pineta Corn Tortillas

Prep Steps

  1. Heat Planters Peanut Oil in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add onion and garlic and cook until tender
  3. Stir in flour, mixing till smooth
  4. Gradually add chicken broth, water and enchilada sauce, mixing until smooth
  5. Stir in chili powder and salt
  6. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thick
  7. Reserving 1/2 cup cheese, mix remaining cheese with chicken and olives
  8. Dip Pineta Corn Tortillas in prepared sauce for about 5 seconds
  9. Spoon 3 Tbs. chicken filling across center of each
  10. Roll up and place seam side down, in a shallow 2 and 1/2-quart baking dish
  11. Pour remaining sauce over tortillas
  12. Bake at 350F for 15 minutes
  13. Sprinkle with reserved cheese and continue baking for 10 minutes
  14. Garnish with sliced ripe olives if desired and serve immediately
  15. Makes 4 servings

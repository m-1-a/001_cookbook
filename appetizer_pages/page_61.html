Basic A.1 Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt (optional)
  • 1/4 cup A.1 Steak Sauce

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl, lightly combine all ingredients
  2. Form 6 patties to fit bread shape of your choice
  3. Broil, barbecue or pan fry until cooked as desired
  4. Makes 6

English Cheddar Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded sharp yellow Cheddar cheese (4 ozs.)
  • 2 Tbs. minced fresh onion
  • 1clove garlic, medium size, crushed
  • 2 Tbs. A.1 Steak sauce
  • 6 Basic A.1 Hamburgers, cooked
  • 6 English muffins, fork split, toasted and buttered

Prep Steps

  1. Combine cheese, onion, garlic and A.1
  2. Spread 2 Tbs. over each cooked hamburger patty
  3. Broil to melt cheese
  4. Serve on muffins
  5. Makes 6

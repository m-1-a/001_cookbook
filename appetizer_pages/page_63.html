Greek Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (4 ozs.) Feta cheese, rinsed and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup sliced ripe olives
  • 1 Tbs. A.1 Steak Sauce
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 2 Tbs. mayonnaise
  • 6 Basic A.1 Hamburgers, cooked
  • 3 Pita (or pocket) bread, cut in half
  • 6 tomato slices
  • Shredded lettuce

Prep Steps

  1. Combine cheese, olives, A.1, lemon juice and mayonnaise
  2. Place hamburgers in Pita bread
  3. Spoon topping over hamburgers
  4. Heat in preheated 425F oven for 5 minutes
  5. Garnish with tomato and lettuce
  6. Makes 6

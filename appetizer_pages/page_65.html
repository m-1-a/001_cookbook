San Francisco Burgers

Ingredients

  • 2 large, ripe avocados, peeled and mashed
  • 2 Tbs. A.1 Steak Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh onion
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked, drained, and crumbled
  • 6 Basic A.1 Hamburgers, cooked
  • 6 tomato slices
  • 12 San Francisco sour-dough bread, toasted

Prep Steps

  1. Combine avocado, A.1, salt, onion, and bacon
  2. Serve hamburgers, topped with sauce and tomato on toast
  3. Makes 6

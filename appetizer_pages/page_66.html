Corned Beef Funburger

Ingredients

  • 3 hamburger rolls, sliced, heated
  • 1 (4 and 1/2-oz.) can Underwood Corned Beef Spread
  • 3/4 tsp. horseradish
  • 3 Tbs. chopped walnuts
  • 3 green pepper rings
  • 3 Tbs. drained crushed pineapple

Prep Steps

  1. Partially hollow top half of each roll
  2. Combine corned beef spread and horseradish, spread on bottom half
  3. Sprinkle corned beef mixture with nuts, top with a pepper ring
  4. Fill ring with pineapple and cover with top half of roll
  5. Makes 3 sandwiches

Hamburgers Angostura

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 2 tsps. Angostura
  • 1/2 cup ice water

Prep Steps

  1. Combine meat with salt and pepper
  2. Mix Angostura with ice water and pour over meat
  3. Blend thoroughly and shape into 8 patties
  4. Broil or pan-fry hamburgers quickly to desired degree of doneness
  5. Serve hot with sauce(below:)

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp. Angostura
  • 1 Tbs. minced onion
  • 2 Tbs. minced green pepper
  • 2 Tbs. minced celery

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and simmer 10 minutes
  2. Makes 8 patties

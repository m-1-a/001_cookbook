Super Burger Topper

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 Tbs. salad oil
  • 1/2 cup Heinz Tomato Ketchup
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. Saute green pepper and onion in salad oil until tender
  2. Stir in ketchup, water, chili powder and salt
  3. Simmer 10 to 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Serve on hot dogs, hamburgers or other meat sandwiches
  5. Makes about 1 cup

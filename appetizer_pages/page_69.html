Bavarian Wranglers Franks

Ingredients

  • 4 Wranglers Smoked Franks
  • 1 and 1/3 cups prepared German potato salad
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 4 slices crusty French bread
  • Hormel bacon bits

Prep Steps

  1. Split meaty, smoke tasting Wranglers Franks almost to one end
  2. Slash sides lightly and form into circles, fastening with toothpicks
  3. Place on baking sheet
  4. Stir parsley into potato salad; spoon 1/3 cup into each frank
  5. Bake for 10 minutes at 350F
  6. Top each bread slice with stuffed frank; garnish with bacon bits
  7. Makes 4

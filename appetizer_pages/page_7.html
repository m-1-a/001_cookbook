Deviled Ham Stuffed Cucumbers

Ingredients

  • 2 medium cucumbers
  • 1 (4 and 1/2-ounce) can Underwood Deviled Ham
  • 1 hard-cooked egg, coarsely chopped
  • 1 Tbs. finely chopped onion
  • 1 Tbs. finely chopped sour pickle
  • 1 tsp. prepared mustard

Prep Steps

  1. Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise and scoop out seeds
  2. In a bowl, mix together deviled ham, chopped egg, onion, pickle and mustard
  3. Spoon mixture into cucumber shells
  4. Chill
  5. When ready to serve, cut cucumber diagonally into 1 inch pieces
  6. Makes about 2 dozen

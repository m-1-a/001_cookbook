Spicy Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup wheat germ
  • 2 Tbs. chopped chilies
  • 1/4 tsp. basil, dried and crushed
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 4 to 6 whole wheat English muffin halves, toasted
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Minced parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Combine first 6 ingredients and mound on English muffin halves
  2. On each muffin, place a slice of tomato and criss-cross with 2 strips of sliced Cheddar cheese
  3. Broil 3 to 4 inches from source of heat for 5 minutes, or until cheese melts
  4. Sprinkle with minced parsley
  5. Makes 4 to 6 open-faced sandwiches

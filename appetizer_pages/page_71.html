    Prep Steps

    1. For cheese sauce, mix together grated cheese, Pet Milk, salt and dry mustard
    2. Cook over boiling water until cheese melts and mixture is smooth, stirring frequently
    3. Add kidney beans and keep warm until needed
    4. Turn on oven and set at 375F
    5. Toast bread on one side only, then put bread, toasted side up, in shallow baking pan
    6. Arrange sliced tomatoes on top of toast and sprinkle with salt and pepper
    7. Bake until tomatoes are thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes
    8. Put on serving plate and serve at once with cheese sauce

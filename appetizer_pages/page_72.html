French's Tomato Rarebit

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, halved
  • Sugar
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 Tbs. (1/4) stick butter
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
  • 2 tsps. French's Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 lb. sharp Cheddar cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1/2 cup beer or ale
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup whipping cream or milk
  • Toast

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat broiler
  2. Sprinkle tomatoes generously with sugar, salt and pepper
  3. Place on baking sheet and broil just until tender, turning once to cook both sides
  4. Turn oven to low and keep tomatoes warm
  5. Melt butter in top of double boiler or chafing dish over simmering water
  6. Add mustards, Worcestershire, paprika and salt and stir well to blend
  7. Add cheese, making sure water does not boil or cheese will become tough
  8. Allow most of cheese to melt, stirring frequently
  9. Add beer and continue stirring until cheese is completely melted
  10. Beat yolks with cream and add slowly to cheese mixture, stirring until blended and thickened
  11. Spoon immediately over warm tomatoes
  12. Arrange toast around edge of dish
  13. Makes 4 servings

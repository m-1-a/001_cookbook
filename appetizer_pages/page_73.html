Chicken and Asparagus Rarebit

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 and 3/4-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 cup shredded Chedar cheese
  • 1 (5-oz) can Swanson Chunk Chicken
  • Cooked asparagus spears
  • Toast

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, combine soup, water and cheese
  2. Heat until cheese melts; stir occasionally
  3. Add chicken; heat
  4. Arrange asparagus on toast
  5. Pour chicken mixture over asparagus
  6. Makes about 2 and 1/2 cups, or 3 servings

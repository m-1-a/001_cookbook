Cheese Fondue

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sauterne or other dry white wine
  • 1 clove of large garlic, minced
  • 1 lb. natural Swiss cheese, cubed or shredded
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 (11-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup
  • French or Italian bread cubes

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan or fondue pot, simmer wine and garlic
  2. Combine cheese and flour; gradually blend into wine
  3. Heat until cheese melts; stir often
  4. Blend in soup
  5. Heat, stirring until smooth
  6. Spear bread with fork or toothpick and dip into fondue
  7. Makes about 4 cups

Unsalted Nut Butter

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Planters Dry Roasted Unsalted Cashews or Unsalted Peanuts
  • 3 Tbs. Planters Oil
  • 1 tsp. honey

Prep Steps

  1. Place Planters Dry Roasted Unsalted Cashews or Unsalted Peanuts in blender or food processor
  2. Blend on high speed until coarsely chopped
  3. Continue blending, adding Planters Oil in a steady stream
  4. Add honey and blend until smooth and creamy
  5. Makes 1/4 cup

Mushroom Liver Pate

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2/3 cup chopped fresh mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 2 (4 and 3/4-ounce) can Underwood Liverwurst Spread
  • 2 Tbs. brandy
  • 2 tsps. dried chives
  • 1/2 tsp. Ac'cent flavor enhancer

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium low heat
  2. Add mushrooms and onions
  3. Saute for 10 minutes, until all moisture has evaporated
  4. Stir in liverwurst spread, brandy, chives and flavor enhancer
  5. Spoon into a serving bowl
  6. Chill
  7. Serve cold with crackers
  8. Makes 1 and 1/2 cups spread

Roast Chuck Neapolitan

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 and 1/2-lb.) boned chuck roast (about 2 inches thick)
  • 2 Tbs. shortening
  • 1 (10 and 3/4-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup sliced onion
  • 1 clove large garlic, minced
  • 2 tsps. oregano, crushed
  • Generous dash pepper

Prep Steps

  1. In large heavy pan, brown meat in shortening
  2. Pour off fat
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients
  4. Cover; cook over low heat for 2 and 1/2 hours or until tender
  5. Spoon of fat
  6. Thicken sauce if desired
  7. Makes 6 servings

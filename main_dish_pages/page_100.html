Broiled Chicken Italiano

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 to 3 lb. chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup Wish-Bone Italian Dressing

Prep Steps

  1. Place chicken on broiler rack, sprinkle with salt and brush with Wish-Bone Italian Dressing
  2. Broil for about 45 minutes, basting with remaining dressing and turning chicken frequently frequently
  3. Makes about 4 serving

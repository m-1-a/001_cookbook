Tangy Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 to 2 and 1/2 lbs. broiler-fryer pieces
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup Heinz 57 Sauce
  • 1/2 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. Brown chicken in skillet in butter
  2. Combine 57 Sauce and water; pour over chicken
  3. Cover and simmer, basting occasionally, for 30 to 40 minutes or until chicken is tender
  4. Remove the cover the last 10 minutes of cooking
  5. Skim excess fat from sauce before serving
  6. Spoon sauce over chicken
  7. Makes 4 servings (about 3/4 cup sauce)

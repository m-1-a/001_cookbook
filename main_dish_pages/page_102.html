Baked Chicken That Makes Its Own Gravy

Ingredients

  • 3 to 3 and 1/2 lbs. frying chicken pieces
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2/3 cup undiluted Carnation Evaporated Milk
  • 1 (10 and 3/4 ounces) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup (4 ounces) grated process American cheese
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 2 cups (1-lb.) can drained whole onions
  • 1/4 lb. sliced mushrooms
  • Dash paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Coat chicken with flour and arrange chicken in single layer with skin down in melted butter in 13 * 9 * 2-inch baking dish
  2. Bake uncovered in 425F oven for 30 minutes and turn, bake until brown, about 15 to 20 minutes longer until tender
  3. Remove from oven and reduce temperature to 325F and pour off excess fat
  4. Combine evaporated milk, soup, cheese, salt and pepper then add onions and mushrooms to chicken
  5. Pour soup mixture over chicken and sprinkle with paprika
  6. Cover dish with foil and return to oven for 15 to 20 minutes
  7. Serves 6

Chicken with Lime

Ingredients

  • 1 broiler-fryer chicken, cut in parts
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 cup corn oil
  • 1 Tbs. minced onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cummin
  • 1/2 tsp. ground coriander seed
  • 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. turmeric
  • 2 Tbs. soy sauce
  • 2 limes, quarted

Prep Steps

  1. Sprinkle chicken with salt and black pepper and heat corn oil in a large frying pan over medium heat
  2. Add chicken and brown on all sides, push chicken to side of the pan, and add onion and garlic; saute until golden
  3. Add cumin, coriander, red pepper and turmeric, stir around to distribute, and add soy sauce
  4. Reduce heat and cook slowly, covered, stirring frequently about 20 minutes or until fork can be inserted in chicken with ease
  5. Serve over rice and squeeze 1 lime on chicken before serving, section remaining lime to squeeze on chicken while eating
  6. Makes 4 servings

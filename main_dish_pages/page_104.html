Progresso's Chicken Cacciatore

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 to 3 lb. spring chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup Progresso Pure Olive Oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. Progresso Basil
  • 1 Tbs. parsley
  • 1/4 cup Sherry, Marsala, or white wine
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 (17-ounce) can Progresso Peeled Tomatoes, cut up (or 1/2 (35-ounce) can)

Prep Steps

  1. Brown chicken pieces in hot olive oil until lightly colored; add onion, garlic, basil, and parsley and brown along with chicken until chicken until chicken is golden-brown and vegetables are soft and slightly colored
  2. Wet with wine & allow to evaporate and season with salt and pepper to taste
  3. Add tomatoes and their juices then cover and simmer for about 20 to 30 minutes or until chicken is tender and sauce has thickened a little

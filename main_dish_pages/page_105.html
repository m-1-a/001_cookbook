Easy Chicken Tetrazzini

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 envelope Ehlers Gravy Mix for Chicken
  • 1 and 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup cup cooking sherry
  • 2 cups cooked, cubed chicken
  • 1 (4-ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 2 Tbs. grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Prepare spaghetti as label directs; drain
  3. Place spaghetti in a greased 8-inch square baking dish
  4. Mix together remaining ingredients, except cheese
  5. Pour mixture over spaghetti
  6. Sprinkle with cheese
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until it becomes bubbly
  8. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving
  9. Makes 4 servings

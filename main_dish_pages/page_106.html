Chicken Macaroni Triumph

Ingredients

  • 3 whole chicken breasts, boned, skin removed
  • 3 slices boiled ham, cut in half
  • 3 slices American cheese, cut in half
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 (10 and 3/4 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup milk or light cream
  • 8 ounces (3 and 1/2 to 4 cups) Mueller's Sea Shell Macaroni
  • 1 (9 ounce) package frozen cut green beans, cooked

Prep Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast in half; pound with flat side of knife and on each piece of chicken place a piece of ham and cheese
  2. Roll, tucking cheese in; fasten with toothpicks and brown rolls in butter in a skillet; remove toothpicks; and place in a 11 * 8 * 2-inch baking dish
  3. Blend soup, wine, and milk into drippings; season to taste with salt and pepper; pour over chicken and bake at 350F for about 45 minutes or until tender
  4. Cook macaroni as directed on package; drain and in a large serving dish, combine green beans and macaroni; stir in sauce from chicken; arrange chicken rolls on top
  5. Makes 4 to 6 servings

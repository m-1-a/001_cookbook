Avocado With Curried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter (1/2 stick)
  • 1/2 cup chopped pared apple
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 Tbs. curry powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup light cream
  • 1 cup chicken bouillon
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 2 cups cut up, cooked chicken
  • 3 or 4 avocados, halved and peeled
  • 3 to 4 cups cooked rice
  • Condiments, given below

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, saute apple, onion, garlic and curry powder in butter until onion is crisp-tender and stir in flour
  2. Gradually add cream and bouillon; cook & stir until sauce boils 1 minute and add salt, pepper and chicken
  3. Cook over low heat. about 10 minutes and arrange avocados halves on rice in heat proof serving dish
  4. Heat in 350F oven for about 5 minutes and spoon curried chicken over avocado halves
  5. Serve with Indian or Euphrates bread and a choice of these condiments: chopped egg, crumbled bacon, sweet mixed pickles, coconut, raisins, chutney, Bombay duck, preserved ginger, chopped peanuts
  6. Makes 6 servings

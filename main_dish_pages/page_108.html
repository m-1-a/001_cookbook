Baked Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. corn oil margarine
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onioin
  • 1/2 cup Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise or Best Foods Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup dairy sour cream
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup silvered alomonds, toasted
  • 1 (6 ounces) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1/4 cup crushed potato chips

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet melt margarine over medium heat and add celery and onion; cook for 4 minutes or until tender
  2. Remove from heat and stir in next 5 ingredients until well blended
  3. Add chicken, alomonds and mushrooms; toss to coat well and spoon into 1 and 1/2-quart casserole
  4. Sprinkle with potato chips and bake in 325F oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until hot
  5. Makes 4 to 6 servings

Microwave Directions:

Prep Steps

  1. In 1 and 1/2-quart microprooof casserole dish microwave margarine with full power until melted
  2. Stir in celery and onion; microwave for 3 minutes
  3. Stir in next 5 ingredients
  4. Add chicken, almonds and mushrooms; toss to coat well
  5. Microwave for 5 minutes or until hot, stirring once and sprinkling with potato chips after 3 minutes

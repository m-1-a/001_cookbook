Oven Baked Short Ribs with Garden Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. beef short ribs
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 lb. carrots, cleaned and halved
  • 1 lb. potatoes, pared and halved
  • 1/2 lb. fresh green beans
  • 4 small white onions
  • 1 (13 and 3/4-oz.) can beef broth
  • 2 tsps. prepared mustard
  • 2 Tbs. horseradish
  • 2 Tbs Argo Kingsford's Corn Starch
  • 1/4 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. Trim excess fat from meat
  2. Place in 13 * 9 * 2-inch baking pan; sprinkle with salt and pepper
  3. Bake uncovered in a 350F oven for 2 hours; drain fat
  4. Add next 4 ingredients
  5. Mix broth, horseradish and mustard; pour over meat and vegetables
  6. Cover with foil; bake for 1 to 1 and 1/2 hours longer or until tender
  7. Arrange meat and vegetables on serving platter; keep warm
  8. Strain broth; remove excess fat
  9. Add water, if necessary to make 2 cups and return to baking pan
  10. Mix Corn Starch and 1/4 cup cool water; stir into pan
  11. Bring to boil for 1 minute
  12. Serve gravy with meat and vegetables
  13. Makes 4 servings

Tyson's Cornish Hens Steamed in Foil

Ingredients

  • 4 Tyson brand Cornish Hens, thawed
  • For each Cornish Hen:
  • 1 square of foil, 16 inches at least
  • 1/4 tsp. sage or thyme
  • 1 sprig parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and season each hen with salt, pepper, sage, or thyme
  2. Place on foil, lay a sprig of parsley on top of hen and wrap securely in foil and place the hens on a baking sheet or in a baking pan
  3. Place in oven for 1 hour then remove hens and raise the heat to 425F
  4. Carefully open foil (watch out for escaping steam) to expose hens then brush with butter and return to oven to brown for 5 to 10 minutes
  5. Serve hens with juices accumulated in foil
  6. Makes 4 servings

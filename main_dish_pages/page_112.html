Crispy Fried Fish

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup French's Prepared Mustard
  • 1 tsp. French's Parsley Flakes
  • 1/2 tsp. French's Seafood Seasoning
  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. fish fillets
  • French's Big Tate Mashed Potato Flakes
  • Oil or melted shortening

Prep Steps

  1. Beat toghther eggs, mustard, parsley, and seafood seasoning
  2. Dip fish in the mustard mixture; roll in potato flakes
  3. Fry in hot oil or shortening, around 3 to 4 minutes on each side or until fish flakes easily and is golden brown
  4. Makes 4 servings

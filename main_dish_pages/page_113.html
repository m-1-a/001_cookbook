Veal Scaloppine al Marsala

Ingredients

  • 4 veal scallops (about 1 lb. cut from leg, each about 1/8-inch thick)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 Tbs butter or margarine
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 lb. mushrooms sliced
  • 1/2 cup Holland's House Marsala Cooking Wine

Prep Steps

  1. Dredge veal in flour and add salt & pepper to taste and melt 3 Tbs butter or margarine in skillet; brown garlic
  2. Then add veal, browning quickly then remove veal from shallow baking pan
  3. Add 1 Tbs. butter to skillet and brown mushrooms and also add Holland's House Marsala Cooking Wine and bring to a boil
  4. Simmer for approximately 2 minutes to blend flavors and pour wine-mushroom sauce over veal
  5. Cover and pan bake for approximately 20 minutes at 350F
  6. Serves 2 to 3

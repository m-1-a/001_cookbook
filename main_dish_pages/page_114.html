Broiled Chicken Deluxe

Ingredients

  • 1 broiler-fryer chicken, halved
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted, divided
  • 2 tsps. granulated sugar

Prep Steps

  1. In broiler pan place chicken skin side up and rub surface of chicken with lemon, squeezing some juice onto meat
  2. In bowl mix together salt, pepper and paprika and sprinkle the mixture over chicken
  3. Brush with 1/2 butter; sprinkle with sugar then place broiler pan as far from heat as possible and cook at 450F for 10 minutes allowing seasonings to penetrate
  4. Move broiler pan up so that chicken is 3 to 6 inches from the heat in gas range or 6 to 9 inches from heat in an eclectic range
  5. Broil, basting frequently with remaninig butter and turning to insure even browning, for about 35 minutes or until fork can be inserted in chicken with ease
  6. Makes 2 large servings

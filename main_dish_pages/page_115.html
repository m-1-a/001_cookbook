Chicken Marseillaise

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 and 1/2 to 3 lb.) broiler-fryer, cut up
  • Kraft Catalina French Dressing
  • 1 (16-oz.) can tomatoes
  • 8 onion slices, 1/4-inch thick
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. celery seed
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 cup wine or water
  • 2 Tbs. flour

Prep Steps

  1. Brown chicken in 1/3 cup dressing over low heat
  2. Add 1/4 cup cup dressing, tomatoes, onions and seasonings
  3. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes
  4. Remove chicken and vegetables to serving platters
  5. Gradually add wine to flour, stirring until well blended
  6. Gradually add flour mixture to hot liquid in pan; cook stirring constantly
  7. Serve with chicken and vegetables
  8. Makes 4 servings

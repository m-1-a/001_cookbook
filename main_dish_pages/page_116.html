Chicken Jardiniere au Vermouth

Ingredients

  • 1 (3-lb.) broiler chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 Tbs. oil
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 small zucchini
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1/4 tsp. thyme
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup Holland House Vermouth Cooking Wine
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 small can whole mushrooms
  • 2 tsps. corn starch

Prep Steps

  1. Rub chicken with garlic
  2. Heat oil in saucepan, add chicken and brown on both sides
  3. Add fresh vegetables (all cut into 2-inch by 1/4-inch sticks), thyme, pepper and Holland House Vermouth Cooking Wine
  4. Stir to de glaze the pan and cover and simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Add peas and mushrooms then stir in corn starch / water mixture
  6. Bring to a boil, and simmer for 5 more minutes and serve
  7. Makes 6 servings

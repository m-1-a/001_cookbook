Orange Glazed Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Pompeian Olive Oil
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 Tbs. wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. instant chopped onion
  • 1/4 tsp. ginger or cumin
  • 1 chicken, about 3 lbs.

Prep Steps

  1. Combine olive oil, juice, vinegar and salt; crush the instant onion with mortar and pestle or back of wooden spoon, blend with the ginger or cumin
  2. Add to the juice and then marinate either the whole or cut up chicken in the sauce for 1 hour or longer; baste with the marinade as it cooks over charcoal or in the broiler or rotisserie oven
  3. Makes 1 cup sauce

Oven Fried Chicken and Bananas

Ingredients

  • 2 chickens, about 3 lbs. each, cut up
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup Coco Cream of Coconut
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 6 medium size bananas
  • 2 and 1/2 cups cornflake crumbs
  • 3/4 cup melted butter or margarine

Prep Steps

  1. Sprinkle chicken pieces on all sides with salt and pepper next in a bowl, mix Coco Casa Cream of Coconut and lemon juice
  2. Peel bananas and cut each banana into halves crosswise and brush chicken and bananas thickly with coconut mixture and roll in crumbs, pressing firmly to make them adhere
  3. Brush a baking pan with some of the butter then place chicken pieces in a single layer into a pan and drizzle with half of the butter
  4. Bake in preheated 350F oven for 45 minutes
  5. Add bananas and drizzle with the remaining butter then bake for another 15 minutes
  6. Serves 6

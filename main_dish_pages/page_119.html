Oven Fried Chicken with Gravy

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 cups Pepperidge Farm Herb Seasoned Stuffing, crushed
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 to 3 lb. chicken, cut up
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Gravy

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F and put butter in shallow baking pan and place in oven until melted
  2. Meanwhile combine crushed stuffing and salt in a plastic bag then dip chicken parts in egg that has been beaten with water
  3. Then shake, a few pieces at a time, in bag until well coated next place chicken, skin side down, in melted butter
  4. Do not layer in pan and bake uncovered for 30 minutes, turn and continue baking for 15 minutes
  5. Serves 4

Gravy:

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 and 3/4-oz.) can chicken gravy
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Prep Steps

  1. Combine canned gravy with sour cream and heat to just below boiling

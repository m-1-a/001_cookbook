Po-Man's Ribs and Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 lbs. short ribs
  • 1 (10 and 1/2-oz.) can condensed onion soup
  • 1 (4-oz.) can mushrooms
  • 1 cup beef bouillon
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 to 1 tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
  • Hot cooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. Brown short ribs in Dutch oven
  2. Combine remaining ingredients except rice, plus 1/2 cup water
  3. Pour over ribs
  4. Cover and simmer about 2 and 1/2 hours
  5. Serve with hot cooked rice
  6. Makes 4 servings

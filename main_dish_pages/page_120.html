Baked Yogurt Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 cut-up frying chicken, 2 and 1/2 to 3 lbs.
  • Salt and pepper
  • 6 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1 Tbs. paprika
  • 2 cups Dannon Plain Yogurt
  • 1/4 lb. fresh mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
  • 2 Tbs fresh lemon juice
  • 2 Tbs. chopped fresh dill or parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Wash chicken pieces and wipe dry then add salt and pepper
  2. In a large pan, melt 4 Tbs. of butter; fry chicken until golden brown
  3. Remove to buttered shallow baking dish next sprinkle flour and paprika into pan juices and cook, stirring for 1 minutes
  4. Stir in yogurt and mix well and spoon over chicken then saute mushrooms in the remaining 2 Tbs. of butter and lemon juice for 1 minute and spoon over pan
  5. Sprinkle with dill and bake covered in a preheated oven (325F) for about 1 and 1/4 hours, or until chicken is tender
  6. Serves about 4

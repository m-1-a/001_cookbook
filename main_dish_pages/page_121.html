Sweet 'N' Smoky Oven Barbecued Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 broiler fryer chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp. hickory smoked salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • Barbecue Sauce

Prep Steps

  1. Place chicken, skin side up in a baking pan and pour water around chicken
  2. Tuck onion slices in and around the chicken next sprinkle with hickory smoked salt and pepper
  3. Bake chicken uncovered for 30 minutes in 375F oven and mix catsup and mustard, add oil, vinegar and maple syrup
  4. Pour Barbecue Sauce over chicken and bake 30 minutes longer, or until fork can be inserted in chicken with ease
  5. Makes 4 servings

Barbecue Sauce:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup catsup
  • 2 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Prep Steps

  1. Combine above ingredients

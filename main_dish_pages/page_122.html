Chicken Argentina

Ingredients

  • 1 frying chicken (3 lbs.), cut in pieces
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 3/4 tsp. paprika
  • 1 and 1/2 cups pitted California ripe olives
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 medium green pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium red pepper, sliced
  • 1 and 1/2 cups orange juice

Prep Steps

  1. Marinate chicken pieces in juice from the lime (approximately 1 and 1/2) for at least 30 minutes
  2. Combine salt, pepper and paprika; sprinkle over chicken then brown on all sides in oil
  3. Place chicken in baking dish and olives, onion, green pepper, red pepper, and orange juice
  4. Cover and bake at 350F for 45 minutes or until chicken is tender
  5. Remove chicken to platter and serve with pan juices
  6. Serves 4

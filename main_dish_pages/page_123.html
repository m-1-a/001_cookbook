Pettengill Schoolhouse Chicken Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. margarine
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbs. Argo Kingsford's Corn Starch
  • 1 and 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 and 1/2 cups cooked chicken, cut into bite size pieces
  • 2 cups fresh peas, cooked
  • 1 whole pimiento, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp. dried thyme leaves
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 recipe double crust pastry

Prep Steps

  1. In skillet melt margarine and add mushrooms & garlic then saute over medium heat until lightly brown
  2. In a saucepan stir together Corn Starch and milk until smooth, next mix in mushrooms, garlic and pan drippings
  3. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly, and boil for 1 minute
  4. Stir in next 6 ingredients and pour into pastry lined 9-inch pie plate and cover pie with pastry; seal and flute edge
  5. Cut slits in top then bake in 375F oven for 35 minutes or until crust is golden brown
  6. Serves 6 to 8

Country Style Chicken Kiev

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 2 Tbs. grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp. each: basil leaves and oregano leaves
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic salt
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 2/3 cup Land O Lakes Sweet Cream Butter, melted
  • 2 chicken breasts, split (about 1 and 1/2 lbs.)
  • 1/4 cup white wine or apple juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375F and combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, basil, oregano, garlic salt and salt
  2. Dip chicken brest in melted butter, then coat with crumb mixture; reserve remaining butter then place chicken skin side up in an un-greased 9-inch square baking dish
  3. Bake 50 to 60 minutes for minutes or until chicken is for tender, meanwhile add wine, green onion and parsley to reserved melted butter (about 1/2 cup )
  4. When chicken is golden brown, pour butter sauce over chicken and continue baking for 3 to 5 minutes or until sauce is heated through
  5. Serve with sauce spooned over
  6. Makes 4 servings

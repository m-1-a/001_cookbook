Indian Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 2 tart apples, peeled and chopped
  • 1 Tbs. curry powder
  • 6 Tbs. flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup Coco Casa Cream of Coconut
  • 2 cups (1 pint) half and half
  • 3 cups diced cooked chicken, turkey, or lamb
  • Salt and pepper

Prep Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, melt butter and saute onion, celery and apples for 5 minutes then stir in curry and flour
  2. Gradually stir in chicken broth, cream of coconut and half & half then stir over moderate heat until sauce bubbles and thickens
  3. Stir in chicken and season to taste with salt and pepper
  4. Serve spooned over rice
  5. Makes 6 to 8 servings

Glaze Turkey Roast

Ingredients

  • Turkey Roast
  • Apricot jam or preserves
  • 1 cup dried apricots
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 (8-oz.) package Pepperidge Farm Herb Seasoned Cube Stuffing
  • 1/2 cup silvered almonds
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Turkey roasts vary from 2 to 4 and 1/2 lbs. in size. They can be all white meat or a combination of white and dark. Some come with prepared gravy. For the best results use a meat thermometer and roast to 185F. Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing. Allow 1/3 lb. meat for each serving

Prep Steps

  1. Roast turkey according to package directions and for the glaze, brush the top of the roast with the apricot jam frequently during the last 30 minutes
  2. Meanwhile place the apricots, water, brown sugar and lemon juice in a small saucepan and simmer for 5 minutes then drain, reserving the liquid
  3. Add water to liquid to make 1 cup then place in a large saucepan with butter and heat until butter is melted
  4. Stir in stuffing, almonds and salt, next cut apricots in strips and add to mixture
  5. Place in a 1-quart baking dish and bake alongside of turkey for the last 25 minutes
  6. To serve: make a bed of dressing on a hot platter and place roast on top

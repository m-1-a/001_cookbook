Mexican Turkey Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Uncle Ben's Converted Brand Rice
  • 1 (about 3 ozs.) can mild green chilies, drained and chopped
  • 3 cups cubed cooked turkey
  • 1 (12-oz.) can Mexican style corn with sweet peppers, drained
  • 1 (10-oz.) can enchilada sauce
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup dairy sour cream

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare rice according to package directions and reserve 1 Tbs. chilies for garnish
  2. Combine remaining chilies, turkey, corn, enchilada sauce, salt and cooked rice in a large bowl then spoon into greased baking dish 12 * 7 and 1/2 * 2 inches
  3. Cover and bake at 350F, until hot, likely 25 minutes
  4. Spoon sour cream down center; garnish with reserved chilies
  5. Makes 6 servings

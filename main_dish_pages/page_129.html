Old Fashion Turkey Stuffing and Variations

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 cup butter or margarine
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (1-lb.) package Pepperidge Farm Herb-Seasoned Stuffing

Prep Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, saute the onion and celery in butter until tender but not browned
  2. Stir in water and then stuffing
  3. Makes enough to fill a 12 to 16 lb. turkey

Variation:

Prep Steps

  1. Try the following with a 1-lb. bag of herb seasoned stuffing, prepared according to package directions

Parsley:

Prep Steps

  1. 1 cup chopped parsley, 3/4 cup chopped onion and 1 cup chopped celery sauteed in 1 cup butter

Oriental:

Prep Steps

  1. 1/4 cup chopped onion, 1/2 cup chopped celery, 1 cup sliced mushrooms and 1/2 cup sliced water chestnuts sauteed in 1 cup butter

Nut:

Prep Steps

  1. 1 cup chopped celery, 1/2 cup onion and 1 cup nuts sauteed in 1 cup butter
  2. Almonds, Brazil nuts, chestnuts, filberts, pecans or walnuts may be used

Giblet:

Prep Steps

  1. Simer turkey giblets with seasoning for 2 to 3 hours
  2. Remove the liver after 10 to 20 minutes
  3. Drain, reserving broth; chop coarsely
  4. Saute 3/4 cup chopped onion and 1 cup chopped celery in 1 cup butter
  5. Use reserved broth in place of water in recipe
  6. Stir chopped giblets and liver into stuffing

Salisbury Steak, Onion Gravy

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 1/2-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Onion Soup
  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 1/4 cup catsup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. prepared mustard

Prep Steps

  1. In bowl, combine 1/3 cup Soup with beef, crumbs, egg, salt and pepper
  2. Shape into 6 oval patties
  3. In skillet, brown patties; pour off fat
  4. Gradually blend remaining soup into flour until smooth
  5. Add to skillet with remaining ingredients; stir to loosen browned bits
  6. Cover; cook over low heat for 20 minutes or until done
  7. Stir occasionally
  8. Makes 6 servings

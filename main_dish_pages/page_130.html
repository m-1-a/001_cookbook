Fish Fillets Sauterne

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup Regina Cooking Sauterne
  • 1/8 tsp.pepper
  • 1 Tbs. dried parsley flakes
  • 2 Tbs. white fish fillets(sole, flounder, haddock)

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan then stir in Sauterne, pepper and parsley
  2. Place fish in pan and spoon sauce over fish
  3. Simmer covered for 5 to 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork then remove fish to a warm platter
  4. Boil sauce until slightly thickened and spoon sauce over fish
  5. Serves 6

Sole in Almond Shrimp Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1 and 1/2 lbs. sole fillets
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 3 ozs. cooked baby shrimp
  • 4 Tbs. butter
  • 2 Tbs. all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup half and half
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 1/3 cup Blue Diamond Blanched Silvered Almonds, toasted

Prep Steps

  1. Poach fillets in wine in 350F oven until fish flakes easily with fork, but is still moist, 15 to 20 minutes. Do not over cook
  2. Reserve 1/4 cup shrimp; mash remaining with 2 Tbs. butter; set aside
  3. In small saucepan, heat 2 remaining Tbs. butter and add flour; cook for 2 to 3 minutes and gradually stir in half and half
  4. Cook and stir over medium heat until sauce begins to thicken, also stir 1/2 cup of fishing cooking liquid; continue cooking and stirring until sauce boils
  5. Reduce heat, add shrimp butter, salt and pepper; stir until butter melts next stir in 1/4 cup cup of the almonds
  6. Arrange and pour
  7. Makes 4 servings

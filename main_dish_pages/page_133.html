Fillets Baked in Sour Cream

Ingredients

  • 4 tsps. butter or margarine, divided
  • 2 lbs. fish fillets(sole, haddock, or flounder)
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
  • 1 Tbs. paprika
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup (8 ozs.) sour cream
  • 1/4 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Prep Steps

  1. Grease 2-quart baking dish with 1 tsp. of the butter
  2. Arrange fish in baking dish then blend salt, Tabasco pepper sauce, paprika, and Parmesan cheese into sour cream
  3. Spread over fish and top with bread crumbs and dot with the remaining 3 tsps. butter
  4. Bake uncovered in 350F oven for 30 minutes until fish is easily flaked with a fork and serve with lemon slices, if desired
  5. Makes 4 to 6 servings

Baked Fish Fillets Jose

Ingredients

  • 1 (7-oz.) package Ortega Taco Dinner (Shells, Seasoning and Sauce)
  • 2 lbs. fish fillets
  • 1/2 cup butter (or margarine), melted

Prep Steps

  1. Heat Taco Shells according to package directions
  2. In food processor or blender, finely crush shells and blend in Taco Seasoning
  3. Dip fish in butter then coat with crumbs
  4. Arrange in shallow baking dish
  5. Bake in preheated 425F oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until flakes easily with a fork
  6. Serve with Ortega Taco Sauce
  7. Makes 6 servings

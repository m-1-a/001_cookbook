Tuna Shortcake

Ingredients

  • 1 can Campbell's Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 (7-oz.) can tuna, drained and flaked
  • 1 cup cooked peas
  • 1 Tbs. chopped pimiento

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, combine ingredients
  2. Heat; stir often
  3. Serve over biscuits
  4. Makes about 2 and 1/2 cups

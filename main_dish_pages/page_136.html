Tuna Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup silvered almonds
  • 2 (6 and 1/2 oz.) cans tuna, drained
  • 1 (6-oz.) can cut green beans, drained
  • 2 (10 and 1/2 oz.) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
  • 1/3 cup sherry
  • 1 (10-oz.) package Pepperidge Farm frozen Patty Shells
  • 1 egg, well beaten

Prep Steps

  1. Thaw package of Patty Shells in refrigerator overnight or on a kitchen counter until workable, always keeping them cold to the touch
  2. In a skillet, heat butter and saute almonds until golden then stir in tuna, green beans, mushrooms, soup and sherry
  3. Pour mixture into a shallow 1 and 1/2-quart casserole and stack 3 Patty Shells one atop the other
  4. On a lightly floured surface roll out to about 4 * 9 inches then with a cookie cutter cut pastry into 2-inch rounds
  5. Repeat with remaining Patty Shells and place rounds on a cookie sheet and brush tops with beaten eggs
  6. Bake casserole and pastry rounds in 400F oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until rounds are puffed and brown
  7. Place puff pastry rounds over the top of the tuna casserole and serve at once
  8. Serves 6

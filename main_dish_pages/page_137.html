Salmon Sour Cream Puffs

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 and 1/2 oz.) can salmon, drained
  • 1 and 1/2 cups soft bread crumbs (about 4 slices)
  • 1 (8-oz.) container Borden Sour Cream
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2 Tbs. chopped chives
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 1 to 1 and 1/2 cups finely chopped almonds
  • Vegetable oil
  • Lemon wedges

Prep Steps

  1. Remove skin and bones from salmon then in a large bowl, combine salmon, crumbs, Bordon Sour Cream, egg yolkes, salt and pepper; mix well
  2. Beat egg whites until stiff; fold into salmon mixture ten on a large sheet of wax paper, sprinkle half of the almonds
  3. Drop heaping tablespoonfuls of salmon mixture onto almonds; sprinkle remaining almonds over top
  4. In a large skillet, heat oil; lift puffs with spatula into skillet and brown on each side
  5. Serve hot with lemon wedges
  6. Refrigerate leftovers
  7. Makes 6 servings

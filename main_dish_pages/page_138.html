Szechuan Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup minced bamboo shoots
  • 1/2 cup minced scallions
  • 1/4 tsp. minced fresh ginger roots
  • 3 cloves of large garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp. liquid hot pepper sauce
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 1/2 cup catsup
  • 3 Tbs. Dry Sack Sherry
  • 1 Tbs. soy sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp. sesame oil or 1 Tbs. toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 Tbs. corn starch
  • 3 Tbs. water
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Planters Peanut Oil
  • 1 lb. shelled and deveined raw shrimp

Prep Steps

  1. Combine bamboo shoots, scallions, ginger, garlic and red pepper sauce in a small bowl then in a second bowl combine sugar, catsup, Dry Sack Sherry, soy sauce sesame oil or seeds
  2. In a third bowl mix corn starch and water next heat Planters Peanut Oil in a wok or a large skillet to 400F
  3. Have ready a large strainer with a bowl underneath then add shrimp to hot oil, stirring until done, about 2 minutes
  4. Pour oil and shrimp into stainer to drain then heat 2 Tbs. of the strained oil in the same wok or skillet over high heat
  5. Add scallions mixture and stir-fry for 1 minute then add drained shrimp and continue to stir-fry for 30 seconds
  6. Pour in catsup mixture then cook and stir for 30 seconds
  7. Blend corn starch & water then add to wok next cook and stir until slightly thickened
  8. Serves 4

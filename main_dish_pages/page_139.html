Clam Crunch

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 Tbs. snipped parsley
  • 1 (6 and 1/2 ounce) can minced clams, drained (reserve liquid)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • Cooking oil
  • Sour cream, optional

Prep Steps

  1. In a medium bowl combine flour, baking powder, salt, pepper and parsley then slowly stir in clam liquid until smooth
  2. Add egg and clams then mix well and stir in Rice Chex to coat
  3. Let stand for 10 minutes then stir to combine
  4. Heat oil (1/8-inch deep) in skillet then drop 1 heaping tablespoon clam mixture into hot oil
  5. Pat with spoon to form 3-inch patty and repeat this process to make 8 patties
  6. Brown over medium heat, turn, brown, then drain on absorbent paper
  7. Serve immediately and top with sour cream if desired
  8. Makes 4 servings

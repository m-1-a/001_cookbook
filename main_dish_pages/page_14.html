Skillet Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup converted rice, cooked
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 1/4 cup minced, fresh onion
  • 3 Tbs. A.1 Steak Sauce
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (12-oz.) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 6 large green peppers
  • 6 medium carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 3 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. When rice is cooking, in medium skillet brown beef until crumbly
  2. Drain
  3. Add onion
  4. Cook until onion is soft
  5. Remove from heat
  6. Mix in A.1, salt, pepper, tomato sauce, corn and rice
  7. Cut tops, seeds and membranes from pepper; discard
  8. Fill peppers with meat and rice mixture
  9. In 10-inch skillet, place peppers and carrots
  10. Add butter and water
  11. Simmer and cover for 30 to 40 minutes or until carrots are tender
  12. Serves 6

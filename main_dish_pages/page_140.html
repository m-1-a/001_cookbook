Scrumptious Shrimp Supreme

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-ounces) bottle Wish-Bone Italian Dressing
  • 1 cup chili sauce
  • 2 Tbs. corn syrup
  • 2 lbs. large shrimp, shelled and cleaned
  • 4 lemons, cut into wedges

Prep Steps

  1. In a large bowl, blend Wish-Bone Italian Dressing with chili sauce and corn syrup; add shrimp
  2. Cover and marinate in refrigerator for 2 hours, turning occasionally
  3. On skewers, alternate thread shrimp and lemons
  4. Grill or boil, turning and basting frequently with remaining marinade, about 15 minutes or until shrimp is done
  5. Makes about 6 servings

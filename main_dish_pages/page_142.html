Southern Shrimp and Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced green pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, mashed
  • 1 (1 pound) tomatoes
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • Dash cayenne
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 8 ounces cooked shrimp
  • 6 ounces (3 cups) Mueller's Dumplings Macaroni

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in skillet; add onion, green pepper and garlic; cook until crisp-tender but not brown
  2. Stir in tomatoes and seasonings; cover and simmer for about 10 minutes
  3. Add shrimp; heat a few minutes; remove bay leaves meanwhile cook macaroni as directed on package; drain
  4. Serve shrimp mixture over macaroni
  5. Makes 4 servings

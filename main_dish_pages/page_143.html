Seven Seas Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/3 cups water
  • 1 (10 and 3/4 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom or celery soup
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion, optional
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice, optional
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash of pepper
  • 1 and 1/3 cups Minute Rice
  • 1 (10-ounce) package Bird Eyes 5 Minute Sweet Green Peas, partially thawed
  • 1 (7-ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese
  • Paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Combine water, soup, onion, lemon juice, salt and pepper in a saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring occasionally, over medium heat
  2. Pour about half of soup mixture into a greased 1 and 1/2 quart casserole then in separate layers, add rice, peas and tuna
  3. Add remaining soup mixture and sprinkle with cheese and paprika
  4. Cover and bake at 375F for 10 minutes, then stir, next cover and continue baking for 10 to 15 minutes longer
  5. Makes about 5 and 1/2 cups or 4 servings

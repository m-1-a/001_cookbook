Perfect Tuna Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 and 3/4 ounce) can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Celery or Mushroom Soup
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 hard-cooked eggs, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked peas
  • 1 can tuna (about 7 ounces), drained and flakes
  • 1/4 cup slightly crumbled potato chips

Prep Steps

  1. In 1-quart casserole, blend soup and milk; stir in tuna, eggs and peas
  2. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes or until hot; stir
  3. Top with chips; bake for 5 minutes more
  4. Makes about 4 cups

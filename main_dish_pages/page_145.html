Pacific Salmon Loaf & Cucumber Sauce

Ingredients for Pacific Salmon Loaf

  • 1 (16-ounce) can salmon, drained, flaked
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Kraft Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Cumber Sauce

Prep Steps

  1. Combine ingredients except Cucumber Sauce; mix lightly
  2. Shape into loaf in shallow baking dish
  3. Bake at 350F for 40 minutes
  4. Serve with Cucumber Sauce

Ingredients for Cucumber Sauce

  • 1/2 cup Kraft Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup dairy sour cream
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 2 Tbs. chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp. dill weed

Prep Steps

  1. Combine ingredients; mix well
  2. Makes 6 to 8 servings

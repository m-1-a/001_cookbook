Boatman's Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. firm-fleshed white fish (cod, haddock or halibut), cut in large chunks
  • Salt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 Tbs. Wesson Oil
  • 1 (6-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp. each: red and black pepper
  • 1 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 1/3 cup dry white wine
  • 6 slices of Italian bread (toasted, if desired)

Prep Steps

  1. Sprinkle fish with 1/2 tsp. salt; let stand for 1 hour
  2. Lightly brown onion in hot oil; drain
  3. Stir in water, Hunt's Tomato Paste, red pepper, black pepper, 1 and 1/2 tsp. salt, parsley and wine
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes then add fish and continue to simmer for about 10 minutes longer or just until fish flakes easily with a fork
  5. To serve, place a bread in each soup bowl; ladle soup over
  6. Makes 6 servings

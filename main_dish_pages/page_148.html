Avocados with Curried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter (1/2 stick)
  • 1/2 cup chopped pared apple
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 Tbs. curry powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup light cream
  • 1 cup chicken bouillon
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cut up or 1 and 1/2 lbs. shrimp, shelled and cleaned
  • 3 or 4 avocados, halved and peeled
  • 3-4 cooked rice
  • Condiments, given below

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan: saute apple, onion, garlic and curry powder in the butter until onion is crisp-tender next stir in flour
  2. Gradually add cream and bouillon; cook and stir until sauce boils; 1 minute then add salt, pepper and chicken or shrimp
  3. Cook over low heat for 10 minutes then arrange avocado halves on rice in a heatproof serving dish and heat in 350F (moderate) oven for about 5 minutes
  4. Spoon curried chicken or shrimp over avocado halves
  5. Serve with Indian or Euphrates bread and a choice of these condiments, coconut, raisin, chutney, Bombay duck, preserved ginger, chopped peanuts
  6. Makes 6-8 servings

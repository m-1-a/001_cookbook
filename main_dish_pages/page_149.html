Stuffed Flounder Supreme

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Wish-Bone Deluxe French Dressing
  • 1 lb. flounder fillets
  • 3/4 cup seasoned croutons, crushed
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped celery

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and brush 2 Tbs. Wish-Bone Deluxe French Dressing on top side of fillets
  2. In a small bowl, combine 3/4 cup dressing, croutons, and celery; equally divide mixture on fillets and roll up
  3. Brush fillets with remaining dressing and bake for 35 minutes or until fish flakes
  4. Makes about 4 servings

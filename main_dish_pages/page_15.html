Quick Chinese Pepper Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. round steak, cut 1/2 inch thick
  • 2 Tbs. salad oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 medium green pepper, sliced
  • 1 envelope Ehlers Au Jus Gravy Mix
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 4 tsps. soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. ground ginger
  • 3 cups hot cooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. Cut meat in half lengthwise with a sharp knife, then crosswise into thin slices
  2. Cook meat, onion and pepper in skillet in hot oil until meat is browned
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients except rice
  4. Cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly
  5. Serve over rice
  6. Makes 4 servings

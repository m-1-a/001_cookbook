Flounder Au Gratin

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound flounder or sole fillets
  • 1/4 cup Hellmann's Best Foods Real Mayonnaise

Prep Steps

  1. In a shallow dish or on sheet of waxed paper combine crumbs and cheese
  2. Brush all sides of fillets with Real Mayonnaise; coat with crumb mixture
  3. Arrange in single layer in shallow baking pan
  4. Bake in 375F oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden and fish flakes easily
  5. Makes 4 servings

Barbecued Red Snapper

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. red snapper steaks or fillets
  • 1 (6-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 Tbs. lime juice
  • 2 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 Tbs. Wesson Oil
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. garlic salt

Prep Steps

  1. Thaw fish if frozen; cut into serving size portions then combine remaining ingredients in a small bowl
  2. Arrange fish on grill or broiler pan for about 4 inches from heat source and brush generously with sauce
  3. Cook for 6-10 minutes and turn
  4. Brush with more sauce and cook for 7-10 minutes longer or just until fish flakes when tested with a fork (length of cooking time depends on thickness of fish)
  5. Do not over cook
  6. Makes 6 servings

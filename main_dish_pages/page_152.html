Imperial Fish Baltimore

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. butter or margarine, divided
  • 1/2 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tsps. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. powdered mustard
  • 2 cups flaked cooked white fish
  • 3/4 cup soft bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped pimiento
  • 1/2 tsp. paprika

Prep Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan melt 1 Tbs. of the butter and add green pepper; saute for 2 minutes
  2. Remove from heat; stir in mayonnaise, Lea & Perrins, salt and mustard next gently blend in fish
  3. Turn into a buttered 1-quart casserole then in a small saucepan melt remaining 2 Tbs. butter
  4. Stir in bread crumbs, pimiento, and paprika and sprinkle over fish mixture
  5. Bake in a preheated moderate oven (350F) until crumbs are golden, about 30 minutes
  6. Makes 4 servings

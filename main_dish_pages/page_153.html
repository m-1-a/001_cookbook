Stuffin'-Topped Halibut

5

Ingredients

  • 2 cups soft bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Squeeze Parkay Margarine
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 tsp. sage
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 4 frozen halibut steak, 3/4-inch thick (thawed)

Prep Steps

  1. Combine crumbs, margarine, celery, onion and seasoning; mix well
  2. Place in 11 and 3/4 * 7 and 1/2-inch baking dish; top with crumbs mixture
  3. Bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes or until crumbs mixture is golden brown and fish flakes easily with fork
  4. Makes 4 servings

Variation 1: Substitute salmon steaks for halibut

Variation 2: Substitute dill weed for sage

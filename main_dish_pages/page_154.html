Crisco's French-Fried Butterfly Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. shelled, deveined shrimp
  • 1 cup sifted all-purpose enriched flour
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup ice water
  • 2 Tbs. Crisco Oil
  • 1 egg
  • Crisco oil for deep frying
  • Hot sauce

Prep Steps

  1. With a sharp knife slit shrimp down back without cutting all the way through and then wash shrimp and dry between paper towels
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar and salt then add ice water, 2 Tbs. Crisco Oil and egg; beat until smooth
  3. Dip shrimp, a few at a time, into the mixture and fry in deep Crisco Oil heated to 375F
  4. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes, until golden brown and drain on paper towels
  5. Serve hot with your favorite hot sauce
  6. Makes 6 servings

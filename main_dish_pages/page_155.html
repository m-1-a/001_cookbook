The Luxury Liner

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup zucchini, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup Holland House Sherry Cooking Wine
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tbs. tomato sauce or 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 chicken flavored bouillon cube
  • 1/2 lb. medium-size fresh or frozen shrimp, shelled and deveined
  • 1 Tbs. corn starch, dissolved in 2 Tbs. cold water
  • 1 Tbs. bread crumbs
  • 3 California avocados, halved, pitted, and peeled

Chicken breast cut into 1/4-inch cubes may be substituted for the shrimp

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan then add onion and zucchini and cook for 5 minutes over medium heat
  2. Stir in sherry, water, bouillon cube and tomato next cook for 3 minutes over high heat
  3. Add shrimp and cook, stirring about 1 minute until shrimp turn pink then add corn starch mixture and cook, stirring, until mixture comes to a boil
  4. Remove from heat and place avocado halves on baking dish
  5. Fill each half with shrimp mixture, spooning remainder of sauce around them
  6. Sprinkle with crumbs and broil for 1 to 2 minutes until crumbs brown
  7. Serve immediately, as an appetizer for 6 people or as a luncheon entree for 3 people

Classic Crab Newburg

Ingredients

  • 1 (6-oz.) package Wakefield Snow Crab meat
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp. cayenne
  • 2 cups half and half or light cream
  • 3 egg yolkes, slightly beaten
  • 1 and 1/2 Tbs. dry sherry
  • Hot, cooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. Thaw and drain Crab meat and melt butter in a medium saucepan
  2. Add flour, nutmeg and cayenne and stir until smooth
  3. Gradually add half and half and cook over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes or until slightly thickened, stirring constantly
  4. Gradually add 1/2 cup hot sauce mixture to egg yolkes, beating to blend
  5. Add egg yolk mixture to remaining sauce mixture; mix well then add crab and crab liquid
  6. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes or until thickened, stirring constantly then remove from heat and stir in sherry
  7. Serve over hot, cooked rice
  8. Serves 4 to 5

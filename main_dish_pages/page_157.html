Crab Louis

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 and 3/4 oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped onion
  • Generous dash pepper
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • 4 cups cooked flaked crab meat (about 1 and 1/2 lbs.))
  • Hard cooked egg, cut in wedges
  • Tomatoes, cut in wedges

Prep Steps

  1. Blend Soup, chili sauce, mayonnaise, onion and peppers; fold in whipped cream
  2. Add crab; chill
  3. Place crab on bed of lettuce
  4. Garnish with egg and tomato
  5. Makes about 4 cups

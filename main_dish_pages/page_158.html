Deviled Seafood

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 cup finely chopped celery
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 (6 to 7 oz.) can shrimp, drained
  • 1 (6 to 7 oz.) can crab meat, flaked
  • 2 cups Pepperidge Farm Herb-Stuffing, crushed
  • 1 cup mayonnaise

Prep Steps

  1. Stir together all ingredients until blended
  2. Spoon into a 1-quart shallow casserole or 8 oven-proof shells
  3. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes or until lightly brown
  4. Serves 6 to 8

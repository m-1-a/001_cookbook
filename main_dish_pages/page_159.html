Macaroni & Beans Italiano

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 medium zucchini, cut into 1/8-inch slices
  • 3 Tbs. margarine or olive oil
  • 3/4 cup Heinz Tomato Ketchup
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. oregano leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 (1-lb.) can Heinz Vegetarian Beans in Tomato Sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 cups cooked macaroni
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Saute first 3 ingredients in margarine until tender
  2. Stir in ketchup and next 5 ingredients
  3. Combine with beans and macaroni in a 1 and 1/2-quart casserole
  4. Bake in 375F oven for 35 to 40 minutes and stir occasionally
  5. Serve with Parmesan cheese
  6. Makes 4 to 5 servings (about 4 and 1/2 cups)

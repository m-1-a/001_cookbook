At-Ease Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. Borden Country Store Butter
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 1/2 cup Bordon Milk
  • 1 and 1/2 cup (6 ozs.) shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8-oz.) container Bordon Sour Cream
  • 1 cup uncooked macaroni, cooked and drained (2 cups cooked)

Prep Steps

  1. In medium saucepan, melt Butter; blend in flour, salt and pepper
  2. Gradually stir in Milk and cheese; cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and smooth
  3. Stir in Sour Cream and macaroni; heat through (do not boil)
  4. Garnish as desired and refrigerate leftovers
  5. Makes 4 to 6 servings

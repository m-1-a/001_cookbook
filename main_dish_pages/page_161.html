Spinach Fettucine

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. fresh spinach
  • 1 clove garlic, mashed
  • 2 Tbs. chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 8 ozs. (5 and 1/2 to 6 cups) Mueller's Medium Egg Noodles
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Remove and discard tough stems from spinach; tear or coarsely chop leaves
  2. In pan, cook garlic and onion in half the butter until golden and add spinach; cover and cook until just wilted
  3. Meanwhile, cook noodles as directed on package; drain and mix with remaining butter
  4. Then toss with cream, cheese and spinach and pass the pepper mill or shaker
  5. Makes 3 to 4 servings

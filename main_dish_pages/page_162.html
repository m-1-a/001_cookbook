Fettucine Romano

Ingredients

  • 8 ozs. fettucine or wide egg noodles
  • Boiling salted water
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 (6-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Cook fettucine in boiling salted water until tender; drain well
  2. Meanwhile saute onion in butter and thoroughly blend in Hunt's Tomato Paste and water; heat through
  3. Pour over hot cooked fettucine and toss with Romano cheese
  4. Fold in sour cream and sprinkle with parsley
  5. Makes 4 to 6 servings

Gourmet French Omelet

Ingredients

  • 2 (2 and 1/2-oz.) jars sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 3 Tbs. Parkay Margarine
  • 6 eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3/4 cup (3 ozs.) shredded Cracker Barrel Brand Sharp Natural Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 tsp. finely chopped chives

Prep Steps

  1. Saute mushrooms in 1 Tbs. Margarine and melt the remaining margarine in 10-inch skillet over low heat
  2. Combine eggs, milk and seasonings; pour into skillet then cook slowly
  3. As egg mixture sets, lift slightly with a spatula to allow uncooked portion to flow underneath
  4. Cover omlet with 1/2 cup Cheese, mushrooms and chives; fold in half and sprinkle with remaining Cheese
  5. Makes 3 to 4 servings

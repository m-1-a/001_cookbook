Wheat Germ Vegetarian Torte

Ingredients

  • 1 cup saltine cracker crumbs (about 26 crackers)
  • 3/4 cup Kretschmer Regular Wheat Germ, divided
  • 8 Tbs. butter or margarine, divided
  • 2 (8 ozs.) medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp. marjoram leaves, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. tarragon leaves, crushed
  • 1 cup grated Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 medium tomato, thinly sliced

Prep Steps

  1. Combine cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup wheat germ and 6 Tbs. melted butter in a small bowl and stir well
  2. Press evenly on bottom and about 1 inch up sides of 9-inch springform pan or on bottom and sides of 9-inch pie pan
  3. Bake at 400F for 8 to 10 minutes until very lightly browned then remove from oven
  4. Saute zucchini and onion in remaining 2 Tbs. butter until tender crisp then add seasonings to vegetable mixture and stir well
  5. Place half of the vegetables in crumb crust and sprinkle with about 3 Tbs. of the remaining wheat germ
  6. Top with half the cheese, remaining vegetables, then about 3 Tbs. wheat germ then beat eggs and milk together
  7. Pour into center of vegetable mixture and arrange tomato slices on top and sprinkle with remaining cheese and wheat germ
  8. Bake at 325F for 40 to 45 minutes until hot and bubbly
  9. Makes 6 servings

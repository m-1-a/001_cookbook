French Egg Nests

Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices French bread
  • 3 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Tabasco pepper sauce
  • Grated Cheddar cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Take French bread slices and hollow out the center of each and dot with butter
  2. Break egg into each hollow also salt lightly and add 3 drops of Tabasco pepper sauce to each egg
  3. Sprinkle with cheese then bake at 325F oven until eggs are set
  4. Serves 2

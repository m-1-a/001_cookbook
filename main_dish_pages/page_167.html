Fish & Chips

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fresh or frozen firm, white-fleshed fish fillets
  • 1 lb. potatoes, peeled, (about 3 potatoes)
  • Crisco for deep frying
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 Tbs. water
  • 1 Tbs. Crisco, melted
  • 1 egg white, stiffly beaten
  • 1/4 cup flour

Prep Steps

  1. Thaw fish, if frozen and cut into serving size pieces
  2. Cut potatoes in uniform strips, slightly larger than french fries
  3. To make chips: deep fry potato strips until golden brown in Crisco heated to 375F (about 7-8 minutes)
  4. Remove, drain on paper towels, and keep warm then in a medium bowl, combine first 1/4 cup flour and salt
  5. Make well in center; add egg yolk, water, and melted Crisco then stir until batter is smooth
  6. Fold in egg white then dip fish in the remaining 1/4 cup flour, then into batter
  7. Deep fry fish until golden brown in Crisco heated to 375 (about 1 and 1/2 minutes on each side)
  8. Sprinkle fish with vinegar, if desired and also sprinkle fish and chips with salt
  9. Makes 3-4 servings

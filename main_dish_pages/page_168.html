Fillet of Sole en Croute

Ingredients

  • 4 small fillets of sole
  • Salt, pepper, thyme, tarragon
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup julienne onions
  • 1/2 cup julienne mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup julienne carrots
  • 1 (17 and 1/4-oz.) package Pepperidge Farm Frozen "Bake It Fresh" Puff Pastry Sheets
  • 1 egg, beaten

Prep Steps

  1. Sprinkle sole fillets lightly with salt, pepper, thyme and tarragon, and refrigerate
  2. Melt butter in skillet; cut vegetables into narrow matchstick like strips (julienne) and add to skillet
  3. Season with salt and pepper and cook briefly, 3-5 minutes and vegetables should remain crisp
  4. Allow puff pastry sheets to thaw for 20 minutes, then unfold and cut each sheet into 4 squares
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll first square until it's slightly larger then the fillet and place fillet in center of pastry, spoon 1/4 cup of the julienne mixture on top
  6. Roll second square of pastry only enough to cover sole fillet and border of the first sheet and firmly press down edges to seal, trim excess with pastry wheel
  7. If desired, use scraps to decorate pastry and attach with beaten egg and repeat with remaining sole fillets and pastry
  8. Place on baking sheet, brush top with beaten egg and bake in preheated 375F oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown
  9. Makes 4 generous servings

Teriyaki Fish Fillets

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 Tbs. water
  • 1 lb. fish fillets
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced green onions and tops

Prep Steps

  1. Combine teriyaki sauce with lemon juice and water
  2. Place fish in single layer in shallow baking pan; pour teriyaki mixture over fish and marinate for 5 minutes on each side
  3. Bake fish in sauce in preheated 350F oven 10-15 minutes, or until fish flakes easily with fork
  4. Remove fish onto serving platter, sprinkle with green onions and spoon sauce over all
  5. Serve immediately
  6. Makes 4 servings

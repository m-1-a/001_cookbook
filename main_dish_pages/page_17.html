Ranchero Supper Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. oil
  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. beef cubes
  • 1 envelope Lipton Onion Soup Mix
  • 2 (16 ounces) cans whole tomatoes, un-drained
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tsps. chili powder
  • 2 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup celery, thinly sliced
  • 2 potatoes, diced

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet, heat oil and brown beef
  2. Add onion soup mix blended with tomatoes, water, and chili powder
  3. Simmer, stirring occasionally 30 minutes
  4. Add carrots, green pepper, celery, and potatoes
  5. Cook covered for 45 minutes or until vegetables are tender and gravy is slightly thickened
  6. Makes about 6 servings

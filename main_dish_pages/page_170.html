Batter Fried Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. uncooked shrimp, large
  • 3/4 cup unsifted flour
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Planters Peanut Oil

Prep Steps

  1. Peel shrimp leaving tails on; devein then split shrimp part way through, butterfly fashion
  2. Combine flour, water, egg, sugar and salt to make a batter
  3. Dip shrimp into batter and fry in deep, hot (375F) Planter Peanut Oil until golden brown
  4. Drain on paper towels
  5. Makes about 6 servings

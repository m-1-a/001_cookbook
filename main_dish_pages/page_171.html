Tuna Croquettes

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. Crisco
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped onion
  • 1 Tbs. snipped parsley
  • 2 tsps. lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • Dash paprika
  • 2 (6 and 1/2 or 7-oz.) cans tuna, drained and flaked
  • 2/3 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 (8-oz.) package frozen peas with cream sauce
  • Crisco for deep frying

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, melt the 3 Tbs. Crisco then blend in the flour
  2. Add milk then cook and stir till thickened and bubbly
  3. Add the onion, parsley, lemon juice, salt, pepper and paprika; stir in the tuna
  4. Cover and chill thoroughly, about 3 hours next with wet hands, shape tuna mixture into 8 cones, using about 1/4 cup for each
  5. Roll in crumbs and dip into a mixture of beaten eggs and 2 Tbs. water; roll in crumbs again
  6. Prepare peas with cream sauce according to package directions; keep hot meanwhile fry a few croquettes at a time till brown and hot in deep Crisco heated to 350F for about 3 minutes
  7. Drain on paper toweling and spoon pea sauce over croquettes
  8. Makes 4 servings

Tuna Creole in Rice Ring

Ingredients

  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced celery
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 Tbs. pure vegetable oil
  • 1 (6-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 1 and 1/4 cups water
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. dill weed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Dash Tabasco
  • 2 (6 and 1/2 to 7-oz.) cans chunk style tuna, drained and flaked
  • 4 cups hot cooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. Saute vegetables and garlic until tender in oil
  2. Add Hunt's Tomato Paste, water and seasonings
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Stir in tuna; heat through
  5. Pack hot cooked rice in buttered ring mold, turn out onto serving platter
  6. Spoon tuna creole into center
  7. Makes 4 servings

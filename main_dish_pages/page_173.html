Lake Placid Steak 'n' Pepper

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. tender steak, very thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 clove large garlic, mashed
  • 3 large onions, chopped
  • 2 small green peppers. chopped
  • 1/4 cup dry sherry
  • 1/4 cup sliced pimiento
  • 1 to 2 tsps. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 8 oz. Mueller's Thin Spaghetti
  • 2 Tbs. vegetable oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet brown meat in butter with garlic; remove meat to warm platter
  2. Add onion and green pepper to skillet and cook until crisp tender; add sherry, pimiento and seasonings; return meat to skillet
  3. Meanwhile cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain
  4. Toss spaghetti with oil, then combine with meat-vegetable mixture
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese
  6. Makes 4-6 servings

Chalet Casserole

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Mueller's Thin Spaghetti
  • 2 cups meatless spaghetti sauce
  • 4 oz. thinly sliced pepperoni
  • 1 (4-oz.) can mushrooms stems and pieces, undrained
  • 1 Tbs. grated onion
  • 2 slices (4 oz.) Mozzarella cheese, cut in half diagonally

Prep Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain
  2. In 11 * 8 * 2-inch baking dish combine spaghetti with sauce, pepperoni, mushrooms with liquid, and onion; top with cheese
  3. Bake at 350F for about 20 minutes or until bubbling
  4. Makes 4 servings

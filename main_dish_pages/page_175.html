Frank-ly Fabulous Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. frankfurters, cut in 1-inch slices
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 3/4 cup diced green pepper
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2 (8-oz.) cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cumin, if desired
  • 1/3 cup sliced stuffed olives
  • 8 oz. Mueller's Thin Spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Cook frankfurters, onion and green pepper in butter until vegetables are tender
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, chili powder and cumin then simmer, covered, 20 minutes; stir occasionally
  3. Add olives; heat, meanwhile cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain
  4. Serve frankfurter sauce over spaghetti; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
  5. Makes 4-6 servings

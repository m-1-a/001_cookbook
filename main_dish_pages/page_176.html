Unbelievable Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 4 to 5 cups spaghetti sauce
  • 8 oz. lasagne
  • 1 lb. Ricotta cheese
  • 8 oz. Mozzarella cheese, shredded or thinly sliced
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. In 13 * 9 * 2-inch baking pan, spread about 1 cup sauce; arrange a layer of uncooked lasagne; top with some sauce, Ricotta, Mozzarella, Parmesan and sauce
  2. Repeat, gently pressing lasagne pieces into cheese mixture below end with a final layer of lasagne; pour remaining sauce over, making sure all lasagne pieces are covered with sauce; top with remaining Mozzarella and Parmesan
  3. (Do not be concerned with the empty space at the ends of the pan - during cooking the lasagne will expand and take up most of the area)
  4. Bake at 350F for 45-55 minutes until lightly browned and bubbling then allow to stand for 15 minutes; cut into squares to serve
  5. Serves 6-8

Moon over Mostaccioli

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1 medium green pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/2 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped stuffed olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 (1-lb.) jar San Giorgio Spaghetti Sauce with Meat
  • 8 oz. (1/2 package) San Giorgio Mostaccioli Rigati (Pasta)
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in heavy saucepan and saute onion, celery, green pepper and garlic for 10 minutes
  2. Add olives, parsley and sauce; simmer 10 minutes more meanwhile prepare mostaccioli according to package directions
  3. Drain well and toss with sauce in heated bowl
  4. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese
  5. Serves 4

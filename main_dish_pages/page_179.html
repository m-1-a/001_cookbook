Fettucini Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb. bacon
  • 1 (12-oz.) box San Giorgio Fettucini
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 2 Tbs. snipped parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Saute bacon until crisp; drain well and crumble then cook Fettucini according to package directions
  2. Drain well and place in warm serving dish large enough for tossing
  3. Add crumbled bacon, butter, heavy cream, grated cheese, eggs and snipped parsley
  4. Toss until Fettucini is well coated
  5. Makes 6 servings

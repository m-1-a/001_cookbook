Horseradish Dressing for Cold Meats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise (or Best Foods)
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped green onion
  • 1 and 1/2 Tbs. horseradish
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Cover and chill for 1 hour
  3. Serve over slices of beef, tomato and onions
  4. Makes 1 and 2/3 cups

Salmon Quiche

Ingredients

  • 1 (10-inch) unbaked pie shell
  • 1 (15 and 1/2-oz.) can Bumble Bee Pink Salmon
  • 1 (9-oz.) package frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 and 1/2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 (3-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. thyme
  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup milk

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to and 375F bake pie shell for 10 minutes until partially set
  2. Drain salmon, mash bones, and cook spinach according to package directions then drain well
  3. Combine spinach, Monterey Jack cheese, cream cheese, salt and thyme then arrange salmon and mashed bones into pie shell
  4. Spoon spinach mixture on top then combine eggs and milk and pour over salmon and spinach
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 40-45 minutes
  6. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving
  7. Makes 6-8 servings

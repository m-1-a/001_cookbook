Mexican Quiche

Ingredients

  • 1 Pet-Ritz Regular Pie Crust Shell
  • 1 (4-oz.) can Old El Paso Whole Chilies
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and drained
  • 1 (4-oz.) cup Swiss cheese, shredded
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup light cream
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash ground nutmeg

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven and cookie sheet to 425F and drain chilies then dry with toweling
  2. Remove rib and seeds then chop in large pieces
  3. Crumble bacon and mix with chopped chilies then sprinkle Swiss cheese on bottom of unbaked pie shell, then chilies and bacon
  4. Mix together eggs, cream, salt and nutmeg then slowly pour over cheese mixture
  5. (The pie will be very full, but should not spill over)
  6. Place on cookie sheet in preheated oven and bake for 15 minutes
  7. Reduce temperature to 350F and bake an additional 20-25 minutes or until knife inserted in the center comes out clean
  8. Cool 10-15 minutes for before serving
  9. Serves 6-8

Savory Fish Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp. peppercorns
  • 1/4 tsp. tarragon leaves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. flounder fillets
  • 1/2 lb. yellow onions, sliced
  • Water
  • 1 Tbs. finely diced pimento
  • 3 Tbs. Blue Bonnet Margarine
  • 3 Tbs. flour
  • 2 tsps. dry mustard
  • 1 and 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 3 prepared mashed potatoes
  • Paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Tie peppercorns, tarragon and bay leaf in cheesecloth then place in a large saucepan with fillets and onions
  2. Cover with water and bring to a boil then reduce heat; simmer until onions are tender
  3. Drain; reserve 3/4 cup stock and arrange fish and onions in 2-quart oblong dish
  4. Scatter diced pimento over fish and melt Blue Bonnet Margarine in a small heavy saucepan then blend in flour, dry mustard, salt and pepper
  5. Cook over low heat, stirring, until smooth and bubbly then remove from heat and stir in milk and Worcestershire sauce
  6. Return to heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly cook for 1 minute longer next stir in parsley, spoon sauce over fish
  7. Top with mashed potatoes and sprinkle with paprika
  8. Bake in a hot oven (400F) for 30-35 minutes, or until potatoes are lightly browned and edges are bubbly
  9. Makes 6 servings

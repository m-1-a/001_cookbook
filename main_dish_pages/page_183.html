Breakfast Sausage Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Oscar Mayer Pork Sausage Links
  • 1 (11-oz.) package pie crust mix
  • 1 1 lb. (4 oz.) can apple pie filling
  • 1 cup (4 oz.) shredded processed American cheese
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, firmly packed

Prep Steps

  1. Cook pork sausage links; drain on paper towel
  2. Meanwhile use 1/2 package pie crust mix to prepare single pastry for 9-inch pie pan
  3. Line pan with pastry; flute edge and prick bottom and sides with fork and bake for 10 minutes in 375F oven
  4. Pour pie filling into partially baked shell; arrange cooked sausage, spoke fashion, on pie filling; sprinkle with shredded cheese
  5. For topping, combine brown sugar with remaining pie crust mix; sprinkle over pie and return to oven and bake for 25-35 minutes or until crust is golden brown
  6. Serve warm
  7. Makes 6 servings

Italian Zucchini Crescent Pie

Ingredients

  • 4 cups thinly sliced, unpeeled zucchini
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup margarine or butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley or 2 Tbs. parsley flakes
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. basil leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano leaves
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 8 oz. (2 cups) shredded natural Muenster or Mozzarella cheese
  • 8 oz. can Pillsbury Refrigerated Quick Crescent Dinner Rolls
  • 2 tsp. Dijon or prepared mustard

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375F then in 10-inch skillet cook zucchini and onion in margarine until tender, about 10 minutes
  2. Stir in parsley and seasonings then in large bowl, blend eggs and cheese
  3. Stir in vegetable mixture then separate dough into 8 triangles
  4. Place in un-greased 11-inch quiche, or 10-inch pie pan or 12 * 8-inch baking dish; press over bottom and up sides to form crust
  5. Spread crust with mustard and pour vegetable mixture evenly into crust
  6. Bake at 375F for 18-20 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean
  7. (If crust becomes too brown, cover with foil during last 10 minutes of baking)
  8. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving and cut into wedges
  9. To reheat: cover loosely with foil, heat at 375F for 12-15 minutes

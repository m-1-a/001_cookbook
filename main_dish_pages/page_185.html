Ham Corn Bread Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. Fleischmann's Margarine
  • 1 cup onion slices
  • 1 clove large garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/2 cup Planters Cocktail Peanuts, chopped
  • Generous dash pepper
  • 1 (10-oz.) package corn bread mix
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 lb. sliced boiled ham
  • 3 slices pasteurized processed American cheese, cut in half
  • 2 pimiento stuffed olives, sliced

Prep Steps

  1. Melt Fleischmann's Margarine in a large skillet then add onions, garlic and chili powder
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until onions are tender
  3. Remove from heat, stir in Planters Cocktail Peanuts and pepper
  4. Prepare corn bread mix according to package directions, using egg and milk
  5. Spread on bottom of 12-inch pizza pan then arrange ham on corn bread; tp with onion mixture
  6. Bake in hot oven (425F) for then top with cheese and bake 5 minutes longer, or until cheese is melted
  7. Garnish with olive slices
  8. Makes 6-8 servings

Vegetable Nut Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) pastry shell, unbaked
  • 2 Tbs. Blue Bonnet Margarine
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 4 cups coarsely chopped fresh spinach
  • 3/4 cup chopped Planters Pecan Pieces or Planters Pecan Chips
  • 1 and 1/4 cup grated Swiss cheese
  • 1 and 1/4 cup half and half
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. ground black pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Prebake pastry shell at 425F for 10 minutes and melt Blue Bonnet Margarine in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Add onion and red pepper; saute until nearly tender then stir in spinach and saute until wilted
  3. Sprinkle Planters Pecan Pieces or Planters Pecan Chips and cheese in bottom of pastry shell; spread spinach mixture over cheese layer
  4. Beat together half and half, eggs, salt and pepper; pour into pie shell and bake at 350F for 35 minutes, or until puffy and a knife inserted in center comes out clean
  5. Do not overbake
  6. Slice and serve hot
  7. Makes 1 pie

Marinated Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 and 1/2-lb.) flank, London broil or chuck steak
  • 1/2 cup each Regina Cooking Sherry and Japanese Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbs. cooking oil
  • 1 clove of large garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp. ground ginger

Prep Steps

  1. Score steak in criss cross pattern on both sides
  2. Combine remaining ingredients in shallow disk
  3. Marinate steak, covered, for several hours or overnight
  4. Turn occasionally
  5. Grill or broil until cooked as desired
  6. Serves 6

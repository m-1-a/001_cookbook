Mock Sauerbraten

Ingredients

  • 1 (1/2-ounce) package Ehlers Pot Roast Cooking and Gravy Mix
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/8 tsp. ground cloves
  • 1 (3 to 4 lbs.) pot roast
  • 4 carrots, quartered
  • 2 onions, quartered
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup gingersnap cookie crumbs

Prep Steps

  1. Combine gravy mix, water, wine, vinegar and cloves in a 4-quart Dutch oven and stir to blend well
  2. Add pot roast, cover, and cook in a preheated 350F oven for 2 hours
  3. Add carrots and onions, cover and cook for 1 hour longer
  4. Remove pot from oven and set meat aside on warm platter
  5. Stir sour cream and gingersnap crumbs into sauce
  6. Serve sauce separately
  7. Makes 6 servings
  8. Note: Leftover meat may be sliced and served cold with Horseradish Dressing

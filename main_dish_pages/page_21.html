Spanish Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4-lb. pot roast
  • 1 (8-ounce) bottle Kraft Catalina French Dressing
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 8 small onions
  • 8 small potatoes
  • 1 cup stuffed green olive slices
  • 2 Tbs. flour

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat in 1/4 cup dressing
  2. Add remaining dressing and 1/2 cup water
  3. Cover and simmer for 2 hours and 15 minutes
  4. Add onions, potatoes and olives; continue simmering for 45 minutes, until meat and vegetables are tender
  5. Remove meat and vegetables to warm serving platter
  6. Gradually add remaining 1/4 cup water to flour; stirring until well blended
  7. Gradually add flour mixture to hot liquid in pan; cook, stirring constantly, until mixture boils and thickens
  8. Simmer for 3 minutes, stirring constantly
  9. Serve with meat and vegetables
  10. Makes 6 to 8 servings

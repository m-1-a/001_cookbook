Stuffed Beef Ranchero

Ingredients

  • 2 (2 and 1/2-lb.) flank steaks
  • 3 tsp. instant meat tenderizer
  • 1 (6-ounce) package Uncle Ben's Stuff 'n Such Traditional Sage Flavor
  • 1 egg, beaten slightly
  • 1/2 lb. ground pork sausage
  • 1 medium green pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 (6-ounce) jar stuffed green olives, sliced
  • 1 (10 and 1/2-ounce) can condensed beef broth
  • 1 medium ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 2 Tbs cold water

Prep Steps

  1. Pound steaks thin enough to stuff and roll easily
  2. Sprinkle both sides of steaks with tenderizer and treat as manufacture suggest on package label
  3. Prepare Stuff 'n Such according to package directions, using 3/4 cup water
  4. Add egg to stuffing; mix well
  5. Cook sausage meat, pepper and onion in skillet until sausage is lightly browned and onion is tender for about 5 minutes
  6. Drain off excess fat
  7. Add olives and sausage mixture to stuffing and mix well
  8. Spread 1 and 1/2 cup stuffing on each steak
  9. Roll each steak and tie securely with string
  10. Place steaks in a 13 and 1/2 * 8 and 1/2-inch baking dish
  11. Pour broth mixed with tomato over steaks
  12. Bake at 350F oven for 1 hour or less until meat is tender yet rare, basting occasionally
  13. Place remaining stuffing in aluminum foil packet and bake for 30 minutes
  14. Cool meat rolls 5 minutes before slicing
  15. Combine flour and cold water; mix until free of lumps
  16. Stir into pan drippings; continue stirring over medium heat until thickened
  17. Serve with additional stuffing and gravy
  18. Makes 8 to 10 servings

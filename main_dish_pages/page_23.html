Reuben Croquettes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Uncle Ben's Converted Brand Rice
  • 1 (1-lb) can sauerkraut
  • 1 (12-ounce) can corned beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbs. water
  • 1 and 1/2 cups fine dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup prepared mustard
  • 4 tsps. lemon juice
  • Oil for frying

Prep Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package directions for half the basic recipe
  2. Drain sauerkraut very well, pressing out as much liquid as possible
  3. Chop sauerkraut and corned beef very fine
  4. Add onion, 2 eggs, cooked rice, cheese, salt and pepper; mix well
  5. Shape into 18 croquettes or balls, using 1/4 cup of mixture for each
  6. Combine remaining egg and water; beat slightly
  7. Roll each croquettes in crumbs, then egg mixture, and again in crumbs
  8. Let dry for 10 minutes
  9. Fry croquettes in hot shallow oil for 5 to 7 minutes, turning once or bake in 450F oven for 10 minutes longer
  10. Serve with sauce
  11. Makes 18 croquettes or 6 servings
  12. Sauce: Combine and mix mayonnaise, milk, mustard and lemon juice

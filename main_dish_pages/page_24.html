Cheesy Rice Roll Ups

Crepes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • Melted butter or margarine

Prep Steps

  1. Combine milk, eggs and salt in small mixing bowl; beat slightly
  2. Add flour; beat until smooth
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour
  4. Lightly butter a 6-inch frying pan and pour 2 Tbs. of crepe batter into pan; rotate pan quickly to spread evenly
  5. Cook until lightly browned
  6. Turn; brown slightly on second side and turn out
  7. Keep warm

Filling

Ingredients

  • 1 (6-ounce) package Uncle Ben's Long Grain and Wild Rice
  • 1 lb. ground lean beef
  • 1 (4-ounce) can sliced mushrooms, sliced

Prep Steps

  1. Cook contents of rice and seasoning packets according to package directions
  2. Cook ground beef until crumbly; drain
  3. Add cooked rice and mushrooms; mix well
  4. Fill each crepe with 1/3 cup rice mixture; roll up
  5. Place on large heatproof platter or 13 and 1/2 * 8 and 1/2 inch baking dish

Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup cup butter or margarine
  • 2 Tbs. all purpose flour
  • 1 (10 and 3/4-ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 and 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded process American cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter or margarine; stir in flour, then soup and milk
  2. Cook until thickened, stirring constantly
  3. Stir in remaining rice mixture
  4. Pour over filled crepes
  5. Sprinkle with cheese
  6. Bake at 350F oven until cheese is melted, for 15 to 20 minutes
  7. Makes 6 servings, 2 crepes per serving

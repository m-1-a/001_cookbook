Best Beef Bourguignon

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 slices bacon, cut up
  • 2 lbs. boneless beef, cut in 2-inch cubes
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 Tbs. tomato paste
  • 1 and 1/4 cups Holland House Red Cooking Wine
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. thyme
  • 1 small bay leaf
  • 1 peppercorn
  • 1/2 lb. small white onions

Prep Steps

  1. In large pot, saute mushrooms in butter; remove mushrooms and set aside
  2. Fry bacon until crisp; remove and set aside
  3. Add meat to drippings and brown well
  4. Blend in flour
  5. Add garlic, tomato paste, wine and seasonings
  6. Cover and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally
  7. Add onions, mushrooms and bacon; simmer 1 hour longer
  8. Add additional wine if liquid has evaporated
  9. Garnish with cherry tomatoes and serve over rice if desired

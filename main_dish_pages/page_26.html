Best Ever Meat Loaf

Ingredients

  • 1(10 and 3/4-ounce) can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom or Golden Mushroom Soup
  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/3 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. Mix thoroughly 1/2 cup soup, beef, breadcrumbs, egg, onion, and salt
  2. Shape firmly into loaf (8 * 4-inch); place in shallow baking pan
  3. Bake at 375F for 1 hour and 15 minutes
  4. In saucepan, blend remaining soup, water, and 2 to 3 Tbs. drippings
  5. Heat; stir occasionally
  6. Serve with loaf
  7. Makes 6 to 8 servings

Patty Shells

Thaw 1 (10 ounces) frozen patty shells. Prepare loaf as above. Bake at 375F for 30 minutes. Spoon off fat. Increase oven temperature to 400F. On floured board, roll 5 patty shells into rectangle (12 * 8-inch); prick several times wth fork. Cover top and sides of loaf with pastry. Decorate top with remaining patty shell, rolled and cut into fancy shapes. Bake 45 minutes more or until golden brown. Serve with sauce

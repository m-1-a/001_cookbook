Souperburgers

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 can Campbell's Chicken Gumbo Soup
  • 2 Tbs. catsup
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 6 buns, split and toasted

Prep Steps

  1. Brown beef and cook onion in skillet until tender (use shortening, if necessary); stir to separate meat
  2. Add soup and seasonings
  3. Heat; stir often
  4. Serve on buns
  5. Makes about 3 cups

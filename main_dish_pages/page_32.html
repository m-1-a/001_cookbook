Saucy Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 2 Tbs. shortening
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 1 (28-ozs.) can tomatoes, cut into bite size pieces
  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup Heinz 57 Sauce
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Hot buttered noddles

Prep Steps

  1. Combine first 5 ingredients
  2. Shape into 20 meatballs using a rounded Tbs. for each
  3. Brown well in shortening
  4. Drain excess fat
  5. Sprinkle flour over meatballs; stir gently to coat meatballs
  6. Combine tomatoes, Heinz 57 Sauce, sugar and salt; pour over meatballs
  7. Simmer uncovered for 25 minutes, or until sauce is desired constancy, stirring occasionally
  8. Serve meatballs and sauce over noodles
  9. Makes 5 servings (about 3 cups sauce)

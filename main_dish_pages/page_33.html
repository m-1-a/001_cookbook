Sweet - Sour Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 2 Tbs. shortening
  • 1 medium green pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 and 1/2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup Heinz 57 sauce
  • Hot buttered rice

Prep Steps

  1. Lightly combine first 4 ingredients; form into 16 meatballs
  2. Partially brown meatballs in shortening
  3. Add green pepper and onion; saute until vegetables are tender
  4. Stir in pineapple juice and Heinz 57 Sauce
  5. Cover and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until meatballs are cooked
  6. Thickened sauce with corn starch / water mixture, if desired
  7. Serve over rice
  8. Makes 4 servings

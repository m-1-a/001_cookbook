Cheeseburger Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbs. Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup small curd creamed cottage cheese
  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (about 4)

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375F
  2. Mix 1 cup Bisquick Baking Mix and the water until soft dough forms; beat vigorously 20 strokes
  3. Gently smooth dough into ball on floured cloth covered board
  4. Knead 5 times
  5. Roll dough 2 inches larger than inverted pie plate, 9 * 1 and 1/4 inches
  6. Ease into plates; flute edge if desired
  7. Cook and stir ground beef and onion until beef is brown; drain
  8. Stir is salt, pepper, 2 Tbs. Bisquick Baking Mix and the Worcestershire sauce
  9. Spoon into pie crust
  10. Mix eggs and cottage cheese; pour over beef mixture
  11. Arrange tomato slices in circle on top
  12. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese
  13. Bake until set, about 30 minutes
  14. Makes 6 to 8 servings

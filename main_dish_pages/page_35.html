Tangy Meat Loaf

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup catsup
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. powdered mustard
  • 4 tsps. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsps. seasoned salt
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped green pepper
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Wheat Chex cereal
  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. ground beef

Prep Steps

  1. In large bowl combine catsup, brown sugar and mustard
  2. Reserve 4 Tbs. mixture for topping
  3. To remaining mixture add Worcestershire sauce, salt, onion, garlic powder, pepper and egg
  4. Blend well
  5. Stir in green pepper and Wheat Chex
  6. Let stand for 5 minutes
  7. Break up Wheat Chex
  8. Add ground beef and mix well
  9. Shape into loaf in shallow baking pan
  10. Bake in 350F oven for 65 minutes
  11. Spread top with reserved catsup mixture
  12. Bake for an additional 15 minutes
  13. Makes about 6 servings

Skillet Barbecued Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 6 pork chops, 1/2-inch thick
  • 1 Tbs. salad oil
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato catsup
  • 4 tsps. brown sugar, packed
  • 4 tsps. corn starch

Prep Steps

  1. Brown chops slowly in hot oil on both sides; drain
  2. Combine 1/2 cup water with the next 3 ingredients; pour over chops
  3. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes; turn chops over and cook for 30 minutes longer
  4. Remove from pan
  5. Dissolve corn starch in remaining water; stir into pan
  6. Cook and stir until sauce boils and thickens
  7. Return chops and coat both sides with sauce
  8. Makes 6 servings

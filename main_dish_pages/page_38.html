Silverado Taco Casserole

Ingredients

  • 10 Ortega Taco Shells, coarsely broken
  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1 (1 and 1/4-oz.) package Ortega Taco Seasoning Mix
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tbs. dried minced onion
  • 1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 2 cups (1/2 lb.) grated Monterey Jck cheese
  • 1 large tomato, cut into wedges
  • 6 stuffed green olives, sliced
  • Ortega Taco Sauce

Prep Steps

  1. In lightly greased 1 and 1/2-quart casserole, place 1 half of taco chips set aside
  2. In 10-inch skillet, brown beef until crumbled; drain
  3. Add taco seasoning mix and water
  4. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes
  5. Stir in onion and tomato sauce
  6. Spoon meat mixture over taco shells
  7. Sprinkle with 1 and 1/2 cups cheese
  8. Place remaining taco chips over cheese
  9. Sprinkle with remaining cheese
  10. Arrange tomato wedges and olives attractively on to
  11. Bake in preheated 350F oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until hot and bubbly
  12. Serve with Ortega Taco Sauce
  13. Makes 6 servings

Stuffed Cabbage with Creamy Horseradish Sauce

Stuffed Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 firm medium hard cabbage
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 3 Tbs. A.1 Steak Sauce
  • 2 Tbs. horseradish
  • 2 Tbs. minced fresh onion
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbs. dry bread crumbs
  • Creamy Horseradish Sauce (below)

Prep Steps

  1. Remove damaged outer leaves of cabbage
  2. Slice off 1 inch of cabbage top
  3. Remove inner leaves
  4. Reserve top
  5. Scoop out center of cabbage leaving 3/4-inch cabbage shell
  6. Finely chop 1 cup of removed cabbage leaves
  7. Combine remaining ingredients and chopped cabbage
  8. Pack into cabbage cavity
  9. Replace top
  10. Tie stuffed cabbage in cheesecloth
  11. Place on rack, or nest of crinkled foil, in 4-quart saucepan
  12. Pour 2 cups water in bottom of pan and steam for 1 hour
  13. Remove cheesecloth carefully from cabbage
  14. Cut in wedges and serve with Creamy Horseradish Sauce
  15. Serves 6

Creamy Horseradish Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter (or margarine)
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 and 1/4 cups. milk
  • 2 Tbs. horseradish
  • 1 Tbs. A.1 Steak Sauce

Prep Steps

  1. In 2-quart saucepan, melt butter
  2. Stir in flour
  3. Cook, stirring constantly for 2 minutes
  4. Add salt, pepper, milk, horseradish and A.1
  5. Cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened
  6. Spoon over stuffed cabbage

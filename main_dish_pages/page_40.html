Mustard Glazed Ham

Ingredients

  • 1 precooked ham, about 8 lbs. (or 5-lb. canned ham)
  • Whole cloves
  • 1 and 1/2 cups firmly packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup maple flavored syrup
  • 2 Tbs. Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
  • 1 Tbs. corn starch
  • 1 (1 lb.), 4-oz. can unsweetened crushed pineapple

Prep Steps

  1. Cut rind off ham, if present
  2. Score top surface of ham, stud with cloves, and place fat side up on a rack in a shallow baking pan
  3. Bake in a preheated oven (350F) for approximately 15 minutes per pound, or following a wrappers directions
  4. Combine sugar, syrup and mustard
  5. Use 3/4 cup of glaze to baste ham every 10 minutes during last 30 to 40 minutes of baking
  6. Stir corn starch into remaining glaze in saucepan
  7. Add pineapple with juice
  8. Simmer, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens
  9. Serve sauce with ham

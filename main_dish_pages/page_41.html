Golden Praline Ham

Ingredients

  • 1 Hormel Cure/81 Ham
  • 1 (16-oz.) jar apricot-pineapple preserves
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • Pecan halves and Maraschino cherries for garnish

Prep Steps

  1. Combine apricot-pineapple preserves with brown sugar and heat in saucepan, stirring well
  2. Spread mixture over Hormel Cure/81 Ham for last half hout of baking
  3. When ham is finished, garnish with alternating diagonal rows of pecan halves and cherry halves

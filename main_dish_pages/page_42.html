Swiss Hamlets

Hamlets

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 to 8 oz.). package Oscar Mayer brand smoked cooked ham, chopped ham, honey loaf or luxury loaf
  • 4. slices Swiss cheese, 4-inches square
  • 1 (10-oz.). package frozen broccoli spears, thawed in Dill Mustard Sauce (below)

Prep Steps

  1. Top two slices of meat with slices of cheese and one-fourth of broccoli
  2. Roll and secure with wooden toothpicks
  3. Place in shallow baking dish
  4. Repeat and top with sauce
  5. Bake in 350F oven for about 30 minutes
  6. Remove toothpicks; and serve with Dill Mustard Sauce
  7. Makes 2 servings

Dill Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 (1-oz.) package white sauce mix
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1/4 tsp. dill weed

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare white sauce according to package directions
  2. Stir in rest of ingredients

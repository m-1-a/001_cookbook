Spaghettini Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb. mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup Each: chopped onion, celery, and green pepper
  • 2 Tbs. Wesson Oil
  • 3/4 lb. Italian sausage, casings removed
  • 2 (6-oz.) or 1 (12-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/4 cup dry red wine (optional)
  • 1 tp. sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. Italian herb seasonings
  • 1 lb. spaghettini, cooked and drained

Prep Steps

  1. Saute mushrooms, carrot, garlic, onion, celery and green pepper in hot oil in Dutch oven
  2. Add sausage; and cook until sausage loses redness; drain fat
  3. Add remaining ingredients except spaghettini
  4. Simmer uncovered for 30 to 40 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Serve over hot spaghettini
  6. Makes 6 to 8 servings

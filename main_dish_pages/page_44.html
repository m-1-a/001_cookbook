Bucks County Lamb Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. corn oil
  • 2 lbs. lamb stew meat, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 tsp. crushed thyme leaves
  • 4 and 1/2 cups water
  • 6 carrots
  • 12 small white onions
  • 1/4 cup Argo Kingsford's Corn Starch

Prep Steps

  1. In skillet heat corn oil over medium heat
  2. Add beef; and brown on all sides
  3. Add next 4 ingredients and 4 cups of the water
  4. Cover and bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 and 1/2 hours
  6. Add carrots and onion and simmer for 1/2 hour or until tender
  7. Mix Corn Starch and 1/2 cup water
  8. Stir into beef mixture
  9. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly for 1 minute
  10. Makes 6 servings

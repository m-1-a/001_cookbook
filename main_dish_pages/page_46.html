Hiram Walker's Supreme Brandy Burgers

Ingredients

  • 2 shallots (or scallions) finely chopped
  • 4 Tbs. butter
  • 1 lb. ground lean chuck
  • 1/4 lb. boiled ham, ground
  • 4 Tbs. ice water
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • Pinch of thyme
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Flour
  • 1 Tbs. oil
  • 1/4 cup beef consomme
  • 1/4 cup Hiram Walker Apricot Flavored Brandy
  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • Parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Saute the shallots and add the next 7 ingredients
  2. Wet hands and shape into patties
  3. Refrigerate for 1 hour
  4. Dust patties with flour; saute in oil and keep in warm oven
  5. Add consomme, Apricot Brandy, and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add butter and pour over patties
  7. Sprinkle with parsley

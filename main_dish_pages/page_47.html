Old El Paso Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 California avocados, peeled and barely mashed
  • 1/3 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/3 cup Old El Paso Choppped Green Chilies
  • 2/3 cup tomato, chopped
  • 2 Tbs. Old El Paso Taco Sauce
  • 1 Tbs. lemon or lime juice
  • 1 and 1/4 tsp. sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients
  2. In a medium bowl, carefully mix all ingredients
  3. Makes 2 and 2/3 cups
  4. Double the recipe so you'll have plenty of this zezty dip for tortilla chips and chilled fresh vegetables
  5. Will keep 2 to 3 days refrigerated

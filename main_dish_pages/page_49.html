Tamale Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups cold water
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Elam's Stone Ground 100% Whole Yellow Corn Meal
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 lb. ground beef chuck or round
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • 1 (1 lb.) can tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounces) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (8 and 3/4 ounces) can whole kernel corn, drained (1 cup)

Prep Steps

  1. Combine and mix cold water and corn meal
  2. Add 1/2 tsp. salt to boiling water and add corn meal mixture, stirring constantly and bring back to a boil
  3. Partially cover pan; and cook slowly for 7 minutes, stirring often
  4. Line bottom and sides of greased 2-quart cassorole with cooked mush
  5. Cook beef and onion in frypan until beef is brown and crumbly
  6. Stir in flour, remaining 1 tsp. salt and chili powder
  7. Add tomatoes, breaking them up into chunks with spoon
  8. Stir in tomato sauce and corn
  9. Spoon into mush-lined cassorole
  10. Bake in 350F oven until hot and bubbly, for 40 to 45 minutes
  11. Makes 6 servings

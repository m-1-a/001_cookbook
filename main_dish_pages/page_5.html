Easy Does It Swiss Steak

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. pure vegetable oil
  • 2 lbs. round steak, 1-inch thick
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 (8-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. Pour oil in 7 and 1/2 * 12 * 1 and 1/2-inch baking dish
  2. Place in oven at 400F
  3. While dish and oil and heat, trim steak and pound 2 Tbs. flour into each side
  4. Place in heated baking dish; turn to coat both sides
  5. Sprinkle on soup mix
  6. Pour Hunt's Tomato Sauce mixed with water over all
  7. Cover tightly
  8. Reduce oven temperature to 325F
  9. Bake for 2 and 1/2 to 3 hours until fork tender
  10. Skim excess fat from gravy
  11. Makes 6 servings

Frito Chili Pie Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Fritos Brand Corn Chips
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 (19 ounces) can chili
  • 1 cup grated American cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Place 2 cups of Fritos Brand Chips in a baking dish
  2. Arrange chopped onion and half of grated cheese on the corn chips
  3. Pour chili over onion and cheese
  4. Top with the remaining corn chips and grated cheese
  5. Bake at 350F for 15 to 20 minutes
  6. Makes 4 to 6 servings

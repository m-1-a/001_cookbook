Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 (16-ounce) can tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 Tbs. chili powder
  • 1 and 1/4 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 (8 ounce) package tortillas
  • Oil
  • 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded Kraft sharp Cheddar cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat; drain
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, water, onion and seasonings; simmering for 10 minutes
  3. Fry tortillas in hot oil until softened; drain
  4. Place rounded table spoonful of meat sauce and cheese on each tortilla; roll up tightly
  5. Place seam side down in 11 and 3/4 * 7 and 1/2-inch baking dish; top with remaining sauce and cheese
  6. Cover with aluminum foil; bake at 375F for 25 minutes
  7. Makes 6 to 8 servings
  8. To Make Ahead: Prepare recipe as directed. Cover; refrigerate overnight. Bake at 375F for 50 minutes

Hot Mexican Beef Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 (16-ounce) can kidney beans; drained
  • 1/2 cup Catalina French dressing
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 Tbs. chili powder
  • 1 qt. shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onion
  • 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded Kraft sharp Cheddar cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat; drain
  2. Add onion; cook until tender
  3. Stir in beans, dressing, water and chili powder; simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Combine lettuce and green onion
  5. Add meat mixture and 1 and 1/2 cups cheese; mix lightly
  6. Top with remaining cheese
  7. Makes 4 to 6 servings
  8. For variety, serve with toppings such as sour cream, sliced avocado, tortilla chipes and sliced ripe olives

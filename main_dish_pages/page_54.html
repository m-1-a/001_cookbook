San Giorgio Marzetti

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces San Giorgio Medium Egg Noodles
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 (16-ounce) San Giorgio Spaghetti Sauce with Mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup green pepper silvers
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup tomato juice
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed oregano
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar chese
  • San Giorgio Grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare medium egg noodles as directed on package
  2. Brown beef and onion in a heavy skillet until the pink disappears, breaking up the pieces as it cooks
  3. Pour off fat and sprinkle the meat with salt
  4. Add the remaining ingredients, except for cheeses
  5. Simmer covered for 10 minutes
  6. Add the hot drained noodles and blend
  7. Stir until hot; and remove from heat
  8. Blend in Cheddar cheese
  9. Serve in a hot casserole, garnished with Parmesan cheese
  10. Makes 8 (1 cup) servings
  11. Variation: If you desire, place half of the mixture in a freezer container, cover and freeze. Allow to defrost, sprinkle with buttered San Giorgio Seasoned Bread Crumbs and bake

Lasagne Italiano

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 3/4 tsp. oregano leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 8 ounces lasagne noodles, cooked, drained
  • 1 lb. ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 (6-ounces) packages Kraft Mozzarella cheese slices
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat; drain
  2. Add onion; cooking until tender
  3. Stir in tomato paste, water, and seasonings
  4. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes
  5. In 11 and * 7 and -inch baking dish, layer half of noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese and Mozzarella cheese; repeat layers
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
  7. Bake at 375F for 30 minutes
  8. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving
  9. Makes 6 to 8 servings

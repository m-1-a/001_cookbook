Grand Mott's Roast Pork

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 lbs. pork loin roast
  • 1/3 cup Mott's Apple Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Grandma's Molasses
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed

Prep Steps

  1. Have meat retailer loosen chine (back) bone
  2. Place roast fat side up on rack in open roasting pan
  3. Insert meat thermometer with bulb in thickest part not touching bone
  4. Roast in 325F oven for 2 to 2 and 1/2 hours or until meat thermometer registers 170F
  5. In saucepan, combine apple sauce, molasses, ginger and garlic over medium heat
  6. Brush pork with glaze every 10 minutes during last 1/2 hours of cooking
  7. Serve pork with remaning apple sauce
  8. Makes 8 to 10 servings

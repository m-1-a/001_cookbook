Avocado With Chili Con Carne

Ingredients

  • 3 medium onions, coarsely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 3 Tbs. bacon fat or butter
  • 2 lbs. round of beef, diced
  • 1 lb. lean pork, diced
  • 1/3 cup chili powder
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 1 (1 lb., 12 ounces) can pear shapped tomatoes
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 Tbs. Each: salt, oregano, brown sugar, wine vinegar
  • 2 cups pimiento stuffed olives
  • 4 avocados, halved and peeled

Prep Steps

  1. In large saucepan, cook onions and garlic in bacon fat until golden brown
  2. Remove and reserve onions; discard garlic
  3. Brown the meat in same pan over high heat and stir in onions, chili powder and flour
  4. Add tomatoes, bay leaves, salt, oregano, sugar and vinegar to a boil
  5. Bring to a boil; cover; and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally
  6. Discard bay leaves
  7. Stir in olives; cook 30 minutes longer
  8. Serve over avocado halves
  9. Makes 8 servings

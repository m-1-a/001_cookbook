Special Indoor Barbecued Spare Ribs With Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Wesson Oil
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. honey
  • 2 tsps. Tabasco sauce
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. Liquid Smoke
  • 1 clove of large garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. dry mustard
  • 4 to 5 lb. pork spare ribs

Prep Steps

  1. In small saucepan, saute onion in Wesson Oil until transparent
  2. Add remaining ingredients except spare ribs
  3. Mix well and simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Pour over spare ribs in large glass baking dish; and let stand for 1 hour, turning occasionally
  5. Arrange on shallow rack or broiler pan
  6. Bake at 350F for about 1 and 1/2 to 2 hours
  7. Turn ribs and baste with remaining barbecue sauce 2 to 3 times during baking
  8. Cut in portions to serve
  9. Makes 4 servings

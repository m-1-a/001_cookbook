Cantonese Apricot Pork

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 lb. lean, boneless pork
  • 2 Tbs. salad oil
  • 2 cups sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup sliced scallions
  • 2 cloves of large garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. powdered ginger
  • 1 (8-ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and sliced
  • 1 (30-ounce) can apricot halves, drained
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rice

Prep Steps

  1. Slice pork into thin strips
  2. Heat oil in wok; gradually add celery, scallions and garlic
  3. Stir fry over high heat (about 5 minutes) until tender but crisp; remove vegetables and set aside
  4. Add pork to seasoned oil and stir fry over high heat until lightly brown (about 10 minutes)
  5. Mix in soy sauce and ginger
  6. Reduce heat to medium; return vegetables to wok with water chestnuts; heat until hot, stirring constantly
  7. Gently mix in apricots and remove wok from heat immediatly after mixing in apricots to prevent them from getting to soft
  8. Seasoned to taste with salt and pepper
  9. Serve with rice
  10. Makes 6 servings

Lemony Ham Slice

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1 Oscar Mayer Ham Slice

Prep Steps

  1. Lemon Sauce: Grate peel of one lemon; cut lemon in half
  2. Thinly slice one half: squeeze juice from other half
  3. Combine peel, juice, brown sugar, mustard
  4. Add lemon slices
  5. Broil ham slices 4 inches from heat for 5 minutes; turn and top with sauce
  6. Broil for 3 minutes until sauce bubbles
  7. Makes 2 servings

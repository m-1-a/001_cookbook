Quick sweet and Sour Pork

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. corn oil
  • 1 lb. boneless pork, cut in 1-inch cubes
  • 1 (20 ounces) can pineapple chunks in juice
  • 1/2 cup Karo Light or Dark Corn Syrup
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. catsup
  • 2 Tbs. soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 small pepper, cut into 1-inch squares
  • 2 Tbs. corn starch
  • 2 Tbs. water
  • Hot cooked rice or Chinese noodles

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet heat corn oil over medium-high heat; add pork and brown on all sides
  2. Add pineapple, corn syrup, vinegar, catsup, soy sauce and garlic
  3. Bring to a boil and reduce heat
  4. Simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally, for about 10 minutes or until pork is tender
  5. Add green pepper
  6. Mix corn starch and water; stir into pork mixture
  7. Stirring constantly, bring to a boil over medium heat and boil for 1 minute
  8. Serve over rice or noodles
  9. Serves 4

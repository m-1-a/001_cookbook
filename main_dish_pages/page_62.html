Jambalaya

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. fresh pork
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 lb. cured ham
  • 6 small smoked pork sausages
  • 2 Tbs. lard or butter
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 sprig parsley
  • 1 sprig thyme or 1/4 tsp. dried leaf thyme
  • 1 whole clove
  • 1 and 1/2 quarts beef or chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp. Tabasco
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup uncooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. Cut pork (lean and fat) into cubes about 1/2-inch square
  2. Chop onion and galic very fine, then dice ham and cut sasage into pieces
  3. Melt lard in a heavy iron pot or Dutch oven
  4. Add onion, garlic, pork and ham; brown slowly, stirring frequently
  5. Add sausage, bay leaf, parsley, thyme and clove; cook for about 5 minutes
  6. Add broth, Tabasco, and salt; bring mixture to a boil
  7. Add washed rice and cover & simmer gently for half hour or longer until rice is tender
  8. Stir often to mix well
  9. Season to taste
  10. Makes 4 to 6 servings

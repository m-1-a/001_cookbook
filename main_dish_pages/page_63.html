Beans and Franks

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. chopped onion
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 (1 lb., 15 ounces) can Van Camp's Pork and Beans
  • 6 wieners, sliced penny style
  • 1/3 cup brown surgar
  • 1 tsp. prepared mustard
  • 1 tsp. celery salt

Prep Steps

  1. Saute onion in butter until tender
  2. Combine onion with remaining ingredients in a 2-quart casserole
  3. Bake uncoverd at 350F for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Makes 6 servings

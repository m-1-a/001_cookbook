Western Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. beef brisket or boneless round or chunck roast
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup catsup
  • 1/4 cup Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 2 Tbs. cider vinegar
  • 2 tsps. salt

Prep Steps

  1. In heavy pot brown on all sides beef brisket or boneless round or chunck roast in oil
  2. Add chopped onion; and cook until golden
  3. Combine catsup, Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce, brown sugar, cider vinegar and salt; pour over meat
  4. Simmer covered until meat is tender, about 3 hours
  5. Serve sliced with sauce along with onions, carrots, and potato chunks if desired
  6. Serves 8

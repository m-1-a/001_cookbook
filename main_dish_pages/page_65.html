Marinated Steak Italiano

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 to 3-lb. London broil or chuck steak
  • 1/2 cup Wish-bone Italian Dressing

Prep Steps

  1. In large baking dish, place meat and cover with Wish-bone Italian Dressing
  2. Marinate, turning occasionally, for about 3 hours
  3. Broil steak, preferably over charcoal, turning once
  4. Makes about 6 servings
  5. Variation: Add 1/4 cup soy sauce and 1/4 cup brown sugar to Wish-bone Italian Dressing before marinating

Onion Chuck Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 lb. boneless chuck steak
  • 2 Lipton Onion Cup-a-Soup
  • 1 (2 and 1/2 oz.) jar sliced mushrooms, drained

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Place meat on foil in shallow baking pan
  3. Sprinkle both sides with Lipton Onion Cup-a-Soup; top with mushrooms
  4. Wrap loosely, sealing air tight with double fold
  5. Bake for 1 hour
  6. Makes 4 servings

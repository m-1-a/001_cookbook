Kikkoman Chuck Steak Teriyaki

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 lb. chuck steak (about 1 and 1/2 inches thick)
  • Meat tenderizer
  • 2/3 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1 (6-oz.) can tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup salad oil

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare meat with a meat tenderizer according to label directions
  2. Combine remaining 3 ingredients; brush over meat
  3. Place on grill 3-4 inches from hot coals
  4. Cook for about 15 minutes
  5. Turn steak over and brush with additional sauce
  6. Cook to desired degree of doneness
  7. Heat remaining sauce and serve with steak
  8. Makes 4 servings

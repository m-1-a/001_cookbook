New England Boiled Dinner

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. boneless beef round rump roast
  • 2 tsps. shortening
  • 2 (10 and 3/4-oz.) cans Campbell's Onion Soup
  • 1 tsp. prepared horseradish
  • 1 medium bay leaf
  • 1 clove medium garlic, minced
  • 6 medium carrots (about 1 lb.) peeled & cut in 2-inch pieces
  • 1 lb. rutabagas, peeled & sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • 8 whole potatoes (about 1 lb.), peeled
  • 1 medium head cabbage (about 2 lbs.) cut in 6 wedges
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup flour

Prep Steps

  1. In large heavy pan, brown meat in shortening; pour off fat
  2. Add soup, horseradish, bay leaf, and garlic
  3. Cover and cook over low heat for 2 hours
  4. Add carrots and rutabagas and cook for 30 minutes, and stir occasionally
  5. Add potatoes; and place cabbage on top
  6. Cook for 30 minutes more or until done
  7. Place meat and vegetables on serving platter; and keep
  8. Meanwhile, blend water into flour until smooth; stir into sauce
  9. Cook, stirring until thickened
  10. Makes 6 servings

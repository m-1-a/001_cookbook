Grits and Grillades

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. beef round, thinly sliced
  • 6 Tbs. bacon drippings or salad oil
  • 4 Tbs. all purpose flour
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 cup peeled and cubed tomatoes
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1 tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. thyme
  • 3 cups hot cooked grits

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat in oil in deep skillet
  2. Remove meat
  3. Add flour and brown, stirring constantly
  4. Add onion and tomatoes
  5. Simmer a few minutes
  6. Add meat, water, pepper, garlic, bay leaf, parsley, salt, Tabasco pepper sauce and thyme
  7. Cover and simmer for 1 and 1/2 hours or until meat is very tender, adding water if necessary to make sauce the consistency of thick gravy
  8. Serve grillades with portion of hot grits on the side
  9. Serves 4 to 6
  10. Note: If you live in a part of the country where grits are not available, you may serve grillades with hot cooked rice

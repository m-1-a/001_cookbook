Salisbury Steak

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup tomato juice
  • 1 Tbs. French's Minced Onions
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 envelope French's Mushroom Gravy Mix
  • 1 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. Combine egg, bread crumbs, tomato paste and onions; add ground beef
  2. Shape into oblong patties, brown in skillet
  3. Add gravy mix and water, stirring until smooth
  4. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Makes 4 servings

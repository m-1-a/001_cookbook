Dutch Meat Loaf

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
  • 1 cup fresh bread crumbs
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 (8-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1 Tbs. vinegar

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl, lightly mix beef, bread crumbs, onion, 1/2 can Hunt's Tomato Sauce, egg, salt and pepper
  2. Shape into loaf in a shallow baking pan
  3. Combine remaining Hunt's Sauce with the rest of the ingredients; pour over loaf
  4. Bake at 350F for 1 and 1/4 hours
  5. Baste loaf several times
  6. Makes 5-6 servings

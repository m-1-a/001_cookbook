Rodeo Hash

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 3 cups diced raw potatoes
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 2 T shortening
  • 1 can Campbell's Golden Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chili sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Generous dash pepper

Prep Steps

  1. In skillet, brown beef in shortening with potatoes and celery; stir to seperate meat
  2. Pour off fat
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Cover; and simmer for 10 minutes, stir often
  5. Makes 4 and 1/2 cups

Chili Beef and Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove large garlic, minced
  • 2 tsps. chili powder
  • 2 (16-oz.) cans Campbell's Pork and Beans in Tomato Sauce
  • 1 (10-oz.) can tomatoes and green chilies

Prep Steps

  1. In large saucepan, brown beef and cook onion until tender (use shortening if necessary); stir to separate meat
  2. Combine with garlic and chili powder
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Bring to a boil; reduce heat; and simmer for 10 minutes, stir occasionally
  5. Makes 6 and 1/2 cups, and 6 servings

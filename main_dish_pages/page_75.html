Veal Goulash

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. veal cubes
  • 1 Tbs. paprika
  • 1/4 cup Planters Peanut Oil
  • 1 and 1/4 cups chopped onions
  • 2 medium tomatoes, peeled and wedged
  • 1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • Hot cooked noodles

Prep Steps

  1. Dredge veal with paprika and set aside
  2. Heat Planters Peanut Oil in Dutch oven
  3. Add chopped onions and saute until lightly browned; remove onions using slotted spoon and set aside
  4. Brown dredged veal on all sides
  5. Return onions to Dutch Oven
  6. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce and salt; simmer gently, uncovered for about 1 hour, or until veal is tender
  7. Serve over noodles
  8. Makes about 6 servings

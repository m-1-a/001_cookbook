Meatballs with Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 (6-oz.) or 1 (12-oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbs. minced parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 cup soft bread crumbs
  • 3 Tbs. Wesson Oil
  • 2 and 1/2 cups hot water
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. sugar
  • 1 tsp. basil
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 lb. spaghetti, cooked and drained

Prep Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together eggs, 1 Tbs. Hunt's Tomato Paste, onion, cheese, parsley, garlic and 1 tsp. salt
  2. Add beef and bread crumbs; mix thoroughly
  3. Form into 20 balls, about 1 inch in diameter
  4. Lightly brown in a Dutch oven on all sides in hot oil; drain
  5. Blend remaining Hunt's Tomato Paste with hot water, sugar, basil, oregano, 1 tsp. salt and pepper in a bowl
  6. Pour over meatballs and cover; simmering for 10 minutes and then simmer uncovered for 10 minutes longer
  7. Serve over spaghetti
  8. Makes 6 servins

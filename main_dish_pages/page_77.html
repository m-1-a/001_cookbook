Golden Glaze Ham

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 lb.) Armour Golden Star Ham
  • 1 (10-oz.) jar apple jelly
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • Dash of nutmeg

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 325F
  2. Score surface of ham
  3. Heat ham according to label instructions
  4. Combine remaining ingredients in small saucepan
  5. Heat, stirring occasionally, until jelly melts
  6. Spoon glaze over ham during last 30 minutes of heating time

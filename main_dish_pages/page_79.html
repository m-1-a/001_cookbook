Pork Chops 'n' Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. oil
  • 6 pork loin chops, 1/2-inch thick
  • 1 green pepper, cut into strips
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1 (10 and 1/2-oz.) can condensed onion soup
  • 2 Tbs. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 medium tomatoes, cut in wedges

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet heat oil
  2. Add chops, 3 at a time; brown well on both sides; remove and set aside
  3. Add green pepper; saute for 3 minutes; remove and set aside
  4. Stir in flour; cook and stir for 1 minute
  5. Blend in next 4 ingredients and bring to a boiling point
  6. Return chops to skillet; spoon sauce over chops
  7. Cover and simmer until chops are tender, about 1 hour
  8. Stir in tomatoes and sauteed green pepper
  9. Cover and simmer 5 minutes longer
  10. Serves 6

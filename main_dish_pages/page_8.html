Hungarian Goulash

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 lbs. round steak, cut in 1/2-inch cubes
  • 3 Tbs. oil
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbs. paprika
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (1-lb.) can whole tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2/3 cup Pet Evaporated Milk

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat in hot oil in large saucepan
  2. Mix together flour, paprika, salt, pepper and garlic powder
  3. Stir into meat mixture
  4. Add onion, tomatoes and water
  5. Cover, simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally
  6. Gradually stir Pet Evaporated Milk into hot mixture
  7. Simmer 15 minutes stirring frequently
  8. Serve over hot buttered noodles
  9. Makes 6 and 1/2-cup servings

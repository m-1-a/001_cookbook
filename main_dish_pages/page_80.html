Sweet and Sour Pork Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. boneless pork, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 1 (13 and 1/2-oz.) can pineapple tidbits
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic salt
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 cup Uncle Ben's Converted Brand Rice
  • 1 green pepper, cut into small squares
  • 1 tomato, cut into thin wedges

Prep Steps

  1. Brown pork in cooking oil in 10-inch skillet; drain
  2. Drain pineapple tidbits, reserving liquid and add water to liquid to make 2 and 1/2 cups
  3. Add liquid, vinegar, salt, garlic salt and sugar to pork; stir and bring to boil
  4. Reduce heat; cover and cook over low heat for about 20 minutes
  5. Remove cover, stir in rice and cover and continue cooking for about 25 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and pork is tender
  6. Stir in pineapple tidbits, green pepper and tomato wedges
  7. Heat through
  8. Makes 4-6 servings
  9. Variation: : Nice to add sliced water chestnuts or bamboo shoots at the end of cooking, or substitute cubed ham or cubed uncooked chicken for pork

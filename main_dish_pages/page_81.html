Braised Ginger Pork

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. lean 1-inch pork cubes
  • Flour
  • 3 Tbs. Planter Peanut Oil
  • 1/3 cup chicken broth
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 Tbs. cherry
  • 1/4 cup chopped green or yellow onion
  • 1 clove small garlic, crushed or minced
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 tsp. ground ginger
  • Dash pepper
  • Hot cooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. Dredge meat in flour and heat Planters Peanut Oil in Dutch oven or large skillet
  2. Add half of meat and brown quickly; remove meat and set aside
  3. Brown remaining meat; remove and set aside
  4. Pour off excess oil from pan and combine chicken broth, soy sauce and sherry
  5. Add onion, garlic, sugar, ginger and pepper
  6. Place in cooking pan along with meat and simmer covered for 15, or until meat is tender
  7. Serve over rice
  8. Makes about 6 servings

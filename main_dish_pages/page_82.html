Speedy Baked Beans with Wieners

Ingredients

  • 1 (8-oz.) package Oscar Mayer Bacon
  • 3 (1-lb.) can baked beans
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. molasses
  • 2 tsps. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1 (1-lb.) package Oscar Mayer Wieners cut up

Prep Steps

  1. Cut bacon into 1-inch pieces; cook in skillet until crisp; and add remaining ingredients
  2. Simmer, covered for 15 minutes
  3. Makes 6 servings

Country Fried Chicken

Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 and 1/2 lbs.) to 3-lb. frying chicken, cut-up or use chicken pieces
  • Crisco Shortening
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tsps. garlic salt
  • 2 tsps. MSG
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. poultry seasoning
  • 1 tsp. black pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Blend milk and egg and combine flour and seasonings in plastic or paper bag
  2. Shake chicken in seasoned flour then dip chicken pieces in milk/egg mixture
  3. Shake chicken a second time in seasoning mixture to coat thoroughly and evenly

Pan Fried Chicken

Prep Steps

  1. Melt Crisco in skillet to about 1/2 to 1-inch deep heat at 365F
  2. Brown chicken on all sides, reduce heat to 275F, continue cooking until chicken is tender about 30-40 minutes, and do not cover
  3. Turn chicken several times during cooking; drain on paper towels

Deep Fried Chicken

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare as above and cook in deep Crisco heated to 365F for 15-18 minutes
  2. Drain on paper towels
  3. Makes about 4 servings

Variation: For extra spicy chicken. Increase poultry seasoning to 1/2 tsp. and black pepper to 2 tsps.

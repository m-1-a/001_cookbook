Oven Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs. margarine or butter
  • 2/3 cup Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. paprika
  • 1 and 1/4 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 and 1/2 to 3 and 1/2-lb. broiler-fryer chicken, cut up

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425F
  2. Heat margarine in rectangular pan (13 * 9 * 2 inches) until melted
  3. Mix baking mix, paprika, salt and pepper; coat chicken
  4. Place chicken, skin side down in a pan and bake uncovered 35 minutes
  5. Turn; bake until done, about 15 minutes longer
  6. Makes 6 servings

Variations:

Italian Coating:

Prep Steps

  1. Add 1 Tbs. Italian seasoning and 1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Mexican Coating:

Prep Steps

  1. Decrease baking mix to 1/2 cup
  2. Add 2 Tbs. cornmeal and 1 to 2 Tbs. chili powder

Parmesan Coating:

Prep Steps

  1. Decrease baking mix to 1/3 cup and salt to 1 tsp.
  2. Add 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Sesame-Herb Coating:

Prep Steps

  1. Decreased salt to 1 tsp.
  2. Add 2 Tbs. sesame seed and 2 tsps. each dried thyme leaves, ground sage and parsley flakes

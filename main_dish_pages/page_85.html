Zesty Orange Barbecued Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 (6-oz.) can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
  • 1/4 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Sauce
  • 1 Tbs. instant minced onion
  • 1 tsp. parsley flakes
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 8-10 pieces of frying chicken

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, except chicken; let stand for 10 minutes
  2. Place chicken and sauce in large plastic bag; press air out and close securely
  3. Refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight; turning over occasionally
  4. Remove chicken; reserve marinade and place on grill 6-8 inches from hot coals
  5. Cook for about 20 minutes on each side; baste with reserved marinade during last 15 minutes of cooking time
  6. Makes 6-8 servings

Sunshine Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup French's Prepared Mustard
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 8 slices lean bacon
  • 2 whole chicken breasts, split, boned, skinned and slice in halfway crosswise, making 8 pieces in all

Prep Steps

  1. In small mixing bowl, combine mustard, pineapple juice, and brown sugar, mixing until sugar is dissolved
  2. Wrap each chicken piece with bacon, fastening with wooden picks
  3. Brush mustard sauce over chicken and bacon
  4. Grill over hot coals about 10 minutes, turning frequently and brush with the additional mustard sauce
  5. Remove wooden picks
  6. To serve, spoon remaining sauce over chicken
  7. Makes 4 servings

Pizza Chicken Italiano

Ingredients

  • 2 whole chicken breast (split, boned, skinned)
  • 1 (15-oz.) can Del Monte Tomato Sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp. oregano
  • 1 tsp. chopped parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. each garlic salt, onion powder and sugar
  • 1 (2 and 1/4-oz.) can sliced ripe olives, drained
  • 4 sliced Mozzarella cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Place chicken in 2-quart baking dish
  2. Combine tomato sauce, oregano, chopped parsley, garlic salt, onion powder and sugar
  3. Pour over chicken, cover, and bake at 425F for 15 minutes
  4. Top with olives and Mozzarella cheese and continue to bake, uncovered for 10 minutes
  5. Designed for oven to table service
  6. Serves 4

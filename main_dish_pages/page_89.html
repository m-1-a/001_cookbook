Wild Rice Chicken Supreme

Ingredients

  • 1 (6-oz.) package Uncle Ben's Long Grain and Wild Rice
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Dash black pepper
  • 1 cup half & half
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1/3 cup chopped pimiento
  • 1/3 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare contents of rice and seasoning packets according to package directions
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in large saucepan
  3. Add onion and cook over low heat until tender
  4. Stir in flour, salt and pepper and gradually stir in half & half and chicken broth
  5. Cook stirring constantly until thickened and stir in chicken, pimiento, parsley, almonds and cooked rice
  6. Place in 2-quart casserole and bake uncovered in a 425F oven for 30 minutes
  7. Makes 6-8 servings

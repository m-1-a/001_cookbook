Hungarian Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 to 4-lb.) lean chuck or rump roast
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp. paprika
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbs. pure vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 to 10 small whole white onions
  • 8 small carrots, pared
  • 2 (8-oz.) cans Hunt's Tomato Sauce with Mushrooms
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. onion salt
  • 2 Tbs. minced parsley
  • 1 cup sour cream (optional)

Prep Steps

  1. Trim excess fat from meat
  2. Sprinkle with paprika, salt and pepper
  3. Brown in oil in Dutch oven over medium heat
  4. Add water and bay leaf; simmer, covered for 2 hours or until meat is almost tender
  5. Skim off fat
  6. Place onions and carrots around meat
  7. Add Hunt's Sauce, garlic and onion salt
  8. Cover; simmer 50 to 60 minutes for longer until meat and vegetables are tender
  9. Add parsley just before serving
  10. Remove from heat and gradually stir in sour cream, if desired
  11. Makes 6 to 8 servings

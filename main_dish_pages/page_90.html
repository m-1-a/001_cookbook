Savory Crescent Chicken Squares

Ingredients

  • 1 (3-oz.) package cream cheese, softened
  • 3 Tbs. margarine or butter, melted
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken or 2 (5-oz.) cans boned chicken
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbs. milk
  • 1 Tbs. chopped chives or onion
  • 1 Tbs. chopped pimiento, if desired
  • 1 (8-oz.) can Pillsbury Refrigerated Quick Crescent Dinner Rolls
  • 3/4 cup seasoned croutons, crushed

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 F and in a medium bowl, blend cream cheese and 2 Tbs. of the margarine until smooth
  2. Add the next 6 ingredients; mix well and separate dough into 4 rectangles; firmly press perforations around edges together to seal
  3. Spoon 1/2 cup meat mixture onto center of each rectangle and pull 4 corners of dough to top center of chicken mixture, twist slightly and seal edges
  4. Place on un-greased cookie sheet and brush tops with reserved 1 Tbs. margarine; sprinkle with croutons crumbs
  5. Bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown
  6. Serves 4

Country Cornish Hens

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. bulk pork sausage
  • 2 cups bread cubes, toasted
  • 1 cup chopped, peeled apples
  • 1/2 cup celery slices
  • 1/3 cup raisins
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 3/4 cup Parkay Margarine, melted
  • 1/3 cup sherry
  • 1/4 tsp. sage
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • 6 (1 to 1 and 1/2-lb.) Rock Cornish hens

Prep Steps

  1. Brown meat; drain
  2. Add bread, apples, celery, raisins, onion, 1/4 cup margarine, 2Tbs. sherry and seasonings; mix well
  3. Lightly stuff hens with dressing mixture; close openings with skewers
  4. Bake at 400F for 1 hour or until tender, basting occasionally with combined remaining margarine and sherry
  5. Garnish with celery leaves, if desired
  6. Makes 6 servings

Stuffed Turkey

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 1 cup celery, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 (8 and 1/2-oz.) package herb-seasoned stuffing mix
  • 2 cups coarse cornbread crumbs
  • 1 (10 and 1/2-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Chicken Broth
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 10-pound turkey
  • 1 (10 and 3/4-oz.) can Campbell's Condensed Soup
  • 1/2 cup whole berry cranberry sauce
  • 1/4 cup orange juice

Prep Steps

  1. In skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove and crumble
  2. Pour off all but 2 Tbs. drippings and cook celery & onions in drippings until tender
  3. Toss lightly wit stuffing mix, cornbread, broth, and egg and fill cavity of turkey loosely with stuffing
  4. Truss; place in roasting pan and cover wit foil
  5. Roast at 325F for about 4 hours (25 minutes per pound or until tender) then uncover at the last hour to brown
  6. Remove turkey to serving platter and skim fat from drippings
  7. Add remaining ingredients
  8. Heat; stir to loosen browned bits
  9. Makes 12 servings

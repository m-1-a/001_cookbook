Beer Batter Fish or Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fish fillets or cooked large shrimp (or 1/2 lb. of each)
  • 3 to 4 Tbs. Bisquick Baking Mx
  • 1 cup Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup beer

Prep Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil 1 and 1/2 inches in heavy saucepan or deep fry to 350F
  2. Cut fish in serving-size pieces and lightly coat fish or shrimp with 3 to 4 Tbs. baking mix
  3. Mix 1 cup baking mix, the salt, egg and beer until smooth
  4. Dip fish or shrimp into batter; letting excess drip into bowl
  5. Fry until golden brown, about 2 minutes on each side; drain
  6. Makes 4 servings

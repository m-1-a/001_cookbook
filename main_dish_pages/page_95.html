Quiche Lorraine with Bacon

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups milk
  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 2 (8 ounces) can shredded Swiss cheese
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 lb. Armour Star Bacon, crisply cooked, crumbled
  • 1 (9-inch) unbaked pastry shell

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F
  2. Combine milk, eggs and seasonings; mix well
  3. Toss cheese with flour; add cheese mixture and bacon to egg mixture
  4. Pour into pastry shell
  5. Bake at 350F for 40 to 45 minutes
  6. Makes 6 servings

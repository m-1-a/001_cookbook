Kellogg's Crunchy Baked Chicken Variations

Prep Steps

  1. Wash 3 lbs. frying chicken pieces and pat dry
  2. For any of the following variations, dip chicken in liquid mixture and coat evenly with crumb mixture
  3. Place in single layer, skin side up, in well greased or foil lined shallowing baking pan
  4. Drizzle with 3 Tbs. melted margarine, or butter if desired
  5. Bake in oven heated to 350F for about 1 hour or until chicken is tender
  6. Do not cover pan or turn chicken while baking
  7. Makes 6 servings

Variations:

Corn-Crisped Chicken:

Prep Steps

  1. Dip in 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  2. Coat with mixture of 1 cup. Corn Flake Crumbs, 1 tsp. salt and 1/8 tsp. pepper

California Crusty Chicken:

Prep Steps

  1. Dip in mixture of 1/4 cup melted margarine or butter, 3 Tbs. lemon juice and 1 tsp. grated lemon peel
  2. Coat with mixture of 1 and 1/4 cups Corn Flake Crumbs, 1 and 1/2 tsp. salt snd 1/4 tsp. pepper

Baked Chicken Italiano:

Prep Steps

  1. Dip in 1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing
  2. If desired, marinate chicken in dressing for at least 1 hour
  3. Coat with 1 and 1/4 cups Corn Flake Crumbs

Oven-Fried Chicken:

Prep Steps

  1. Dip in mixture of 1 slightly beaten egg and 2 Tbs. milk
  2. Coat with mixture of 1 and 1/4 cups Corn Flake Crumbs, 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper

Parmesan Crisped Chicken:

Prep Steps

  1. Dip in mixture of 1 slightly beaten egg and 2 Tbs. milk
  2. Coat with mixture of 3/4 cup Corn FLake Crumbs, 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt, 1/4 tsp. pepper and 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Zesty Crisped Chicken:

Prep Steps

  1. Dip in mixture of 1 slightly beaten egg and 1/4 cup soy sauce
  2. Coat with 1 and 1/4 cups Corn Flakes Crumbs

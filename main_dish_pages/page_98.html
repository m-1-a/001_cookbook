Chicken Oriental

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs. chicken pieces

Marinade Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mott's Apple Sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. ground pepper
  • 3 drops Tabasco

Prep Steps

  1. Combine marinade ingredients and pour over meat
  2. Refrigerate for 8 to 12 hours and drain meat and place on rack in baking pan
  3. Bake covered for 1 hour at 350F then uncover and baste with marinade; bake for 15 minutes
  4. Turn pieces, baste again, and bake for 15 minutes more
  5. (Final "baking-and-basting" may be done on charcoal grill)
  6. Makes 4 to 6 servings

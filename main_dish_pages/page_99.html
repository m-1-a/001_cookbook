Chicken A L'Orange

Ingredients

  • 2 broiler-size (2 to 2 and 1/2-lb.) chickens, quartered
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika
  • 1/4 tsp. dried tarragon
  • 1/2 cup minced, green onion
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped celery
  • 4 more Tbs. butter
  • 1/4 cup Cointreau
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 2 cups chicken stock or broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • Minced parsley for garnish

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Arrange quartered chicken pieces in a long shallow baking dish
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, turning pieces twice during cooking and in a small skillet melt butter, stir in salt, pepper, paprika and tarragon; pour evenly over chicken pieces
  4. Continue to bake for about 15 minutes, until chicken is tender and brown while chicken bakes prepare sauce; saute onion, garlic and celery in butter until soft
  5. Add remaining ingredients; stir until blended; bring to a boil; lower heat and let simmer very gentle about 30 minutes or until thick and smooth
  6. Arrange chicken pieces on serving platter
  7. Pour sauce over and sprinkle with parsley
  8. Makes 8 servings

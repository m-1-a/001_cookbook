Jell-O Molded vegetable Relish

Ingredients

  • 1, 3-ounce package Jell-O (Lemon or Lime) Gelatin
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 3/4 cup cold water
  • 2 Tbs. vinegar
  • 2 tsps. grated onion
  • Dash of pepper
  • vegetable combinations (at bottom of the page)

Prep Steps

  1. Dissolve Jell-O and salt in boiling water next add cold water, vinegar, grated onion and pepper
  2. Chill until thickened then fold in your choice vegetable combination then pour into individual molds for salads or small molds for relish
  3. Chill until firm, about 3 hours then unmold
  4. For salad, serve with crisp lettuce and garnish with mayonnaise, if desired
  5. Makes about 3 cups or 6 salad servings or 8 relish servings

vegetable Combinations:

  1. 1/2 cup each finely chopped cabbage, celery, carrots, and 3 Tbs. finely chopped green pepper
  2. 3/4 cup each finely chopped cabbage and celery, 1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper, and 2 Tbs diced pimento
  3. 3/4 cup each finely chopped cabbage and celery, 1/2 cup chopped pickle, and 2 Tbs. diced pimento
  4. 3/4 cup each drained cooked peas and diced celery and 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  5. 1 cup each finely chopped cabbage, 1/2 cup sliced stuffed olives; omit salt
  6. 2/3 cup grated carrots and 1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper

Summer Salad

Dressing Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cup dairy sour cream
  2. 1 Tbs. horseradish mustard
  3. 2 tsps. sugar
  4. 1 tsp. lemon juice

Dressing:

  1. Combine ingredients; chill
  2. Serve with salad

Salad Ingredients

  • Leaf lettuce
  • 1, 29-ounce can peach halves, drained
  • 1, 17-ounce can pear halves, drained
  • 1, 14 1/4-ounce can sliced pineapple, drained
  • 1 lb. Armour Golden Star Ham, thinly slice
  • Cream cheese
  • Chopped walnuts
  • Canned blueberries
  • Whole strawberries

Salad:

  1. Line platter with lettuce; arrange peaches, pears, pineapple slices and ham on lettuce
  2. Roll cream cheese into balls; roll in walnuts
  3. Arrange cream cheese balls with pineapple
  4. Fill each peach half with blueberries; garnish each pear half with a strawberry
  5. Makes 4 servings

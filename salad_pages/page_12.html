Poppy Seed Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1 tsp. fresh grated lemon peel
  • 1 tsp. finely minced onion
  • 1/3 cup fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup salad oil
  • Few drops red food coloring
  • 1 Tbs. poppy seeds

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except food coloring and poppy seeds in electric blender or covered container
  2. Cover and blend or shake until thoroughly mixed
  3. Tint mixture to a delicate pink color with a drop or two of red food coloring
  4. Stir in poppy seeds
  5. Chill before serving with fresh fruits in season
  6. Makes 1 1/2 cups

Old Fashion Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's (or Best Foods) Real Mayonnaise
  • 1 cup minced onions
  • 3 Tbs. white vinegar
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • 3 lbs. potatoes, cooked, peeled, cubed (about 8 cups)
  • 2 cups sliced celery

Prep Steps

  1. In large bowl stir together Mayonnaise, onion, vinegar, salt, pepper and eggs
  2. Add potatoes and celery; toss to coat well
  3. Cover; refrigerate for at least 4 hours
  4. If desired, sprinkle with paprika
  5. Makes about 8 cups

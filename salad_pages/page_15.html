Tomato French Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1, 10 3/4-ounce can Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup
  • 1/2 cup salad oil
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard

Prep Steps

  1. In covered jar or shaker, combine ingredients; shake well before using (Or mix in electric blender)
  2. Makes about 1 1/2 cups
  3. Variations: To 1 recipe of Tomato French Dressing add any one of the following:
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1 clove of medium garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish

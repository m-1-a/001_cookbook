Pecan Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Hellmann's (or Best Foods) Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup Karo Light Corn Syrup
  • 1 cup (8 ounces) Dannon Plain Yogurt

Prep Steps

  1. Stir together first 3 ingredients
  2. Fold in yogurt
  3. Chill
  4. Serve over fruit
  5. Makes 1 1/2 cups

Thousand Island Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Hellmann's (or Best Foods) Real Mayonnaise
  • 1 hard-cooked egg, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1 Tbs. chopped pimento stuffed olive
  • 1 Tbs. chopped dill pickle
  • 1 tsp. grated onion
  • 1, 8-ounce cup Dannon Plain Yogurt

Prep Steps

  1. Stir together first 6 ingredients
  2. Fold in yogurt
  3. Chill
  4. Makes 2 cups

California-Style Blue Cheese Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's (or Best Foods) Real Mayonnaise
  • 1, 4-ounce package blue cheese, crumbled (1 cup)
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1 Tbs. grated onion
  • 4 to 5 drops hot pepper sauce

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Cover; chill for 1 hour
  3. Serve over fresh cooked chilled vegetables or tossed salad
  4. Makes 1 2/3 cups

Cannellini Chef's Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (20 ounces each) Progresso Cannellini Beans
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 Tbs. wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tsps. oregano leaves, crumbled
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 3 quarts torn iceberg lettuce
  • 1/4 lb. sliced ham, slivered
  • 2 ounces sliced salami, slivered
  • 1/4 lb. Mozzarella cheese, slivered
  • 1/4 cup sliced roasted peppers

Prep Steps

  1. Drain cannellini beans thoroughly meanwhile, in a medium bowl mix together oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, salt and black pepper
  2. Stir in cannellini beans next place lettuce in a very large bowl
  3. Spoon beans in a circle close to side of bowl and arrange ham, salami, cheese and roasted peppers in any desired pattern over the lettuce
  4. Toss just before serving
  5. Makes 6 portions

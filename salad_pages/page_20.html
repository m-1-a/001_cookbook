Low Calorie Blue Cheese Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1, 8-oz. can tomato sauce
  • 2 Tbs. catsup
  • 4 tsps. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. grated onion
  • 1/4 tsp. Tabasco Hot Pepper Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Blend together all ingredients except blue cheese
  2. Stir in blue cheese
  3. Serve with salad greens
  4. Makes 1 1/3 cups (25 calories per Tbs.)

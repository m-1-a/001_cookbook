Peanut Butter Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Skippy Super Chunk Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 cup Karo Dark corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup milk

Prep Steps

  1. In small bowl stir together peanut butter and corn syrup until blended
  2. Stir in milk then cover; refrigerate
  3. Serve over fresh fruit
  4. Makes about 1 1/3 cups

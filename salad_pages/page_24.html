Sesame Salmon Boats

Ingredients

  • 1, 7 3/4-oz. can Bumble Bee Red Salmon
  • 1 avocado
  • Crisp salad greens
  • Lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp. dill weed
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic salt
  • 2 tsps. toasted sesame seeds

Prep Steps

  1. Drain salmon, remove skin if desired, and mash bones
  2. Halve avocado; remove seed then place each half on a salad line plate with crisp salad greens
  3. Sprinkle with lemon juice next combine mayonnaise, dill weed, garlic salt and 1 tsp. sesame seeds
  4. Fold in salmon and mashed bones next spoon into avocado halves
  5. Sprinkle with remaining sesame seeds to serve
  6. Makes 2 servings

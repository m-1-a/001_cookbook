Crisp and Crunchy Tuna

Ingredients

  • 1, 1-lb., 4-oz. can Dole Sliced Pineapple in Juice
  • 1, 7-oz. can Bumble Bee Solid White Albacore
  • 1, 11-oz. can mandarin orange segments, drained
  • 1 medium cucumber, peeled, scored and sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • Crisp salad greens
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp. curry powder

Prep Steps

  1. Drain pineapple, reserving 2 Tbs. juice next break large chunks of tuna with a spoon
  2. Toss tuna, oranges, cucumber and green onion then spoon onto each of 5 salad plates lined with crisp salad greens
  3. Arrange 2 slices pineapple over each
  4. Combine reserved juice, mayonnaise, lemon juice and curry
  5. Spoon over each salad to serve
  6. Makes 5 servings

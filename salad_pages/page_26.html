Italian Bean Salad

Ingredients

  • 1, 6-oz. envelope Italian dressing mox
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 1, 9-oz. package frozen Italian green beans, cooked, drained
  • 1, 16-oz. can chick peas, drained
  • 1, 7-oz. can pitted ripe olives, drained
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Hellman's Best Foods Real mayonnaise

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl stir together dressing mix and vinegar
  2. Add next 5 ingredients; toss to coat well
  3. Cover; chill overnight
  4. Just before serving, toss salad with Real mayonnaise
  5. Makes 6-8 servings

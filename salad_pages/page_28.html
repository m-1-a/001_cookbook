California Sliced vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 1 Tbs. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. basil leaves, crumbled
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley

Prep Steps

  1. On a shallow platter alternate arrange avocado, tomatoes and onion slices, one slice overlapping the next
  2. In a small container combine remaining ingredients except parsley
  3. Mix well then pour over vegetables
  4. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour
  5. Sprinkle with parsley and serve
  6. Makes 6 servings

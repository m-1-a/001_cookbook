Country-Style Cole Slaw

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Hellmann's Best Food Real Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 2 Tbs. cider vinegar
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1/8 tsp. celery seeds
  • 4 cups coarsely shredded cabbage
  • 3/4 cup coarsely shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced green pepper
  • 2 Tbs. sliced green onions

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl stir together first 6 ingredients
  2. Add remaining ingredients; toss to coat well
  3. Cover; chill at least 2 hours
  4. Makes 4 cups

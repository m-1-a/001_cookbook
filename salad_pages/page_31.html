Acapulco Salad

Ingredients

  • 1, 2 1/4-oz. can sliced ripe olives
  • 1, 8 1/2-oz. can garbanzo beans
  • 1, 8 3/4-oz. can Del Monte Whole Kernel Family Style Corn
  • 1/2 head iceberg lettuce
  • 1 bunch radishes
  • Bottle green goddess dressing
  • Garlic salt and pepper to taste
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Drain olives, garbanzo beans and corn
  2. Using sharp knife, shred lettuce, and thinly slice radishes
  3. Toss ingredients with dressing
  4. Garnish with garlic salt, pepper and cheddar cheese

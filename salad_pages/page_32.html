Ensalada Verde

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive or salad oil
  • 2 Tbs. vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, halved
  • 1 tsp. each: dry mustard, salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup pimento stuffed olives
  • 1 package each: frozen Italian green beans, French-style green beans, green peas, asparagus spears - all cooked and drained
  • 1 large cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
  • Salsa Aioli (see below)

Prep Steps

  1. Combine for marinade; oil, vinegar and seasonings then beat or shake well
  2. Arrange avocados, olives and vegetables in serving dish
  3. Pour marinade over vegetables and chill for several hours
  4. Makes 6-8 servings

Salsa Aioli:

  1. Crush 6 cloves garlic; beat in 2 egg yolks
  2. With fork or wire whisk, slowly beat in 1 cup olive oil and juice of 1 lemon
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Makes about 1 cup

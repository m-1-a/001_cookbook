Avocado and Mushroom Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 Tbs. white wine vinegar or white wine
  • 1 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, halved
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 avocados, peeled, pitted and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 lb. mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • Parsley, for garnish

Prep Steps

  1. Combine and chill marinade; oil, vinegar, seasonings and lemon juice
  2. Layer sliced avocados and mushrooms on platter
  3. Marinate in dressing for 1 hour
  4. Makes 6-8 servings

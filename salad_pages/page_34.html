Waldorf Salad a la Russe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Wish-Bone Russian Dressing
  • 1/4 cup dairy sour cream
  • 1 cup diced apple
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl, blend Wish-Bone Russian Dressing with sour cream
  2. Add apple, celery and walnuts; toss together
  3. If desired, serve in lettuce cups
  4. Makes about 4 servings

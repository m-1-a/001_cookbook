Summer Salad with Sour Cream Dressing

Salad Ingredients

  • 2 tsps. unflavored gelatine
  • Sour Cream Dressing (see below)
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • 1 cup (1/4 pound) crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. minced scallion
  • 1 tsp. Tabasco Red Hot Pepper Sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • 3 cups cut-up fresh fruit in season (melon balls, strawberries, pear strips, citrus sections, etc.)
  • 6 lettuce cups

Salad Prep Steps

  1. Sprinkle gelatine over water; stir over hot water until gelatine is dissolved next combine cheese; blend in gelatine
  2. Stir mayonnaise, scallion and Tabasco into cheese mixture until all ingredients are thoroughly blended then fold whipped cream into cheese
  3. Pour into lighly oiled quart mold and chill until firm, about 4 hours
  4. Carefully unmold cheese salad onto plate next surround mold with individual fruit salads in lettuce cups
  5. Serve with Sour Cream Dressing

Sour Cream Dressing

  • 1 Tbs. sour cream
  • 1 Tbs. mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp. Tabasco Red Hot Pepper Sauce

Dressing Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Refrigerate until ready to serve
  3. Makes 6 servings

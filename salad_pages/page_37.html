Salmon Dill Mousse

Ingredients

  • 2, 3-oz. packages or 1, 6-oz. package Jell-O Lemon Gelatin
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 3 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1, 1-lb. can pink salmon, drained and flaked
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. minced onions
  • 1 1/2 tsp. dill weed

Prep Steps

  1. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water then add cold water and lemon juice
  2. Chill until thickened next mix salmon with remaining ingredients
  3. Blend into thickened gelatin and pour into an 8 * 4-inch loaf pan
  4. Chill until firm - about 4 hours then unmold
  5. Garnish with dill and thinly sliced cucumbers, if desired
  6. Makes 5 1/3 cups or 6-8 servings

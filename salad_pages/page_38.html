Beets in a Mustard Ring

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 Tbs. dry mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1, 3-oz. package Royal Lemon Gelatin
  • 3/4 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped
  • Small whole pickled beets

Prep Steps

  1. Break eggs in top of double boiler and beat until foamy next add vinegar, sugar, dry mustard and salt; blend well
  2. Dissolve Royal Lemon Gelatin in boiling water, add to egg mixture and cook over simmering water, stirring frequently, until mixture is of custard consistency
  3. Cool and fold whipped cream into cooled mixture then pour into a 5-cup ring mold and chill until firm
  4. To serve, unmold and fill center with pickled beets
  5. Makes 8-10 servings

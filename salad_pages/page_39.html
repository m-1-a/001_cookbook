California Fruit Salad Rose

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope Knox Unflavored Gelatin
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 3/4 cup boiling water
  • 1 1/4 cup rose wine
  • 1 cup thinly sliced peaches
  • 1/2 cup bananas
  • 1/2 cup strawberries

Prep Steps

  1. In medium bowl, mix Unflavored Gelatin with sugar; add boiling water and stir until Gelatin is completely dissolved
  2. Stir in wine and chill, stirring occasionally, until mixture is consistency of unbeaten egg whites
  3. Fold in peaches, bananas and strawberries then turn into 4-cup mold or bowl and chill until firm
  4. Makes about 6 servings

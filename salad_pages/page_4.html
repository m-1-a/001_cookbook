Sunkist Onion and Orange Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 large mild flavored onion
  • 2 large Sunkist Oranges, peeled
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. oregano, crushed
  • 1/4 cup salad oil
  • 1 Tbs. fresh squeezed orange juice
  • 1 Tbs. fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 Tbs. ripe black olives, sliced
  • Coarsely ground black pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Cut onion into 8 thin slices, and oranges into 6 slices each
  2. Place in glass dish; marinate for 15 minutes in mixture of salt, oregano, oil, orange and lemon juice
  3. Arrange in a row on individual beds of crisp lettuce, alternating 3 orange slices and 2 onion slices per serving
  4. Top with sliced olives and spoon over remaining marinade then add a generous sprinkling of fresh ground pepper
  5. Makes 4 servings

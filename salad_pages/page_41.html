Hot Dan's Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup French's Prepared Mustard
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 2 Tbs. vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. half and half or undiluted evaporated milk
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients; beat with rotary beater until light and fluffy
  2. Makes 1/2 cup

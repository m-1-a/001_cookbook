Family French Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Heinz Tomato Ketchup
  • 1/2 cup salad oil
  • 1/4 cup Heinz Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 tsps. confectioner's sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, split
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in jar
  2. Cover; shake vigorously
  3. Chill to blend flavors
  4. Remove garlic; shake again before serving
  5. Makes 1 1/4 cups

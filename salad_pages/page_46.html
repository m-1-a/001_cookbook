Louis Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's Best Foods Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup spinach leaves
  • 5 watercress sprigs
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp. sugar

Prep Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in blender container; cover
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Cover; chill
  4. Makes 1 1/4 cups

Remoulade Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's Best Foods Real Mayonnaise
  • 4 sprigs parsley
  • 2 Tbs. chili sauce
  • 2 Tbs. capers
  • 2 Tbs. milk
  • 2 tsps. prepared mustard
  • 1 small clove garlic

Prep Steps

  1. Place ingredients in blender container; cover
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Cover; chill
  4. Makes 1 1/4 cups

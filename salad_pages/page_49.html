Tangy Coconut Salad Dressing for Fruit, Ham or Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup corn oil
  • 1/2 cup Coco Casa Cream of Coconut
  • 1, 6-oz. can frozen concentrated orange juice, thawed and undiluted
  • 1/4 tsp. curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine all ingredients and beat until smooth and thick
  2. Chill until ready to serve
  3. Beat again and toss with salad when ready to serve
  4. Makes 1 3/4 cups

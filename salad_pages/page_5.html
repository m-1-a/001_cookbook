Under The Sea Salad

Ingredients

  • 1, 16-ounce can pear halves
  • 1, 3-ounce package Jell-O Lime Gelatin
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 2, 3-ounce package cream cheese
  • 1/8 tsp. cinnamon
  • Chicory or watercress
  • Mayonnaise, optional

Prep Steps

  1. Drain pears, reserving 1/8 of the syrup next dice pears and set aside
  2. Dissolve Jell-O and salt in boiling water then add the reserved syrup and lemon juice
  3. Pour 1 1/4 cups into an 8 * 4-inch loaf pan and chill until set but not firm, about 1 hour
  4. Meanwhile, soften cheese until creamy next very slowly blend in remaining gelatin, beating until smooth
  5. Blend in pears, spoon into pan and chill until firm, about 4 hours
  6. Unmold and garnish with chicory or watercress and serve with mayonnaise, if desired
  7. Makes about 3 and 1/2 cups or 6 servings
  8. Note: Recipe may be doubled, using a 9 * 5-inch loaf pan. Recipe may be chilled in a 4-cup mold

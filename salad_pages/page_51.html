California Tossed Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup large pieces Diamond Walnuts
  • 1/3 cup salad oil
  • 1 tsp. seasoned salt
  • 3 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. marjoram, crumbled
  • 1/4 tsp. dill weed
  • 1 1/2 quarts torn mixed salad greens
  • 6 ozs. cooked shrimp or prawns (or 1, 5-oz. can drained)
  • 1 large tomato, cut in wedges
  • 1/4 cup radish slices
  • 1/4 cup ripe olive slices
  • 2 Tbsp. chopped chives or green onion
  • 1/4 cup cheese cubes

Prep Steps

  1. Toast walnuts lightly in small skillet over low heat with 1 tsp. oil, stirring frequently, about 5 minutes
  2. Remove from heat; sprinkle with 1/4 tsp. seasoned salt, cool and beat together remaining oil, salt, lemon juice, sugar, marjoram and dill weed
  3. In large bowl, combine greens, shrimp, tomato, radish, olives and chives next stir dressing; pour over salad
  4. Toss until evenly coated and add walnuts; toss again
  5. Sprinkle with cheese then serve at once
  6. Makes 2 quarts salads, 4 large servings

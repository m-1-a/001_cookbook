Crescent City Salad

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup salad oil
  • 1/3 cup Heinz Wine Vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp. caraway seeds
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 6 cups torn salad greens, chilled
  • 1/2 lb. cooked shrimp
  • 1 cup tomato chunks
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion

Prep Steps

  1. Combine first 6 ingredients in jar then cover; shake vigorously
  2. Add mushrooms; chill to blend flavors
  3. Shake again before tossing with salad greens, shrimp, tomatoes and onion
  4. Makes 8 servings (about 8 cups)

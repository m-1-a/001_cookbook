Ivan's Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 10 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 6 Tbs. oil
  • 3 Tbs. vinegar
  • 3 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 tsp. powdered dill
  • Dash of salt and pepper
  • 4 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 Tbs. (1/2 oz.) Romanoff Caviar
  • Additional Caviar for garnish

Prep Steps

  1. Cook potatoes in boiling salted water until barely tender, about 8 minutes then drain
  2. In large bowl, combine oil, vinegar, lemon juice, onion, salt and pepper next add potatoes; toss to coat
  3. Mash eggs wit mayonnaise and gently stir in Caviar and fold into potatoes
  4. Cover; keep cold then at serving time, garnish with additional Caviar
  5. Makes 8 generous servings

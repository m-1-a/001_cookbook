Mexican Macaroni Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's Best Foods Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup skim milk
  • 2 tsps. instant chopped onion
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/8 tsp. garlic powder
  • 6 drops hot pepper sauce
  • 1, 8-oz. package elbow macaroni, cooked, drained
  • 1, 16-oz. can red kidney beans, well drained
  • 1 cup diced part-skim Mozzarella cheese (4 ozs.)

Prep Steps

  1. In large bowl stir together first 7 ingredients
  2. Add remaining ingredients; toss to coat well
  3. Cover; chill at least 2 hours
  4. Makes 8, 1-cup servings

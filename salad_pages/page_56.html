Southwestern Noodle Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. medium to small shell macaroni, cooked al dente, thoroughly drained
  • 2/3 cup cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup minced celery (about 2 stalks)
  • 1/2 cup chopped (about 1/2) large green pepper
  • 6 green onions, minced
  • 1, 2-oz. jar chopped pimento, drained
  • 3 generous dashes French Company Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 dashes hot pepper sauce
  • 1 Tbs. minced, roasted green chili pepper
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. freshly ground pepper
  • 1, 15-oz. can black-eyed peas, drained
  • 1, 12-oz. can corn, drained
  • 1/2 cup pitted black olives, drained and chopped
  • 1, 2-oz. jar green olives with pimento, drained and chopped
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise (about)

Prep Steps

  1. Place macaroni in large bowl
  2. Pour vinegar over and let stand while preparing other ingredients
  3. Add all other ingredients to macaroni and mix well
  4. Cover and refrigerate 2 to 3 days
  5. Taste for seasoning before serving
  6. Makes 12 servings

Four-Layer vegetable Salad

Dressing Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Land O Lakes Sour Cream
  • 1/2 cup salad dressing or mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. dill weed

Salad Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 cup frozen peas, thawed and drained
  • 5 cups torn head lettuce (1/2 to 3/4 head)
  • 1 cup (4 ozs.) shredded Land O Lakes Medium Natural Cheddar Cheese
  • 5 slices bacon, fried and finely crumbled

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all dressing ingredients in large bowl; blend well
  2. Layer onion, celery, green pepper, peas and lettuce over dressing
  3. Sprinkle with Cheese and top with bacon
  4. Cover and refrigerate overnight or at least 4 hours
  5. Toss before serving
  6. Makes 6, 1-cup servings

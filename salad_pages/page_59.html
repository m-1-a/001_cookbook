Cool Corn Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup commercial sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 2 tsps. white vinegar
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1, 17-oz. can Stokely's Finest Whole Kernel Golden Corn, drained
  • 1, 2-oz. can Stokely's Finest sliced Pimentos, drained and sliced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup diced onion

Prep Steps

  1. In medium-size bowl, make dressing by combining sour cream, mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper
  2. Add remaining ingredients and toss to blend
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour
  4. Makes 4 to 6 servings

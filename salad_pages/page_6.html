Cool 'N Creamy Coleslaw

Ingredients

  • 2 envelopes Knox Unflavored Gelatin
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups boiling water
  • 1 1/3 cups mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 4 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion

Prep Steps

  1. In large bowl, mix Knox Unflavored Gelatin and sugar; add boiling water and stir until gelatin is completely dissolved
  2. With wire whisk or rotary blender, blend in mayonnaise and lemon juice; chill until mixture is consistency of unbeaten egg whites
  3. Stir in cabbage, carrots, and onion; pour into 11 * 7-inch pan and chill until firm
  4. To serve, cut into squares
  5. Makes about 8 servings

Studio City Special

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots (4 large carrots)
  • 1/2 cup Sun-Maid Seedless Raisins
  • 1, 13 1/4-oz. can pineapple tidbits, drained
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbs. instant minced onion
  • 1 tsp. prepared mustard
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. celery seed

Prep Steps

  1. Pare and cut carrots into chunks
  2. Whirl (dry) in blender until grated
  3. Combine all ingredients
  4. Serve in lettuce cups
  5. Make 4 servings

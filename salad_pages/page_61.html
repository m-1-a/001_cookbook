Tart Cranberry Mold

Ingredients

  • 1, 3-oz. package lemon-flavored gelatin
  • 1 1/2 cups boiling water
  • 1 1/2 cups raw cranberries
  • 1/2 medium orange, seeded, unpeeled
  • 1/3 cup chopped Heinz Sweet Pickles
  • 3 Tbs. sugar
  • 2 cups prepared whipped topping mix

Prep Steps

  1. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; chill slightly thickened meanwhile, put cranberries and oranges through food chopper using fine blade
  2. Stir in pickles and sugar next fold in cranberry mixture into thickened gelatin; then gently fold in whipped topping mix
  3. Pour into a 1-quart (or 8 1/2-cup) molds then chill until firm
  4. Unmold on lettuce or endive
  5. Makes 8 servings

California Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope French's Sour Cream Sauce Mix
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. French's Parsley Flakes
  • 2 Tbs. vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. anchovy paste, if desired
  • 1/2 tsp. French's Garlic Salt
  • 1/8 tsp. French's Pepper Salad greens

Prep Steps

  1. Prepare Sour Cream Sauce with milk as directed on envelope
  2. Stir in mayonnaise, Parsley Flakes, vinegar, anchovy paste, Garlic Salt and Pepper
  3. Serve with salad greens
  4. Makes 2 cups dressing
  5. Note: If prefered, toss salad with crisp croutons and tiny cooked shrimp just before adding dressing

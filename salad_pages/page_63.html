Yogurt Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Dannon Plain Yogurt
  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 4 Tbs. flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a skillet and stir in flour
  2. Add milk all at once and stir over medium heat until thick
  3. Remove from heat and beat in egg yolk, lemon juice, mustard and salt
  4. Stir in yogurt and cool
  5. Makes approximately 2 cups

Chicken Salad Habanera

Salad Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups boned, cooked chicken, in thin strips
  • 1 cup California ripe olives, sliced in wedges
  • 1 California avocado, cut into crescents
  • 1/3 cup green pepper strips
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 to 3 Tbs. pimiento, in thin strips
  • 4 cups lettuce shredded
  • Dressing (see ingredients below)

Prep Steps

  1. Combine ingredients; hold aside avocado, cover, and chill
  2. Combine dressing ingredients in a jar and shake well next refrigerate
  3. Just before serving add avocados, shake dressing and pour over salad then toss lightly, serve on bed of shredded lettuce
  4. Serves 4

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup salad oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp. fresh garlic, minced

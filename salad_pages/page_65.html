New Delhi Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped chutney
  • 2 tsps. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. curry powder
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 2, 5-oz. cans Swanson Chunk Chicken
  • 1 small apple, diced (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 2 Tbs. toasted silvered almonds
  • 4 slices pineapple

Prep Steps

  1. In bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, chutney, lemon juice, curry and salt
  2. Toss lightly with Chicken, apple, celery and salt
  3. Chill and serve on pineapple slices
  4. Garnish with additional almonds if desired
  5. Makes about 2 1/2 cups; 4 servings

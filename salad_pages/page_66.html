Chicken Salad Supreme

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 Tbs. Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
  • 2 Tbs. each chopped pimiento and pickle relish
  • 3/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup silvered toasted almonds
  • 2 hard-cooked eggs, diced
  • 1 Tbs. minced onion

Prep Steps

  1. Combine mayonnaise and mustard
  2. Toss dressing lightly with remaining ingredients
  3. Chill
  4. If desired, serve on a bed of greens
  5. Serves 4

Deviled Ham Stuffed Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 2, 4 1/2-oz cans Underwood Deviled Ham
  • 1 cup (4 ozs) shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped pimentos-stuffed olives
  • 2 Tbs. chopped onion
  • 4 medium tomatoes, cut into quarters, to within 1/4 inch of bottom

Prep Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together deviled ham, cheese, olives and onion
  2. Spoon mixture into center of tomatoes
  3. Chill
  4. Makes 4 servings

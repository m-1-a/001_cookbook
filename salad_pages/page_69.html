Celebration Salad

Ingredients

  • Cooked asparagus spears
  • Bottled Italian dressing
  • 8 ozs., (2 cups) Mueller's Elbow Macaroni
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. catsup
  • 1/2 tsp. prepared horseradish
  • 1/4 tsp. dry mustard
  • 2 Tbs. sliced scallions
  • 2 cups silvered cooked ham
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • Watercress
  • Cherry tomatoes

Prep Steps

  1. Marinate asparagus in Italian dressing overnight, or at least 3 to 4 hours
  2. Cook Mueller's Elbow Macaroni as directed on package; drain then rinse with cold water; drain again
  3. In bowl blend mayonnaise, catsup, horseradish, mustard and scallions; toss in macaroni, ham and celery
  4. To serve, arrange asparagus spears on platter; top with macaroni salad
  5. Garnish with Watercress and cherry tomatoes
  6. Makes 6 servings

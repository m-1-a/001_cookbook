Cranberry Apple Waldorf

Ingredients

  • 3 envelopes Knox Unflavored Gelatin
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 3 1/2 cups cranberry juice cocktail
  • 1 cup chopped apple
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/3 cup chopped walnuts

Prep Steps

  1. In large bowl, mix Know Unflavored Gelatin and sugar; add bowling water and stir until gelatin is completely dissolved
  2. Add cranberry juice; chill until mixture is consistency of unbeaten egg whites
  3. Fold in apple, celery and walnuts; pour into 8 or 9-inch square pan and chill until firm
  4. To serve, cut into squares, serves as a relish
  5. Makes about 8 servings

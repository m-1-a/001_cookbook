Salad Oscar

Ingredients

  • 1, 12-oz. package Oscar Mayer Cheese Smokies (links)
  • 1 small head lettuce, (about 1 lb.)
  • 1/2 medium green pepper, sliced into rings
  • 2 slices onion, separated into rings
  • 1 medium tomato, cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup sliced fresh mushrooms, cauliflowerettes or ripe olives
  • 3/4 cup creamy French dressing
  • 1/2 cup flavored croutons

Prep Steps

  1. Cut links into 1/2-inch slices; set aside next wash lettuce; use darker green leaves to line individual serving plates
  2. Tear remaining lettuce into bite size pieces then arrange lettuce pieces and remaining vegetables on lettuce lined plates
  3. In saucepan combine smoked sausage links with French dressing; heat to boiling, stirring often and reduce heat to low; cover; heat for 5 minutes
  4. Top salad with smoked sausage link mixture and croutons
  5. Serve immediately
  6. Makes 2 to 4 servings

Perfection Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope Knox Unflavored Gelatine
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 1/4 cups water, divided
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage, red or green
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 pimiento, cut in small pieces, or 2 Tbs. chopped sweet red or green pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Mix Knox Unflavored Gelatine, sugar and salt thoroughly in a small saucepan then add 1/2 cup of water
  2. Place over low heat, stirring constantly until gelatin is dissolved next remove from heat and stir in remaining 3/4 cup of water, vinegar and lemon juice
  3. Chill mixture to unbeaten egg white consistency then fold in shredded cabbage, celery and pimiento or pepper
  4. Turn in a 2 cup mold or individual molds and chill until firm
  5. Unmold on serving plate and garnish with salad greens
  6. Serve with favorite salad dressing

Variations

  1. Pineapple Perfection Salad: Substitute 1/2 cup canned pineapple juice for 3/4 cup of water. Reduce sugar to 2 Tbs.
  2. Olive Perfection Salad: Substitute 1/2 cup chopped ripe olives for for the pimiento
  3. Peanut Perfection: Substitute 1/2 cup chopped peanuts for the celery
  4. Cucumber and Onion Perfection: Substitute 1/2 cup chopped cucumbers and 1 small onion, chopped for the celery
  5. Cauliflower Perfection Salad: Substitute 1/2 cup finely cut crisp raw cauliflower and 2 Tbs. chopped green pepper for 1/2 cup of the chopped celery

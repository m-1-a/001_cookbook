Jello Artichoke Salad

Ingredients

  • 1, 9-ounce package Birds Eye Deluxe Artichoke Hearts
  • 1 cup (about 1/4 lb. ) sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 cup prepared Good Seasons Italian Salad Dressing
  • 1, 3-ounce package Jell-O (Lemon or Lime)
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 2 tsps. vinegar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 Tbs. sliced pimiento

Prep Steps

  1. Cook artichoke hearts as directed on package then drain and combine with mushrooms in a bowl
  2. Pour dressing over vegetables and allow to marinate for at least 1 hour next drain, reserving marinade
  3. Dissolve Jell-O in boiling water then add vinegar and cold water
  4. Chill until thickened and fold in the drained mushrooms and artichoke hearts, and pimiento
  5. Pour into a 4-cup mold next chill until firm, about 4 hours
  6. Makes 3 1/2 cups or 6 salads
  7. Note: If desired, reserve marinade can be combined with mayonnaise and used as a dressing for the salad

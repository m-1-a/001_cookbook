Lemon Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 tsp. prepared mustard
  • Grated peel and juice of a 1/2 fresh Sunkist Lemon
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing

Prep Steps

  1. In a saucepan, melt butter then remove from heat; stir in flour, salt and pepper
  2. Gradually blend in water and mustard then cook over medium heat, stirring until thickened
  3. Add lemon peel and juice then remove from heat; blend in mayonnaise
  4. Makes about 1 and 1/3 cups

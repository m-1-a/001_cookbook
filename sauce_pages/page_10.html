Basic Newburg Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 egg yolk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 Tbs. Holland House Sherry Cooking Wine

Prep Steps

  1. Heat butter in large skillet then sprinkle flour into butter and blend thoroughly
  2. Gradually add heavy cream to mixture and cook, stirring constantly until thick and smooth (do not boil)
  3. Pour mixture over well-beaten egg yolk, stirring constantly with spoon or whisk
  4. Salt and pepper to taste

Seafood Newburg

Prep Steps

  1. Add to Newburg sauce 1 lb. cooked shrimp or lobster meat
  2. Seasoned by tossing with 2 Tbs. Holland House Sherry Cooking Wine
  3. Cook over boiling water for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly
  4. Stir in cooking wine
  5. Heat through and serve over rice or toast
  6. Makes 4 average servings

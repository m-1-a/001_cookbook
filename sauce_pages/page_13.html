Blender Hollandaise Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine (1 stick)
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2-3 Tbs. fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash of pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Heat butter or margarine until bubbly
  2. Meanwhile place egg yolks, fresh lemon juice, salt and pepper in electric blender
  3. Turn blender on and off quickly then turn to high speed and slowly add bubbly butter in a very thin but steady stream
  4. Serve immediately over broccoli, asparagus, or grilled tomatoes
  5. Also good on fish or Eggs Benedict
  6. Makes about 1 cup

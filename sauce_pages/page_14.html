Continental Lemon Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Hellmann's Best Foods Real Mayonnaise
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. dry mustard

Prep Steps

  1. In small saucepan with wire whisk beat all ingredients until smooth
  2. Stirring constantly,cook over medium-low heat until thick (do not boil)
  3. Serve over vegetables, seafood, or poached eggs
  4. Sprinkle with paprika
  5. Makes 1 and 1/2 cups

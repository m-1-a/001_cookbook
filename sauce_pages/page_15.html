Burgundy Steak Sauce a la Worcester

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2, 10 1/2-oz. cans beef gravy
  • 3 Tbs. Burgundy or other dry red wine
  • 4 tsp. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley

Prep Steps

  1. In a small saucepan melt butter and add onion; saute for 3 minutes
  2. Stir in gravy, wine and Lea & Perrins then cook and stir until hot, about 2 minutes
  3. Stir in parsley
  4. Serve hot over steak or hamburgers
  5. Makes approximately 3 cups

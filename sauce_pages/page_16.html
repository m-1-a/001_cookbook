Hunt's Hawaiian Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 2 tsps. cornstarch
  • 1, 8-oz. can Hunt's Tomato Sauce
  • 1, 8 1/2-oz. can crushed pineapple, undrained
  • 1 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 Tbs. minced crystallized ginger
  • 1/4 tsp. onion salt
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Blend brown sugar and cornstarch in 1-quart saucepan
  2. Add remaining ingredients; blend thoroughly
  3. Bring to a boil, stirring
  4. Lower heat; cover, simmer gently for 15 - 20 minutes and stir occasionally
  5. Use as a basting or serving sauce for chicken, ribs, meatballs
  6. Makes 2 cups

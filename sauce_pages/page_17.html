Hunt's Quick Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 3, 8-oz. can Hunt's Tomato Sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1, 4-oz. can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 2 Tbs. minced onion flakes
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp. light brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 3/4 tsp. oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. basil
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic salt
  • 1/8 tsp. marjoram

Prep Steps

  1. Saute ground beef in skillet; drain
  2. Add remaining ingredients then heat to boiling
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes and stir occasionally
  4. Serve over hot cooked spaghetti or other pasta
  5. Makes 4 servings

Come and Get It Sauce for Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. Tabasco Pepper Sauce

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat gently; do not allow to boil
  2. Spoon over vegetables when serving, or place vegetable in a casserole, top with sauce, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and place under broiler until crumbs are brown, 1 - 2 minutes
  3. Ideal with cooked fresh asparagus, broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, green beans, etc.
  4. Sauce for 4 - 6 servings

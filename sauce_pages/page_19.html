Beef Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 cup chili sauce
  • 1/2 cup beer
  • 1/2 cup slice stuffed olives

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer for 5 minutes or until flavors are blended
  2. Makes 1 1/2 cups sauce

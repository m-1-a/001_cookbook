Lemon Tartar Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped dill pickle
  • 2 Tbs. finely chopped green onion
  • 1 Tbs. chopped canned pimiento, optional
  • 1 tsp. fresh grated Sunkist Lemon peel
  • 2 tsps. fresh squeezed lemon juice

Prep Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine all ingredients
  2. Makes about 3/4 cup

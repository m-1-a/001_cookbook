Barbecue Sauce for Hot Dogs

Ingredients

  • 1, 7 3/4-oz. jar Gerber Junior Peach Cobbler
  • 1/3 cup catsup
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. ginger
  • 1/4 tsp. mace
  • 1 tsp. onion salt

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients thoroughly
  2. Score hot dogs diagonally on three sides
  3. Barbecue, basting with sauce about three times while cooking
  4. Makes 1 1/2 cups sauce (enough for 2 lbs. of hot dogs)
  5. Variations: Use on pork or chicken. Use heated sauce in chafing dish with sliced hot dogs

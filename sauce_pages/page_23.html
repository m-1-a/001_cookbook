Worcestershire Butter Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp. Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/8 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. In a small saucepan melt butter
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients
  3. Heat thoroughly
  4. Serve over fish or vegetables

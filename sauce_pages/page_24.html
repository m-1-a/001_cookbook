Texas Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1, 5-oz. bottle Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cider vinegar
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 2 tsps. salt
  • 1 tsp. garlic salt
  • 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper

Prep Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan combine all ingredients and bring to a boiling point
  2. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 10 minutes
  3. Cool and pour into tightly covered container
  4. This sauce will keep refrigerated for several weeks
  5. Use for chicken, hamburgers or steaks
  6. Makes 4 cups

Delmonico Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup plain French dressing
  • 1 tsp. tomato catsup
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp. finely chopped green peppers
  • 1 hard boiled egg finely chopped or rubbed through a sieve

Prep Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients and serve on freshly cooked broccoli or green beans, or use as a superb salad dressing on crisp greens

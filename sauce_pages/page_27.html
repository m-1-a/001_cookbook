Tartar Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbs. of capers
  • 2 Tbs. of olives
  • 2 Tbs. of gherkins
  • 1 Tbs. of parsley
  • 1/4 tsp. of onion juice or finely chopped shallots or white onions

Prep Steps

  1. Chop capers, olives, gherkins, and parsley very fine
  2. Fold into the mayonnaise and serve cold
  3. Serve with fried fish or cold seafood

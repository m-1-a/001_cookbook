Creamy Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 tsps. Colman's Mustard (dry)
  • 2 to 3 tsps. water
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup beef bouillon
  • 1 Tbs. corn starch
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 Tbs. butter

Prep Steps

  1. Combine Colman's Mustard and water; let stand for 10 minutes
  2. Boil vinegar in small saucepan until reduce to 1 Tbs.
  3. Stir in heavy cream, bouillon, corn starch and salt
  4. Cook and stir until thickened
  5. Add butter and mustard mixture
  6. Serve with beef, pork, lamb and vegetables

Basic Bordelaise Sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. butter
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1 cup beef bouillon
  • 1/2 cup Holland House Red Cooking Wine
  • 1 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed

Prep Steps

  1. Heat butter in a large skillet
  2. Saute onion in butter until golden; add flour and blend thoroughly
  3. Add bouillon and stir until smooth
  4. Blend in Holland House Red Cooking Wine, parsley and garlic
  5. Delicious on steak, roast beef and chops

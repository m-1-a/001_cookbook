Creole Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup Heinz Tomato Ketchup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 Tbs. Heinz Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, saute onion and green pepper in butter until tender
  2. Stir in ketchup, water, Worcestershire Sauce, salt and pepper
  3. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  4. Serve over baked, broiled or fried fish, omelets or other meat or chicken dishes
  5. Makes 1 and 1/2 cups sauce

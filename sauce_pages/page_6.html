Slimmed-Down Pasta Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 (24-oz.) can tomato juice
  • 1 (6-oz.) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 2 cloves of large garlic, mashed
  • 1 tsp. oregano leaves, crushed
  • 1 tsp. onion salt
  • 1 medium bay leaf
  • Dash pepper

Prep Steps

  1. To make basic sauce, combine all ingredients in saucepan
  2. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Remove bay leaf
  4. Makes about 3 and 1/2 cups sauce (300 calories total)

Variations

:

Ground beef: In saucepan brown 1/2 lb. ground beef (10% fat); add basic sauce ingredients and proceed as directed. Makes about 4 and 1/3 cups sauce

Mushrooms: Add 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms, or a 4-oz. can sliced mushrooms (with liquid) to basic sauce ingredients; proceed as directed. Makes about 4 and 2/3 cups sauce

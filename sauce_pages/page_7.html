Easy Meat Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 envelope Lipton Onion Soup mix
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano
  • 1 (28-oz.) can tomato puree
  • 1 cup water

Prep Steps

  1. In large saucepan, brown ground beef with garlic; stir in envelope Lipton Onion Soup mix, oregano, tomato puree and water
  2. Simmer, covered, stirring occasionally, about 30 minutes
  3. Serve over hot noodles, spaghetti or rice
  4. Makes about 5 cups sauce

San Clemente Ham Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Sun-Maid Seedless Raisins
  • 1/2 cup Sun-Maid Golden Seedless Raisins
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 cup corn starch
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup dry mustard
  • Generous dash cloves
  • 2 Tbs. vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. ham drippings or butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup orange or pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup Chablis

Prep Steps

  1. Bring all ingredients to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes
  2. Makes 2 cups sauce

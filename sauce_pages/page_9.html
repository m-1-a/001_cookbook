Cranberry Wine Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 (16-oz.) can whole berry cranberry sauce
  • 1/4 cup Burgundy wine
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar, packed
  • 1 Tbs. prepared mustard
  • 1/4 tsp. onion salt

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, combine all ingredients
  2. Simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes
  3. Serve warm with sliced turkey
  4. To serve with cold sliced turkey, chill sauce and spoon over turkey
  5. Makes 2 and 1/2 cups cups

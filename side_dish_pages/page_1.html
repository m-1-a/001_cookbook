Fried Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes, half ripe
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 2 and 1/2 tsps. sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 3/4 cup Pet Evaporated Milk
  • Oil for frying

Prep Steps

  1. Wash tomatoes but do not peel them then cut into 3/4-inch slices
  2. Place on paper towels to drain next combine flour, salt, sugar and pepper
  3. Dust tomatoes in flour mixture on both sides and add evaporated milk to remaining flour mixture to make a thick batter
  4. Dip floured tomatoes in batter and fry in hot oil 1/2-inch deep until golden brown on both sides
  5. Makes 6 servings

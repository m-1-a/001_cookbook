Green Bean Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 10 and 3/4-ounce can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp. soy sauce
  • Dash of pepper
  • 2 packages (9 ounces each) frozen green beans, cooked and drained
  • 1 can (3 and 1/2 ounces) French fried onions

Prep Steps

  1. In 1 and 1/2-quart casserole, stir soup, milk, soy, and pepper until smooth; mix in green beans and 1/2 can onions
  2. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes; stir
  3. Top with remaining onions
  4. Bake 5 minutes more
  5. Makes about 4 cups

Corn 'N' Pepper Fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 can (12 ounces) golden whole kernel corn with sweet peppers, undrained
  • 1 Tbs. Planters Peanut Oil
  • 1 and 1/2 cups unsifted flour
  • 1 Tbs. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • Dash pepper
  • Planters Peanut Oil

Prep Steps

  1. Beat egg in large bowl and stir in milk, corn and 1 Tbs. Planters Peanut Oil
  2. Add and beat flour, baking powder, salt and pepper next drop by tablespoonfuls into deep or shallow hot (375F) Planters Peanut Oil
  3. Fry until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes each side and drain on paper towels
  4. Serve hot and if desired serve with syrup
  5. Makes about 24 fritters

Sweet Sour Shellie Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 strips bacon
  • 1/3 cup onion, finely diced
  • 1 1-lb. can Stokely's Finest Shellie Beans
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • Few grains pepper
  • 2 Tbs. white distilled vinegar

Prep Steps

  1. Cut bacon in 1/2-inch pieces and brown lightly with diced onion
  2. Add liquid from Shellie Beans and cook down to about 1/2 cupful
  3. Add remaining ingredients and Shellie Beans
  4. Heat and serve
  5. Makes 4 servings

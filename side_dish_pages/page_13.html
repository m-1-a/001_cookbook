Apple Kraut Bavarian

Ingredients

  • 1 10-ounces package brown and serve sausage links
  • 1 1-lb. can Stokely's Finest Bavarian Style Sauerkraut, drained
  • 1 1-lb., 1-ounce can Stokely's Finest Applesauce

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet, brown sausage links
  2. Add remaining ingredients to skillet and heat to serving temperature
  3. Delicious with mashed potatoes
  4. Makes 4 servings

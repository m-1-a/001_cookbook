Spaghetti With Zucchini Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 medium zucchini, sliced (about 6 cups)
  • 3 cups diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. basil leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano leaves
  • 8 ounces Mueller's Spaghetti Grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet or pot, saute onion in hot oil until crisp tender then add zucchini, tomatoes,salt, bay leaf, pepper, basil and oregano
  2. Simmer covered for 15 minutes; uncovered and simmer 10 minutes longer then discard bay leaf
  3. Meanwhile cook spaghetti as directed on package; drain then serve spaghetti topped with zucchini sauce and grated Parmesan cheese
  4. Makes 4 to 6 servings

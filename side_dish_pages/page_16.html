Grant Street Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 1, 20 ounce can Dole Pineapple Chunks, in syrup
  • 2 Tbs. soy sauce
  • 1 tsp. grated fresh ginger root
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup long-grain brown rice
  • 1/2 tsp. rosemary
  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 2 Tbs. chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup toasted slivered almonds

Prep Steps

  1. Drain pineapple syrup into saucepan then add water and bring to boil
  2. Stir in rice and rosemary next cover and simmer 35 to 45 minutes, until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed
  3. Mix in pineapple chunks and remaining ingredients and heat through
  4. Makes 4 to 6 servings

Mushroom Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp. basil, leaves, crushed
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 2 Tbs. Fleischmann's Margarine
  • 2/3 cup uncooked converted rice
  • 1 and 1/3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup chopped toasted blanched almonds

Prep Steps

  1. Saute mushrooms, onion, garlic, parsley, basil and pepper in margarine about 5 minutes, stirring frequently next stir in rice and water
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat then cover; reduce heat and simmer until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender, about 25 minutes
  3. Stir in almonds
  4. Makes 7 servings

Campfire Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 1 Tbs. flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded Hoffman's Smokey Sharp
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1, 12 ounce package frozen shredded hash brown potatoes

Prep Steps

  1. Saute onion in butter or margarine next place in shallow one-quart baking dish or small skillet, brush with butter
  2. Add flour, salt and pepper and mix well then add cheese
  3. Pour milk over cheese and top with frozen potatoes
  4. Cover and bake at 350F for 30 to 35 minutes, or until potatoes are completely thawed
  5. Mix well then return to oven uncovered and bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown
  6. Makes 4 servings

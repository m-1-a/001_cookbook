Creamed Spinach

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fresh spinach
  • 2 Tbs. all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup Pet Evaporated Milk
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Few grains pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Wash spinach thoroughly and shake water out of leaves
  2. Tear leaves into bite size pieces and place in saucepan
  3. Cover and cook over low heat for 8 minutes or until tender
  4. The water that clings to the leaves is enough to cook the spinach then drain
  5. Sprinkle flour over spinach and stir to evenly coat leaves
  6. Add evaporated milk, salt and pepper then heat until sauce thickens
  7. Makes 4 servings, 1/4 cup each

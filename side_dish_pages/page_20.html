Candied Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Karo Dark Corn Syrup
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
  • 2 Tbs. corn oil margarine
  • 12 medium sweet potatoes, cooked, peeled, halved lengthwise

Prep Steps

  1. In small saucepan, heat corn syrup, brown sugar and margarine to boiling; reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes
  2. Pour 1/2 cup of the syrup into 13 * 9 * 2-inch baking dish
  3. Arrange potatoes, overlapping if necessary, in syrup and top with remaining syrup
  4. Bake in 350F oven, basting often, for 20 minutes, until well glazed
  5. Makes 12 servings

Creamy Seasoned Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1, 8-ounce package wide egg noodles or spaghetti
  • 1 envelope Good Season's Italian Salad Dressing Mix
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Chopped parsley, optional

Prep Steps

  1. Cook noodles as directed on package; drain well
  2. Add remaining ingredients and toss lightly to blend thoroughly
  3. Sprinkle with chopped parsley, if desired
  4. Makes 4 cups or 8 servings

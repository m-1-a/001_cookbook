Sauteed Wheatena

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Wheatena
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cups water

Prep Steps

  1. Combine Wheatena, egg and salt then in skillet, melt butter; saute onion until golden
  2. Add Wheatena mixture and water; mix well then bring to boil; cover and cook over low heat until light in color, stirring occasionally
  3. Cook about 10 to 12 minutes, until desired thickness
  4. Makes about 5 to 6 servings

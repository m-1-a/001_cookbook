Fried Wheatena

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Wheatena
  • 3 and 1/2 cups water
  • 1 tsp. salt

Prep Steps

  1. Combine Wheatena, water and salt in large saucepan then bring to rolling boil
  2. Cook for 5 minutes or until thick and remove from heat; cool for about 5 minutes
  3. Pour into 9 * 5 * 3-inch loaf pan then cover; chill until firm
  4. Unmold; cut into 1-inch slices then saute, turning to brown on both sides
  5. Serve hot with brown sugar or maple syrup
  6. Makes 9, 1-inch slices

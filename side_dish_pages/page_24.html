Boston Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. (2 cups) dried pea, marrow, Great Northern or navy beans
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 lb. salt pork
  • 3/4 cup Grandma's Unsulphured Molasses
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. dry mustard

Prep Steps

  1. Rinse beans in cold water and drain next place in large saucepan and add water
  2. Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes then remove from heat, cover loosely and let stand for 1 hour
  3. Return to heat and bring to a boil; cover and simmer gently over low heat for a hour, until beans are tender
  4. Drain beans and reserve liquid then turn beans into 2 and 1/2-quart bean pot or casserole; add onion mix lightly
  5. Cut through surface of salt pork every 1/2 inch, making cuts about 1 inch deep
  6. Bury pork in beans then mix 2 cups reserved bean liquid with molasses, salt and dry mustard; pour over beans
  7. Cover and bake in 300F oven for 5 to 6 hours
  8. Check beans about once an hour and add additional bean liquid or water if the beans become dry; at the beginning of the cooking time the beans should be covered with liquid, and at the end of cooking the beans should be very moist and coated with syrupy liquid
  9. Makes 8 servings

Charleston Perloo with Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 Tbs. olive or vegetable oil
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 cup thinly sliced onion
  • 1 cup Uncle Ben's Converted Brand Rice
  • 2 and 1/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. white pepper
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley

Prep Steps

  1. Saute mushrooms and garlic in olive oil in 10-inch skillet until mushrooms are tender, but not brown then remove and reserve mushrooms; discard garlic
  2. Add butter to and saute onion until tender next add rice; cook, stirring constantly 5 minutes
  3. Add chicken broth, mushrooms, wine, salt and pepper then bring to boil; reduce heat and cover tightly and simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and stir in cheese and parsley
  5. Let stand, covered, until all liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes
  6. Makes 6 servings

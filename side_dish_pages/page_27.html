Cheese 'n' Cream Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 package Betty Crocker Sour Cream 'n' Chive Potatoes
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese (about 4 oz.)

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F then grease 1 and 1/2-quart round casserole
  2. Cover potatoes with boiling water in small bowl and let stand uncovered for 10 minutes; drain thoroughly
  3. Beat milk and eggs with hand beater then mix all ingredients in casserole
  4. Bake uncovered until potatoes are tender and top is golden brown, about 45-50 minutes
  5. Makes 6 servings

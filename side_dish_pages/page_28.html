Pennsylvania Dutch Potato Bake

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 1, 10 and 3/4-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Chicken Broth
  • 2 Tbs. flour
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 2 Tbs. diced pimiento
  • 1/4 cup diagonally sliced green onions
  • 1/2 tsp. celery salt
  • 1/4 tsp. hot pepper sauce
  • 6 cups cooked sliced potatoes

Prep Steps

  1. In skillet, cook bacon until crisp; remove then pour off all but 1/4 cup drippings
  2. Gradually blend broth into flour until smooth; slowly stir into drippings next add remaining ingredients except potatoes
  3. Cook, stirring until thickened then in 1 and 1/2-quart shallow baking dish (10 * 6 * 2 inches) are arrange potatoes; pour broth mixture over potatoes
  4. Cover; bake at 400F for 30 minutes and garnish with bacon
  5. Makes about 6 cups

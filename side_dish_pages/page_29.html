Pan-Fried Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/3 cup Crisco

Prep Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes then cut potatoes in 1/8-inch slices
  2. Season with salt and pepper next in a medium skillet, fry potatoes in hot Crisco over medium heat for 10 minutes
  3. Turn potatoes carefully and cook, uncovered, about 10 minutes longer, loosening slices of potatoes, occasionally and browning all sides
  4. Makes 4 servings

For Herbed Potatoes: Prepare pan-fried potatoes as above. The last 5 minutes of cooking, sprinkle the fried potatoes with 2 Tbs. finely chopped celery, 2 Tbs. snipped fresh parsley, 2 Tbs. finely chopped onion, amd 1/2 tsp. dried oregano, crushed. Sprinkle potatoes with salt and pepper to taste. Makes 4 servings

Twice-Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 8 medium baking potatoes
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 can Campbell's Cheddar Cheese Soup
  • 1 Tbs. chopped dried chives

Prep Steps

  1. Bake potatoes until done then cut potatoes in half lengthwise; scoop out insides leaving a thin shell
  2. With electric mixer, mash potatoes with butter and salt next gradually add soup and chives; beat until light and fluffy
  3. Spoon into shells and sprinkle with paprika
  4. Bake in 2 and 1/2-quart shallow baking dish (13 * 9 * 2 inches) at 450F for 15 minutes or until hot
  5. Makes 8 servings

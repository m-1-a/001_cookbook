Freckle-Faced Taters

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes, baked
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese
  • 4 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2 Tbs. milk
  • 1/4 tsp. Tabasco Pepper Sauce
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/2 cup crushed croutons or bread crumbs

Prep Steps

  1. Quarter hot potatoes lengthwise and place in a bake and serve dish and sprinkle grated cheese over potatoes
  2. Melt butter in a skillet, add remaining ingredients, stirring until crushed croutons are moist
  3. Crumble mixture over potatoes and place under broiler until cheese has browned
  4. Makes 4 servings

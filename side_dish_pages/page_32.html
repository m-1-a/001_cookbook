Spanish Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. bacon drippings
  • 1 cup uncooked Riviana Rice
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1, 14-oz. can stewed tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 1 tsp. chili powder

Prep Steps

  1. Heat 2 Tbs. fat in large skillet; add rice then brown lightly, stirring frequently
  2. Add remaining 1 Tbs. fat, onion, green pepper and celery; cook until soft next add remaining ingredients, cover pan tightly, and simmer for 20-30 minutes
  3. If rice is then not sufficiently tender, add a little more water, cover, continue to cook until soft
  4. Makes 6 servings (about 1/2 cup each)

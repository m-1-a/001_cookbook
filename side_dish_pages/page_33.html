Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fine noodles, broken in pieces
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 Campbell's Beef Broth
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/2 cup raw long grain rice

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, brown noodles in butter; stir often
  2. Add remaining ingredients
  3. Bring to a boil; reduce heat
  4. Cover; simmer 20-25 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed
  5. Makes about 2 and 1/2 cups

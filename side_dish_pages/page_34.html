Vegetable Sicily

Ingredients

  • 3 cups zucchini slices
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp. oregano leaves, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/3 cup Parkay Margarine
  • 1 medium tomato, cut into wedges

Prep Steps

  1. Saute zucchini, onion and seasonings in margarine
  2. Add tomato; cook 5 minutes or until Vegetables are tender
  3. Makes 4-6 servings

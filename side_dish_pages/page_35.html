French-Style Vegetable Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 small zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium green pepper, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup olive or salad oil
  • 1 medium eggplant, pared, cut into strips (2 * 1/2-inch)
  • 3 Tbs. flour
  • 4 medium tomatoes, peeled, cut into eighths
  • 1/4 cup Heinz Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 Tbs. salt
  • 1 tsp. Heinz Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed oregano leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper

Prep Steps

  1. In Dutch oven, saute first four ingredients in oil until onion is transparent next coat eggplant with flour; add with tomatoes to sauteed vegetables
  2. Combine Ketchup and remaining ingredients; pour over vegetables then cover; simmer for 30-35 minutes; stir occasionally, or until vegetables are tender
  3. Makes 8-10 servings (about 7 cups)
  4. Note: Zucchini may be peeled if skin is tough

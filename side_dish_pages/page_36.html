Sour Creamed Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • Parkay margarine
  • 2, 10-oz. package frozen green beans; thawed, drained
  • 1 cup dairy sour cream
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1 cup (4 oz.) shredded Kraft sharp natural Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup soft bread crumbs

Prep Steps

  1. Saute onion in 1/4 cup margarine and add to combined beans, sour cream, flour and seasonings; mix lightly
  2. Pour into greased 1 and 1/2-quart casserole then sprinkle with cheese; top with crumbs tossed with 1/4 cup melted margarine
  3. Bake at 350F for 25 minutes
  4. Makes 4-6 servings

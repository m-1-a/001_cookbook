Green Beans Polonaise

Ingredients

  • 1, 16-oz. can Del Monte Whole or Cut Green Beans
  • 3 Tbs. butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 Tbs. instant minced onion
  • 3 Tbs. fine dry bread crumbs
  • Few drops lemon juice

Prep Steps

  1. In oven-proof dish, heat green beans in 300F oven next melt butter over low heat
  2. Set aside until milk solids have settled to bottom and skim off clarified butter discards solids
  3. Rub skillet with garlic and heat clarified butter until browned
  4. Saute onion and add bread crumbs and lemon juice then cook until browned, being careful not to burn crumbs
  5. Drain beans, keep warm then top with buttered bread crumbs
  6. Makes four servings

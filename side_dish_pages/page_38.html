Golden Capped Baked Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 6 medium size tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbs. French's Prepared Mustard
  • 1 tsp. French's Minced Onion
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 2 Tbs. fine dry bread crumbs

Prep Steps

  1. Cut each tomato in half and place cut side up in a shallow baking dish; sprinkle cut surface with salt and pepper
  2. Combine the remaining ingredients then spoon a portion on top of each tomato; spread to cover cut surface
  3. Note: Can be prepared in advance to this point. Refrigerate until ready to begin baking. Baking time may need to be increased slightly
  4. Bake in 400F oven for 15 minutes or until topping is lightly puffed and tomatoes are hot
  5. Makes 6 servings

Tomato Sour Cream Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 Tbs. salad oil
  • 1 - 1-lb., 12-oz. can tomatoes
  • 1 package Mexican style "sloppy joe" seasoning mix
  • 1, 4-oz. can green chiles, chopped
  • 1, 5 and 1/2-oz. package Doritos Nacho Cheese Tortilla Chips, slightly crushed
  • 3/4 pound Monterey Jack cheese, grated
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheddar cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Saute onion in oil; add tomatoes, seasoning mix and green chiles then simmer, uncovered, for 10-15 minutes
  2. In greased, deep 2-quart casserole, layer ingredients in the following order; sauce, crushed Doritos Tortilla Chips, Monterey Jack cheese, sauce, Monterey Jack cheese
  3. Top with sour cream then bake at 325F for 30 minutes
  4. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese; bake 10 minutes longer
  5. Makes 6-8 servings

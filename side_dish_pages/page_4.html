Corn Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2 cans corn (8 and 3/4 ounces each)
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 3/4 cup Pet Evaporated Milk
  • 2 Tbs. margarine or butter, melted
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Mix well all ingredients
  2. Pour into a greased 1 and 1/2-quart baking dish
  3. Bake at 350F for 45 minutes, until firm
  4. Makes 6 servings, 1/2 cup each

Eggplant Puffed Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches broccoli, cut into spears or 2, 10-oz. package frozen broccoli spears, cooked, drained
  • 2 egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese (2 oz.)
  • 1/2 cup Hellmann's Best Foods Real Mayonnaise

Prep Steps

  1. Arrange hot cooked broccoli in shallow 1 and 1/2-quart pan or broiler proof serving dish
  2. In small bowl with mixer at high speed beat egg whites and salt until stiff peak form then fold in cheese and mayonnaise; spoon evenly over broccoli
  3. Broil 6 inches from source of heat for 4 minutes or until golden brown and serve immediately
  4. Makes 6-8 servings

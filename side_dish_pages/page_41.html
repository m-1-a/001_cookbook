Candy-Coated Carrots

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 4 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • 6-8 carrots, sliced and cooked

Prep Steps

  1. Place butter and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat, stir until combined then add Tabasco and salt; mix well; add lemon juice
  2. Remove from heat and add hot, drained carrots and toss gently until coated evenly
  3. Makes 4 servings

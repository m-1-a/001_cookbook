Triple Corn Fritters

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/3 cups Pillsbury's All-Purpose or Unbleached Flour
  • 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 Tbs. baking powder
  • 2 tsps. sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 2 Tbs. oil
  • 1, 8 and 1/2-oz. can Green Giant Brand Golden Cream Style Corn
  • 1, 12-oz. can Green Giant Niblets Brand Golden Whole Kernel Corn, drained
  • Oil for deep frying

Prep Steps

  1. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; level off next in a medium bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, baking powder, sugar and salt
  2. In large bowl, combine eggs, milk, oil and cream style corn then blend in dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened next stir in whole kernel corn
  3. In deep fat fryer or heavy saucepan drop batter by tablespoonfuls and fry in 2-3 inches hot fat (365F) for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown
  4. Drain on paper towel and serve warm
  5. Makes 28 fritters
  6. Tip: Self-rising flour is not recommend

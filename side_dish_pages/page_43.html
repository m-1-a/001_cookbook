Now You Like em Greens

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs. (or 3 bunches) fresh spinach, cooked
  • 1/4 tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 4 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1, 3-oz. package cream cheese
  • 2 Tbs. milk
  • 2 Tbs. hard-cooked eggs, sliced or 2/3 cup bread

Prep Steps

  1. To hot, drained spinach, add Tabasco, salt, butter, and cream cheese that has been softened with milk
  2. Stir until butter has melted and ingredients are blended
  3. Place in a casserole and bake for 10 minutes at 375F
  4. Garnish with egg slices or bread crumbs
  5. Makes 6 servings

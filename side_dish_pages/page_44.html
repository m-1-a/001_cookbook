New England Harvest Baked Beans

Ingredients

  • 2, 28-oz. cans B & M Brick Oven Baked Beans
  • 1/2 cup catsup or barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. dry mustard
  • 2 cups baking apples (peeled, cored and cubed)
  • 1/4 to 1/2 lb. bacon

Prep Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a 3-quart baking dish, mix together B & M Brick Oven Baked Beans, catsup, vinegar, maple syrup, dry mustard and baking apples
  3. Arrange bacon slices over top of casserole
  4. Bake uncovered, for 45 minutes
  5. Makes 8 servings

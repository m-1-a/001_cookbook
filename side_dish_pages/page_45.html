Beans Hawaiian

Ingredients

  • 2, 1-lb. cans Heinz Vegetarian Beans in Tomato Sauce
  • 1, 8-oz. can pineapple chucks, drained
  • 1-2 Tbs. light brown sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. Heinz Mild Mustard
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash ground cloves

Prep Steps

  1. Combine ingredients; pour into a 1-quart casserole
  2. Bake, uncovered in 375F oven for 50-55 minutes or until beans are hot, stirring occasionally
  3. Makes 4-6 servings (about 4 cups)
  4. Note: 1, 8 and 1/2-oz. can pineapple slices, drained and cut into chucks may be substituted

Rice a L'Orange

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 cups hot cooked rice
  • 1 tsp. fresh grated orange peel
  • 1 Sunkist navel orange, peeled and cut in bite size pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts, sliced green onions, sliced ripe olives or toasted sesame seeds

Prep Steps

  1. To the rice, stir in orange peel and Sunkist Orange and then add one of the last ingredients for extra flavor

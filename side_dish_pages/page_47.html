Orange Rice

Ingredients

  • 1, 6-oz. can frozen orange juice
  • 2 and 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp. ground saffron
  • 1 Tbs. Angostura
  • 2 Tbs. butter
  • 3 cups precooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. In a 2-quart saucepan mix orange juice with water, saffron, Angostura and butter then bring to a boil
  2. Slowly stir in rice next stir to blend well
  3. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand for 10 minutes stir with a fork to fluff
  4. Makes 8 servings

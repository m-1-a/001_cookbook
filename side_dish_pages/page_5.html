Broccoli Saute

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. fresh broccoli
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup Planters Peanut Oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper

Prep Steps

  1. Wash broccoli and spilt ends of large stalks lengthwise into halves or quarters, depending on size
  2. Place in large skillet then sprinkle with water,Planter Peanut Oil, garlic, salt and pepper
  3. Cover tightly; cook over very low heat 20 minutes to 30 minutes, or until stalks are tender
  4. Turn broccoli several times during cooking
  5. Makes 4 servings

Saucy Skillet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs. margarine
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup Hellmann's Best Food Real Mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup cider vinegar
  • 1 Tbs. sugar
  • 1 and 3/4 tsps. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 4 medium potatoes, cooked, peeled, sliced (4 cups)
  • 1 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1 Tbs. cooked crumbled bacon

Prep Steps

  1. In large skillet melt margarine over medium heat
  2. Add onions; cook 2 to 3 minutes or until tender-crisp
  3. Stir in next 5 ingredients
  4. Add potatoes; cook; stirring constantly, 2 minutes or until hot (do not boil)
  5. Garnish with parsley and bacon
  6. Makes 4 to 6 servings

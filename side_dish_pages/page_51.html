Sweet Potato Ring with Cashews

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 Tbs. Blue Bonnet margarine, melted
  • 1/4 cup chopped Planters Cashew Halves
  • 1, 18-oz. can vacuum package sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground allspice

Prep Steps

  1. Combine brown sugar and Blue Bonnet Margarine; spoon into bottom of a well-greased 1-quart ring mold then arrange Planters Cashew Halves in sugar mixture in mold
  2. In electric blender or food processor combine sweet potatoes, milk, eggs, cinnamon, salt snd allspice and blend until smooth and creamy
  3. Spoon into prepared ring mold next place ring mold in pan of hot water and bake at 325F for 1 hour and 10 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean
  4. Loosen edges with a knife; invert onto serving plate and serve immediately
  5. Serves 6 to 8

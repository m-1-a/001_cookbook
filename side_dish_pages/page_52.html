Sweet Potato Puffs

Ingredients

  • 3 large sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/8 tsp. pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 cup blanched sillvered Blue Diamond Almonds

Prep Steps

  1. Scrub potatoes; cut off ends and place in baking pan then bake in 400F (hot) oven 1 hour
  2. Cut potatoes lengthwise into halves and scoop pulp into mixing bowl next add half the butter, then milk and salt
  3. Beat until light and fluffy and fill shells with potato mixture; return to oven
  4. Cream together remaining butter, sugar and pumpkin pie spice then fold in Almonds
  5. Place a dollop of the almond topping on each potato half and return to oven and heat for five minutes or until topping is melted
  6. Makes 6 servings

Easy Corn Puff

Ingredients

  • 2, 10-oz. package frozen corn or chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup Bisquick Baking Mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese (about 4 ozs)

Prep Steps

  1. Heat oven to 325F next butter 5-cup souffle dish or 1 and 1/2-quart round casserole
  2. Cook corn as directed on package; drain then beat Baking Mix, eggs and salt with hand beater until smooth
  3. Stir in corn and cheese next pour into souffle dish
  4. Bake until knife inserted halfway between center and edge comes out clean, about 1 hour then serve immediately
  5. Makes 6 servings

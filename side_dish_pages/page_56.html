Lemony Vegetable Saute

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. broccoli, cut into flowerets
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 cups thinly sliced carrots
  • 2 cups sliced Dole Fresh Mushrooms
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3 Tbs. sugar

Prep Steps

  1. Saute broccoli in oil until tender-crisp then remove
  2. Stir in carrots and saute until tender-crisp and stir in mushrooms and saute lightly
  3. Return broccoli to skillet next stir in lemon
  4. Combine butter and sugar then add to vegetables
  5. Stir until vegetables are well glazed
  6. Makes 4 to 6 servings

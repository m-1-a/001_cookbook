Savory Baked Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup Pompeian Olive Oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 pimiento, cut into strips
  • 2 Tbs. minced ham
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. marjoram
  • 1 Tbs. minced parsley
  • 1 cup soft bread crumbs

Prep Steps

  1. Cut a 2-inch wide cap from the stem end of the tomatoes next hollow out tomatoes, removing pulp and discard with the caps
  2. Heat 4 Tbs. of olive oil in a skillet, add onion and saute until tender them stir in remaining ingredients, browning slightly
  3. Spoon stuffing mixture into tomatoes and place remaining 2 Tbs. olive oil into a shallow baking pan, arrange stuffed tomatoes in the pan, brush with a little of the oil
  4. Bake at 350F for 40 minutes until cooked through
  5. Makes 4 servings

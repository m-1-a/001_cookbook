Creamed Cauliflower Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head cauliflower
  • 3 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1, 8-oz. package Pepperidge Farm Herb Seasoned Stuffing Mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted

Prep Steps

  1. Break cauliflower into small pieces and cook until just tender then drain and place in a shallow 2-quart casserole
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan next stir in flour and cook together a few minutes, while stirring
  3. Remove from heat and blend in milk then bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and simmer until thickened
  4. Pour over cauliflower in casserole and combine the last three ingredients and spoon on top, pressing down if necessary
  5. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes
  6. Serves 8 to 10
  7. Note: Broccoli in place of cauliflower makes a delicious change

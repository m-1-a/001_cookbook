Peas a la Creme

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup commercial sour cream
  • 1/2 tsp. instant chicken flavored bouillon
  • 1/2 tsp. lemon juice
  • Dash pepper
  • 1, 16-oz. can Stokely's Finest Peas
  • Optional fresh dill

Prep Steps

  1. Mix sour cream and bouillon in saucepan then let stand for 10 minutes to dissolve bouillon
  2. Add lemon juice and pepper next place over low heat to blend flavors; do not boil
  3. Heat peas in their own liquid; drain and place in serving dish; top with sauce
  4. Garnish with fresh dill if desired
  5. Makes 4 servings

Broccoli au Gratin

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch broccoli (about 2 lbs.) or 1 small head cauliflower (or two, 10-oz. frozen packages of either vegetable)
  • 1, 11-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Cheddar Cheese soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 Tbs. buttered bread crumbs
  • 4 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled

Prep Steps

  1. Cook vegetable; drain
  2. Place in shallow baking dish (10 * 6 * 2 inches)
  3. Stir soup; blend in milk; pour over vegetables
  4. Top with crumbs and bake at 350F for 20 minutes or until hot
  5. Garnish with bacon before serving
  6. Makes 4 to 6 servings

Cheese-Stuffed Zucchini

Ingredients

  • 8 medium zucchini, about 2 1/4 lbs.
  • 1 package (8 ounces) May-Bud Farmers Cheese, shredded
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1 Tbs. parsley flakes
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Seasoned Salt
  • Paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Parboil zucchini for about 5 to 7 minutes then cool and cut in half lengthwise
  2. Scoop out zucchini pulp. leaving shell next place zucchini cut side down to drain
  3. Chop pulp then mix pulp with cheese (reserving 1/2 cup), eggs, rice, onion, bread crumbs, parsley and salt
  4. Sprinkle zucchini shells with seasoned salt; then stuff with cheese-rice mixture and place in a greased 15 1/2 * 10 1/2 * 1-inch pan
  5. Bake at 350F for 20 to 25 minutes or until heated through
  6. Last few minutes of baking top with remaining cheese
  7. Makes 16 pieces

Honey-Glazed Squash

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 acorn squash
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine, softened at room temperature
  • 1 Tbs. French Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/4 cup raisins

Prep Steps

  1. Cut squash in half; remove seeds next place, cut-side down, in shallow pan; add about 1/2-inch hot water to pan
  2. Bake in 350F for 40 to 50 minutes, until almost tender and discard water; turn squash cut-side up
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper then combine honey, butter Worcestershire Sauce and raisins; spoon into cavities of squash
  4. Return to oven and bake for 15 minutes, until tender
  5. Makes 4 to 6 servings

French-Fried Onions

Ingredients

  • 2 large Bermuda onions
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose enriched flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Crisco Oil for deep frying
  • Salt

Prep Steps

  1. Cut cleaned onions into 1/4-inch slices and separate into rings
  2. Soak onion rings in milk then dip in flour seasoned with 1/2 tsp. salt next fry a few at a time in deep Crisco Oil heated to 375F
  3. Cook until lightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes then drain on paper towels
  4. Sprinkle with salt
  5. Makes about 4 servings

Monterey Bay Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Sun-Maid Seedless Raisins
  • 1 cup Sun-Maid Golden Seedless Raisins
  • 1 cup Sun-Maid Currents
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 quart diced pared tart apples
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 and 1/2 tsps. powdered ginger
  • 1/2 tsp. allspice
  • 1/8 tsp. cloves
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup dry white wine or water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup diced green pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced pimiento

Prep Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, except green pepper and pimiento
  2. Simmer 20 to 25 minutes, uncovered, until thickened
  3. Add green pepper and pimiento
  4. Cook 5 minutes longer then cool
  5. Makes about 1 and 1/2 quarts

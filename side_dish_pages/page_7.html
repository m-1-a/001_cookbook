Zucchini Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Planters Peanut Oil
  • 8 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 2/3 cup coarsely chopped onion
  • 2 Tbs. chopped parsley
  • 1 clove large garlic, crushed or minced
  • 1 Tbs. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano leaves
  • 1/4 tsp. rosemary leaves
  • 4 cups peeled chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Prep Steps

  1. Heat Planters Peanut Oil in large skillet then add zucchini, onion, parsley, garlic, salt, pepper, oregano and rosemary
  2. Saute mixture over medium heat, stirring often, until zucchini is tender, about 20 minutes
  3. Toss in tomatoes and continue to saute until tomatoes are thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes
  4. Turn mixture into a serving dish; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
  5. Makes 8 to 10 servings

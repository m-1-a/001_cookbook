Orange-Glazed Beets

Ingredients

  • 1 can (1 lb.) Stokely's Finest Cut Beets
  • 1 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 2 tsps. flour
  • 2 Tbs. brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup orange juice

Prep Steps

  1. Heat beets in their own liquid
  2. In small saucepan, melt butter
  3. Remove from heat; add flour, brown sugar and orange juice
  4. Return to heat; stirring constantly until thickened
  5. Drain beets; add sauce to beets
  6. Makes 4 to 5 servings

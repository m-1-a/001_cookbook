Orange Carrots

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. carrots
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. fresh grated orange peel
  • 1 Sunkist orange
  • 2 Tbs. margarine or butter
  • 2 to 3 Tbs of your favorite syrup (cane, molasses, honey, pancake, or brown sugar)

Prep Steps

  1. Wash and peel, scrub or scrape carrots then slice crosswise into rounds or lengthwise into sticks
  2. Bring water and salt to a boil in saucepan next add carrots and cover pan
  3. Cook over medium heat for 10 to 20 minutes, until tender and drain well
  4. Meanwhile grate 1/2 tsp. peel from orange next peel orange and cut into bite-size pieces
  5. Add oranfe pieces and peel, margarine and syrup to drained carrots then place over low heat and stir gently until margarine is melted and oranges are heated
  6. Serve at once
  7. Makes 6 servings
  8. Note: For different flavor, leave out the syrup and add 1 to 2 Tbs. chopped green onions or chives

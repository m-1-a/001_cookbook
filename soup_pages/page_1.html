Danish Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 1, 10 1/2-oz. can condensed tomato soup
  • 1 can milk
  • 1/8 tsp. dill weed
  • Sour cream and toasted blanched silvered almonds for garnish

Prep Steps

  1. Combine and heat ingredients thorough
  2. To serve, garnish with a dollop of dairy sour cream and plenty of toasted blanched silvered almonds
  3. Makes 3 servings

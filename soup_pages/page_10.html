Puree Mongole

Ingredients

  • 1, 11 1/4-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Green Pea Soup
  • 1, 10 3/4-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • Dash curry powder, optional

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, stir Green Pea soup until smooth; gradually blend in remaining ingredients
  2. Heat; stir occasionally
  3. Do not boil
  4. Makes 4 1/2 cups

Oyster Bisque

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen (1 pint) shucked large raw oysters
  • 1 cup oyster liquor
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 slice onion
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 sprig parsley
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 1 3/4 tsps. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. Tabasco pepper sauce
  • Chopped chives

Prep Steps

  1. Drain oysters; reserve 1 cup liquor then dice oysters into saucepan and add liquor; slowly bring oysters to boiling point; remove
  2. In same saucepan scald milk and cream with onion, celery, parsley and bay leaf; stain next blend butter with flour, salt and Tabasco pepper sauce
  3. Slowly stir in scalded milk; stir over low heat until thickened and add oysters and cooking liquid; heat to serving temperature
  4. Garnish with chopped chives
  5. Makes 6 to 8 servings

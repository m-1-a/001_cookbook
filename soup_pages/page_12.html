Seafood Bisque

Ingredients

  • 1, 8-oz. package Birds-Eye Green Peas with Cream Sauce
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 1, 7 3/4-oz. can red salmon, flaked
  • 1 Tbs. catsup

Prep Steps

  1. Combine vegetable, milk and half and half in saucepan
  2. Bring to a full boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally
  3. Remove from heat and stir until sauce is smooth
  4. Add salmon and catsup
  5. Cover; simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Makes 3 servings
  7. Note: You can substitute the red salmon for 1, 7-oz. can tuna, drained and flaked

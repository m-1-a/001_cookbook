Seafood Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/2 green pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup Wesson Oil
  • 1, 14 1/2-oz. can Hunt's Whole Tomatoes
  • 1, 6-oz. can Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed basil leaves
  • 1/2 lb. crab meat
  • 1/2 lb. raw shrimp, shelled and cleaned
  • 1/2 lb. fresh haddock or halibut, cut in chunks
  • 1, 10-oz. pkg. frozen sliced okra, partially thawed
  • 3 cups hot cooked rice

Prep Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, saute garlic, onion and green pepper in hot oil, stirring until tender
  2. Add remaining ingredients except seafood, okra and rice
  3. Simmer, uncovered for 45 minutes; remove bay leaf
  4. Add crab, shrimp, haddock and okra; cover and simmer for 10 to 12 minutes
  5. Serve in soup bowls over rice
  6. Makes 8 servings

Italian Pasta Pot

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp. rosemary leaves, crushed
  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 3, 10 3/4-oz. cans Campbell's Tomato Soup
  • 3 soup cans water
  • 1 about 16-oz can chick peas, drained
  • 1 cup cooked small shell macaroni
  • 2 tsps. chopped anchovy fillets

Prep Steps

  1. In large saucepan, cook pepper with garlic and rosemary in oil until tender
  2. Stir in soup and water next add remaining ingredients
  3. Heat; stir occasionally then garnish with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired
  4. Makes about 9 1/2 cups, 6 to 8 servings

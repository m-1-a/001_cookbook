Zuppa Pasta Fagiola

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound dried white kidney or lima beans
  • Water
  • Ham shank (about 2 lbs) or ham bone
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 medium firm tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. rubbed sage
  • 1/2 tsp. thyme leaves
  • 6 cups water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 2 ozs. (1 cup) Mulleller's Twist Macaroni

Prep Steps

  1. Soak beans in water overnight; drain next remove skin and excess fat from ham shanks
  2. In large kettle combine beans, ham shank, garlic, 2 of the chopped tomatoes, 1/2 tsp. pepper, sage, thyme, and 6 cups of water
  3. Bring to a boil; cover and simmer gently for 2 hours, or until beans are tender then remove shank from kettle; dice meat and return to kettle
  4. While beans are cooking, combine olive oil, the remaining 2 chopped tomatoes, wine, salt and 1/4 tsp. pepper in a medium saucepan
  5. Simmer, uncovered for 20 minutes then pour into bean mixture
  6. Bring to a boil; add Macaroni and continue to cook for 9 to 12 minutes or until macaroni is tender
  7. Serve in large soup bowls
  8. Makes 8 to 10 servings

Frankfurter Creole Chowder

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. frankfurter, cut in 1/2-inch slices
  • 1/4 tsp. basil or thyme leaves, crushed
  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1, 11 1/2-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Split Pea with Ham Soup
  • 1, 10 3/4-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Potato Soup
  • 1/2 soup can water
  • 1 can (about 8 ozs) tomatoes, cut up
  • 1 can (about 8 ozs) whole kernel corn, undrained

Prep Steps

  1. In large saucepan, brown frankfurter with basil in butter
  2. Add soups; gradually stir in water
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Heat; stir occasionally
  5. Makes about 6 cups

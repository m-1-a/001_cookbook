Easy And Deluxe French Onion Soup

Easy Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 1, 10 1/2-oz. can beef bouillon
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 2 Tbs. French's Worcestershire Sauce

Easy Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in medium size saucepan
  2. Add onion and sugar; cook and stir for 5 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned
  3. Add bouillon, water and Worcestershire Sauce; simmer for 10 to 15 minutes

Deluxe Ingredients

  • 4 slices French or pumpernickel bread
  • Easy French Onion Soup (above)
  • 1/2 cup Swiss cheese, shredded
  • 1 Tbs. Parmesan cheese, grated

Deluxe Prep Steps

  1. Arrange bread on baking sheet; bake in 300 F. oven for 20 to 30 minutes, until crisp and dry
  2. Spoon soup into 4 oven proof serving dishes; top each with 1 slice bread and sprinkle generously with cheese mixture
  3. Place under broiler for 2 to 3 minutes to melt cheese
  4. For soup bowls which are NOT oven proof, toast bread as directed
  5. Add cheese and broil, then place on hot soup
  6. Makes 4 servings

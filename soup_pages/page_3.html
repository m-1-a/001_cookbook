Spanish Bisque

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 2/3 cups (13 3/4-oz.) can chicken broth
  • 3 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • Dash cayenne
  • Dash garlic powder
  • 1 cup light cream
  • Garniture (below)

Prep Steps

  1. Puree avocados with broth, lemon juice and seasonings
  2. Blend in cream; cover and chill
  3. Serve with your choice of the garnishes below
  4. Makes 6 to 8 servings
  5. Garnishes: Ceumbled bacon, chopped toasted almonds, buttered croutons, chili relish (mix 2 Tbs. minced onion with 2 canned green chili peppers, chopped, and 3 canned pimentos, chopped), diced ham, silvered prosciutto, finely chopped hard cooked egg or chopped anchovy fillets

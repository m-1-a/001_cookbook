Leek and Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 leeks, white part only
  • 3 Tbs. butter
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 pint rich milk
  • 1 pint heavy cream (half and half)
  • Chopped chives

Prep Steps

  1. Slice leeks thinly, peel and slice potatoes then cook leeks in butter until limp
  2. Transfer them to soup pot and add the potatoes next add salt and broth; let simmer until potatoes are very tender
  3. Put contents of pot through a sieve; return to mixture to soup pot and add milk then let simmer for a few minutes
  4. Add seasoning and add a little ground white pepper if you like next chill in the refrigerator
  5. Just before serving, beat in the cream and serve in chilled bowls and sprinkle the top with chopped chives
  6. Serves 6

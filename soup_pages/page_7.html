Potage Senegalese

Ingredients

  • 1, 10 3/4-oz. can Campbell's Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 soup can water
  • 1/2 cup light cream
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 2 Tbs. shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp. curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic salt
  • 1/2 tsp. onion powder
  • 1 avocado, sliced

Prep Steps

  1. In saucepan, combine all ingredients except avocado
  2. Heat; stir occasionally
  3. Top each servings with avocado
  4. Makes about 3 1/2 cups
  5. For chilled soup: Prepare recipe as seen above; chill for 6 hours. Then serve in chilled bowls

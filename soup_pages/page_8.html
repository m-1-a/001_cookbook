Corn Chowder

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbs. butter or margarine
  • 1 cup diced cooked ham
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped green or red pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced potatoes
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1, 17-oz. can Stokely's Finest Whole Kernel Golden Corn
  • 1, 2-oz. can Stokely's Finest Sliced Pimientos, drained
  • Paprika

Prep Steps

  1. Melt butter in large saucepan next saute ham for 5 minutes and set aside
  2. Saute onion, celery and green pepper until onion is transparent then add chicken broth, potatoes, and pepper
  3. Cover and cook over moderate heat about 20 minutes, or until potatoes are just tender and add milk, cream, corn, reserved ham and pimientos
  4. Reduce heat and cook until thoroughly heated but DO NOT boil
  5. Spoon into bowls and garnish each serving with paprika
  6. Make 6 servings

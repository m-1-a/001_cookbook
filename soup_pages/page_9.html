Golden Cheese Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Land O Lakes Sweet Butter Cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrot
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbs. instant minced onion or 3 Tbs. fresh chopped onion
  • 1 tsp. instant chicken bouillon
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. dry mustard
  • 1/4 tsp. curry powdered, if desired
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 tap. hot pepper sauce
  • 2 cups (8 ozs) shredded Land O Lakes Process American Cheese
  • 1/2 cup beer

Prep Steps

  1. In heavy 2 quart saucepan melt butter over medium heat
  2. saute carrots in melted butter until tender about (5 to 7 minutes)
  3. Remove from heat; stir in flour, onion, bouillon and seasoning until well combined next slowly in milk and hot pepper sauce until smooth
  4. Return to heat, add cheese, and cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until mixture thickens and comes to a boil
  5. Boil gently for 1 minute, stirring constantly then reduce heat; add beer and heat through
  6. Serve immediately and garnish with popcorn
  7. Makes 4 servings
  8. Tip: If using fresh onion, saute with carrot until tender. To reheat or thin soup, add more beer or milk
